Season 1 
- "I Always Get What I Want" 
- 10 houseguests remain and this time they must wash a car in the most creative way, where the winning team will get a Spa day out with Tila. The losing team, however, must camp outside for the night. Then, Tila pits guys against girls in a challenge that involves a spinning chair and glasses. Then, Tila sends 1 guy and 1 girl home leaving 8 people still with a shot at love.
