Ep 8: The Finishing Touch
 Mary finishes her home in time for a dinner party, while Martyn struggles with his sister's pub.
 PREMIERE: Tuesday January 8, 9 pm

Kathryn completes Lindsay Lohan's new home in the Hollywood Hills but drama erupts in the middle of the installation as Lindsay is rushed to the hospital after a horrific car crash and asks Kathryn to come see her. Martyn dodges schedule delays to complete a spectacular renovation of his sister Tina's pub in his hometown, Chislehurst, England. Mary finishes redecorating her home and hosts a cocktail party for all of the designers where Jeffrey and Ross share some surprising news.
