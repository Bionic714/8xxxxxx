Collects SHE-BUCCANEER 1-7! When she saw her love Calico Jack Rackham hung above the docks of Port Royal, she thought her freebooting days were at an end. Aye, but they'd just begun! Soon she'd find her pockets overflowing with treasure from the seas of Persia to the shores of Atlantis...
