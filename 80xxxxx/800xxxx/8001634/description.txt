

Welcome to Five Bar Hotel - Filmed across Britain, from inner cities to country beats, this five-part series reveals what the police are up against every day of their working lives. The first episode joins the staff at the Medway custody suite in Gillingham, Kent - one of the busiest in the country, where 40 suspected criminals are processed every day. From burglary to shop-lifting and assault to drugs possession, Custody Officer Sergeant Sean O'Conner and his team have seen generations progress through their criminal careers
