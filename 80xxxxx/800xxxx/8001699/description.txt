Agnetha Åse Fältskog, (born on 5 April 1950) is a Swedish recording artist. She
achieved success in Sweden after the release of her début album Agnetha
Fältskog in 1968, and reached international stardom as a member of the pop
group ABBA. 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agnetha_F%C3%A4ltskog

This compilation was released 1996 and consists of songs from Agnethas pre-ABBA
career as a soloartist, some gems from the ABBA era and her recordings as solo
artist after ABBA. Here is also two songs from the respective record she made
with daughter Linda (christmas songs) and the son Christian.
