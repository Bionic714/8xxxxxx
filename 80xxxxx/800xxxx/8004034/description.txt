Starkers in Tokyo is a live acoustic album by Whitesnake released only in Japan on September 9, 1997. 
It is performed in the style of the Unplugged series and simply features David Coverdale on vocals and Adrian Vandenberg on acoustic guitar. 
The performance was recorded at the EMI studios in Japan for a small audience of fans. 
This CD was released outside of Japan in June 1998.
