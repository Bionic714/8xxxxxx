Crawl To Me

Genre: Horror/Suspense

English | CBR | 107 Pages

Written by Alan Robert
Art by Alan Robert

As Ryan struggles to protect his family from what appears to be an evil entity living within their basement's crawl space. It is only after a series of violent events occur, that Ryan realizes he must set aside all he believes to be true in order to face his shocking and inevitable reality. This intense thriller will keep you guessing every step of the way.
