Jack's father set him up in the perfect job, heading up an L.A. modeling agency. The only catch is that he has a limited time to show a profit or go to work in the family's oil business. Not willing to give up the playboy lifestyle, he pulls out all of the stops to bring in a very lucrative lingerie catalog account. But as a rival agency goes a step further by using their models as sexual enticements, Jack faces an interesting moral dilemma, and time is running out.
