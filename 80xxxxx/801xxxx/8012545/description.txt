Publication Date: April 28, 2010

Audio Book on 4 CDs recorded by Gordon West, WB6NOA for the 2010-2014 Technician Class FCC Element 2 license examination on 4 audio CDs. 

Author West reviews every possible questions and answer in the FCC question pool and provides a fun, educational answer explanation. Follows the order of his Technician Class book, and includes the exciting sounds of ham radio fun. Includes a User's Guide showing topic areas covered on each track. A great study companion to his book.
