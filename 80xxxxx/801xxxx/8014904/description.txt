Harold and Maude

Harold and Maude is a 1971 American black comedy romantic drama film directed by Hal Ashby and released by Paramount Pictures. (Wikipedia)

Young, rich, and obsessed with death, Harold finds himself changed forever when he meets lively septuagenarian Maude at a funeral. (IMDB)

Release date: 1971 (initial release)
Director: Hal Ashby
MPAA rating: PG
Screenplay: Colin Higgins
Cinematography: John A. Alonzo


************************************************
This release features synced Simplified and traditional Chinese and English subtitles (close caption style). Chinese translation is on the whole good, with only a few small mis-translations. Song lyrics are translated quite well.
