Peaks of Faith

Travel Documentary with no narration published by NHK broadcasted as part of NHK Treasures from the Past series in 2009 - English language


Information
------------------------------

List of World Heritage sites in this episode

 1. Mount Taishan
 2. Ancient Building Complex in the Wudang Mountains
 3. Temple and Cemetery of Confucius and the Kong Family Mansion in Qufu
 4. Yungang Grottoes
 5. Longmen Grottoes
 6. Mount Emei Scenic Area, including Leshan Giant Buddha Scenic Area
