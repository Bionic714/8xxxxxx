Desperadoes is a Weird West-style comic book series written by Jeff Mariotte. It is published by IDW Publishing.


This pack includes the complete Desperadoes saga:


* Desperadoes: A Moment's Sunlight (5-issue mini-series, 1997-1998)
* Desperadoes: Epidemic! (One-shot, 1999)
* Desperadoes: Quiet of the Grave (5-issue mini-series, 2002)
* Desperadoes: Banners of Gold (5-issue mini-series, 2005)
* Desperadoes: Buffalo Dreams (4-issue mini-series 2007)
