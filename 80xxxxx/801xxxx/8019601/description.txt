Title: Realms of Illusion (Sentinel: Descendants in Time)
Genre: adventure / puzzle-solving / 1st-person
Released: 14th December 2004
Developer: Detalion
Publisher: The Adventure Company
Description:
Realms of Illusion (Sentinel: Descendants in Time) is a puzzle-oriented, first-person, 3D adventure game based on the work of award-winning science fiction writer Terry Dowling. It rewards the player for logical, careful thought rather than quick reflexes. No action elements. No timed puzzles. No tricky jump sequences. Just slow, steady exploration of the beautiful worlds of Tastan civilization. Integrated into its fantastic and beautiful worlds are puzzles to solve and traps to avoid in order to unlock the mysteries of the dangerous Tomb 85 and learn the secrets of the Dormeuse, the Sleeping Woman.

How to play:
1) Run setup_realms_of_illusion.exe and install the game;
2) Start the game using main executable or shortcuts (DRM free).
