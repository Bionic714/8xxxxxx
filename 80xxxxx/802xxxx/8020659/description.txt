
SOULFISH-2: FISH MODE

Soulfish part-2 documents another epic global fly fishing adventure! 

Follow some of the most dedicated fly anglers in the world as they chase some of the greatest Game Fish on the planet to waters around the globe. 

Including Taimen in Northern Mongolia, Steelhead on drys in remote British Columbia, a grand slam on the flats of Belize, Largemouth Bass deep in the heart or remote central Mexico, Redfish and Black Drum in Louisiana, giant Bonefish on the beautiful flats of Oaho Hawaii, Nile Perch and African Tiger Fish on the Nile River in Egypt, and you end up back in California for some epic trout fishing on some remote and beautiful streams and rivers. 



90-minute DVD.
