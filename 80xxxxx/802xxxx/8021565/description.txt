Tears to Tiara
Company: Leaf
Release date: Apr 28, 2005
Genres: Drama, Fantasy, Romance, Gameplay, SRPG



Summary: 1,200 years after the Demon King Arawn was sealed, a priest of the "Holy Empire" conspired to free him, using the young oracle Riannon as a sacrifice. However, the appearance of Riannon's brother Arthur, striving to save his sister put his plan in peril. In the ensuing chaos, by a twist of fate, Arawn is unwittingly released by Riannon. So begins their tale...
