Shadowgrounds  is an epic action experience combining modern technology with 

addictive playability.

-Devastating weapons and extensive weapon upgrades

-Realistic lighting effects

-11 exciting missions taking place on various battlegrounds

-Thrilling sound effects and acclaimed soundtrack

-Old-school attitude, modern graphics (including the awesome flamethrower!) 

Minimum system requirements

Windows 2000/XP

1.5GHz Processor

384MB RAM

128MB DirectX compatible Video Card

DirectX compatible Sound Card

1GB Hard Disk Space

DirectX 9.0c

How to install note:

1.Burn or mount the image
2.Copy the content of the PHOPET folder into the game itself
3.Play
