Writer: Garth Ennis
Artist: Jacen Burrows

303 is a six-issue comic-book mini-series created by Garth Ennis and Jacen Burrows, and published by Avatar Press. The story spans two parts titled "Afghanistan's Plains" and "Black Arrow" (after a monologue in J.R.R. Tolkien's "The Hobbit") each consisting of three issues and centers around a Russian Spetsnaz colonel.

The title of the series refers to a .303 British calibre Lee-Enfield rifle.
