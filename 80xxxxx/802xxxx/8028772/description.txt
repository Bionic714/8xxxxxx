Written by F.J. DeSanto and Todd Farmer with art by Federico Dallocchio


Get ready for an explosive new original mini-series where cutting-edge science fiction and hi-tech military action collide! INSURGENT transports readers to the near future where the American government, using a powerful new form of nanotech, builds a secret military force composed of unknowing citizens. But what happens when a mysterious terrorist group starts activating these sleeping super soldiers for something more sinister? Only government-sanctioned bounty hunter John Ravane has the ability to end the threat. But can the government coerce his return to active duty?
