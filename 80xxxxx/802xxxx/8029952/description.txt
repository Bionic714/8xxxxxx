Grimm Fairy Tales Animated Series One-Shot
	
By: Jon Schnepp, TBD	
Genre: Fantasy
Publisher: Zenescope Entertainment	
Publication Date: January 16, 2013
For ages 16+

This special one-shot is based directly on the pilot episode of the new Grimm Fairy Tales Animated Series produced by Titmouse Studios and Directed by Jon Schnepp (Venture Bros. & Metalocalypse).

This incredible issue contains special artwork and the pilot episode character designs by superstar artists Alex Pardee and Bill Sienkiewicz, as well as other bonus content including story boards and a cover by Metalocalypse director Jon Schnepp.

This is a must-have issue for any Grimm Fairy Tales fan!
