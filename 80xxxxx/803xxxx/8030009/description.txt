Video file format: iso

Why iso? 

This container can be ripped directly to a BD-25 disk and played on a standalone player 

How to play on computer 

1. Download a virtual drive like SlySoft Virtual CloneDrive or DVDfab Virtual Drive  

2. Mount the image / file 

3. Play with a native program like Cyberlink PowerDVD or Corel WinDVD
