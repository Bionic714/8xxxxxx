Asifeh - Iradeh EP
  
http://www.asifeh.com

Asifeh - Iradeh EP is a collection of old + new lyrics dealing with life under physical and mental occupation, and the situation humans are in today, combined with synths, samples, scratches,
hip hop breaks, and traditional instruments like the oud and nay.
