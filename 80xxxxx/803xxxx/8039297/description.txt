  The Lexian Chronicles

English | CBR | 5 Issues

Genre: Fantasy

Written by Harry Gallan
Adaptation by Alan Smithee
Art by Inaki Miranda

Based on the novel by Harry Gallan. 

The once peaceful region of Alemar is threatened by the re-emergence of a powerful tribe hell-bent on settling in the area. Amidst the turmoil that their arrival has caused, Reena has made an incredible discovery which throws a new light upon the history of her tribe and the disasters that befell them. Reena struggles with the responsibilities that have been forced upon her, and seeks comfort in her amazing and secret find.
