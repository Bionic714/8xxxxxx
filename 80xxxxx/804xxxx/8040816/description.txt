World in Conflict: complete edition v1.011
-----------------------------------

A repack of one of the best R.T.S. PC games. A great and compressed installer with everything you need and working under any Windows O.S. Just install and play.



This "Jimbus edition" has:
                      ---

- The original game and its expansion, "Soviet Assault", updated to 1.011 version.
- English audio and texts in English and Spanish.
- A trainer.
- The libraries the game requires.


Nothing has been deleted.

Notice this is not an update but the full game. Do not install it over an existing installation.
