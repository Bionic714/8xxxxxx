Since time immemorial, in every civilization, men reporting strange encounters with evil creatures. Sometimes griffins, sometimes vampires, Stryges have lasted for centuries among us.

Night and evil spirits, women body of a bird, they can take human form to seduce mortals and exhaust their vitality to the death. Disembodied deities, cruel and bloodthirsty, they roam at night to suck blood and eat the flesh of sleeping newborns.

By extension, over the centuries, the term Stryge spread to all that man fears: devil, mermaids, vampires ... to the aliens.
