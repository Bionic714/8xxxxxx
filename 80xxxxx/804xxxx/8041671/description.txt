Starring	Jesse Jane, Ella Milano, Missy Martinez, Tommy Gunn, Manuel Ferrara, Erik Everhard, Pressley Carter
Jesse Jane loves her life living with friends Manuel Ferrara and Erik Everhard. Her boyfriend, Tommy Gunn on the other hand isn't too happy with her. Though they share amazing sex, Tommy seems over the relationship. As a tennis instructor, Manuel is at the mercy of his horny student Missy Martinez who loves to play with his racket and balls. He needs to keep dipping in to her to make sure he makes ends meet. For Erik to make rent, he buys a massage table with hopes of a new income. Instead, he is the recipient of happy endings by super hotties Pressley Carter and Ella Milano. After things fall apart between Jesse and Tommy, will Manuel and Jesse realize they were meant for each other?
