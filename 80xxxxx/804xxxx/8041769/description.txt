
A. Mersmann, "Crystallization Technology Handbook"
English | 2001 | ISBN: 0824705289 | 840 pages | PDF | 6.98 MB

An up-to-date review of the engineering science of crystallization, featuring well-organized and clear guidelines for the building, running and optimization of crystallizer operations. Offers new results on direct-contact cooling crystallization, measures of solubility and supersaturation, surface nucleation on crystals, and other advanced topics.
