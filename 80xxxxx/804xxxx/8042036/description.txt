The Odd Future Tape is the debut mixtape by Odd Future. In 2007, Odd Future consisted of Tyler the Creator, Hodgy Beats, Left Brain, Jasper Dolphin, The Super 3 (Matt Martians and Hal Williams) and Casey Veggies, the latter of which is no longer a member of the group. The songs were recorded on a computer camera microphone.[1] The mixtape was made available for free download in 2008[2] ΓÇô LA Weekly dates the release as November 2008.[3]

The songs "Odd Toddlers" and "Slow It Down" were later released on Tyler, the Creator's debut album Bastard. "Odd Toddlers" was previously released on Casey Veggies's mixtape Customized Greatly under the title "Odd".
