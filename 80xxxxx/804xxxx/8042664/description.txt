This independent Australian production follows young couple Beth and Ryan in the days leading up to their marriage. The pair are seemingly very much in love and enjoy a placid relationship but one night after a big fight Ryan wanders into a gay nightclub. There the desires that have remained hidden inside him for so long find their way to the surface and he begins to experiment engaging in erotic encounters with the men he meets. Confused because he still loves Beth though his recent experiences make him doubt their compatibility Ryan struggles to cope with his newly-discovered sexuality and the decisions he must now make.
