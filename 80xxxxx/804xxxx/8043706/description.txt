Hallmark-The Sweeter Side of Life (2013) 

Air Date: 19 Jan 2013

Summary: Pampered Manhattan housewife Desiree Harper has it all. That is until her husband unexpectedly dumps her for his acupuncturist. Faced with an airtight pre-nup, Desiree reluctantly lands a job making cupcakes at her father's bakery in Hackensack. She soon discovers there's more to life than 5th Avenue and true love can be even sweeter on the other side of the river.
