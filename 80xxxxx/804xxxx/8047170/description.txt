The Unfolding Universe (BBC)

Science Documentary hosted by Patrick Moore, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC The Sky at Night series in 1982 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

A look back at a special programme marking the 25th anniversary of The Sky at Night. Patrick Moore reports on what is happening at great observatories all over the world.
