Michael Hutchinson, "Megabrain: New Tools and Techniques for Brain Growth and Mind Expansion"
1987 | ISBN: 0345341759 | PDF | 372 pages | 3 MB

Readers enter an amazing world where recently developed machines and devices allow us to increase brain size and intelligence, regenerate brain cells, trigger specific brain states such as memory, euphoria and creativity and much more. Science writer Michael Hutchison captures all the drama, excitement, and adventure as we finally begin to comprehend the most mysterious and complex structure in the universe--the human brain.
