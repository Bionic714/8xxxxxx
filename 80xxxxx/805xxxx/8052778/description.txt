  The Screen Awards, initiated by the chairman 
  of The Express Group, Mr. Viveck Goenka in   
  1994, is focused on excellence in films in   
  India. The awards attempts to position       
  themselves as India's first awards that are  
  given by the film fraternity to the film     
  fraternity by way of a jury, as opposed to   
  the other "popular" awards. The awards are   
  also the first to be conducted every year.
