Title: Our Children Aka À Perdre la Raison

Country: Belgium/2012

Language: French and Arabic

Subtitle: French

Director: Joachim Lafosse

Writers: Thomas Bidegain, Joachim Lafosse

Stars: Niels Arestrup, Tahar Rahim and Émilie Dequenne

Plot: Like millions of other couples, Mounir and Murielle fall in love. Like millions of other couples, Mounir and Murielle have children. But unlike them, they accept to give up their autonomy by agreeing to live with Mounir's well off adoptive father, Doctor André Pinget. On the material level, all is well. But a house is not a home, and Murielle feels more and more stifled... 

Trivia: The official entry of Belgium to the Best Foreign Language Film at the 85th Academy Awards 2013. Won Un Certain Regard - Best Actress (Émilie Dequenne) in Cannes Film Festival 2012.
