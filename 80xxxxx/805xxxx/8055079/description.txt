Comics exploration of the Ronald D. Moore version of Battlestar Galactica. The series will go deeper into plotlines extant at certain, discreet points on the BSG televised timeline. It will be a collection of multi-issue arcs, with each arc possibly being set in a different time than the one preceding it. Initially, the stories started at or around the time of the televised story, "Home" and concluded before the story "Resurrection".
Start date of this title reflects the actual shipping month, and may not be the cover month.
