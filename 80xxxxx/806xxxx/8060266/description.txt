~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~  Ninja Escape v1.0  ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Requirements: Android 2.2+

Overview: Ninja Escape is a game of fast paced action where you play as a ninja trying to escape your captors. 

Your reaction times will be put to the test as you dash and pound your way through boulders, sprint across mountain tops and time your jumps just right to make it through tunnels and over gaps for as long as you can before falling to your inevitable death.
