Mechanisms and Mechanical Devices Sourcebook(5 ed)[Team Nanban]

Neil Sclater | ISBN: 0071704426 | 2011 | 560 pages | PDF | 28.3 MB

DESCRIPTION:

Fully revised throughout, this abundantly illustrated reference describes proven mechanisms and mechanical devices. 
Each illustration represents a design concept that can easily be recycled for use in new or modified mechanical, electromechanical, or mechatronic products. 
Tutorials on the basics of mechanisms and motion control systems introduce you to those subjects or act as a refresher.
