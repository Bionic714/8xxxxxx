This historical epic, which is set in the sea in the eighteenth century tells the incredible adventures and tragic of Isa. The young heroine, a noble whose identity has been stolen, found on a ship of the Royal Navy Hoel, a sailor who saves her life. Hoel finds himself trapped in a sinister English Pontoon. Aided by her friend Mary English, Isa manages to free him. Isa, Mary Hoel and embark on board a slave ship, the Marie-Caroline, and arrive at the counter of Judah in Dahomey. Faced with the intrigues of power and spells African Isa must fight to cure Hoel poisoning. Marie-Caroline left for Santo Domingo on board the ebony, that is to say the slaves. These mutiny but their revolt was crushed in a bloodbath. The arrival in Santo Domingo will be crucial to Hoel and Isa.
