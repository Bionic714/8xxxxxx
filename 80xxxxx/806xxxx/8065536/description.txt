Mars Attacks the Transformers #1

Story by Shane McCarthy
Art by Matt Frank

The weekly invasion continues: AUTOBOTS vs. DECEPTICONS vs. ... MARTIANS?! The invaders from Mars take advantage of the colossal Cybertronians' conflict and begin their assault on Earth in full force. It's Martian vs. Machine as the audacious Autobots and the devilish Decepticons begrudgingly join forces to repel the inhuman invaders and save the planet!
