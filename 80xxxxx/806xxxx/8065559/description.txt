NOW AN ONGOING SERIES! Peanuts is back for the new year with Snoopy's most beloved personality taking center stage ... The Flying Ace! Will his Sopwith Camel hold up against the Red Baron long enough for Snoopy to make it back to the cafe for a cold, tall root beer? Charles Schulz's beloved creations keep going strong with all-new stories mixed in with Schulz original Sunday strips.
