Drawn from elements of West African folk tales; it depicts how a newborn boy, Kirikou, saves his village from the evil witch Karaba.
In a little village somewhere in West Africa, a boy named Kirikou is born in a spectacular way. But he's not a normal boy, since he can speak and walk immediately after being born. He is also very determined. His mother tells him that an evil sorceress has dried up their spring and devoured all the males of the village except for one.
