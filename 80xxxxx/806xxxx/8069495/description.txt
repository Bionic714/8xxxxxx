My Classmate's Mother [1-2] [English] [UNCENSORED]



Jun is a lovesick student at Seikyo School. One of his classmate's, Sae, has
the hots for him. But Jun has a taste for the forbidden, ripened fruit of
Sae's mother Keiko, who also happens to be their teacher. Little does he know
that he's about to sample the best of both worlds. Whether he's satisfying
Sae's sexual curiosity or satisfying his own with the experienced Keiko,
Jun always gets an ''A'' for effort!
