A 3 and a half hour all nighter in the archive of Musikladen and Beat Club. Some may find the presenters Andreas and Dirk annoying,But on the other hand they really dig deep into the vaults of Radio Bremen.
Broadcast 1999 on N3.

Depeche Mode, Deep Purple, Jimi Hendrix, Creedence, Sweet, Black Sabbath, America, Cream, T. Rex, Ten Years After, Johnny Winter, Steppenwolf, Who, Canned Heat, ELP, Nazareth, Manfred Mann, Free, Meatloaf, Village People and many, many more.
