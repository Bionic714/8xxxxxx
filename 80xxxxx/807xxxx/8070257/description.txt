Canto Ostinato Centraal, concertgebouw, Amsterdam, January 22 2013

Long-time planned performance for Simeon Ten Holt 90th birthday, but he sadly passed away few months ago on nov. 25, 2012.

Concert was broadcasted live on Concertgebouw website, but was removed shortly after.
As it's almost a crime against humanity such a monumental & hearbtreaking event isn't available anymore, I'm offering it here.

there is a brief video introduction (included in set 1) with interview/readings by Simeon Ten Holt & even a glimpse of his funeral, then 2 consecutive performances.


concert 1 : Canto Ostinato for 4 pianos + organ
Jeroen & Sandra van Veen, Elizabeth & Marcel Bergmann, pianos
Aaart Bergwerff, organ
