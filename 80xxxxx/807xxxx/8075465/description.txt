Grace (Angel Locsin) is a single mother willing to sacrifice everything to save her ill son, Botchok (Miguel Vergara).

disease became severe, Grace was forced to reconnect with Botchok’s biological father, Edward (Dingdong Dantes). 

Edward, an accomplished man married to an equally successful Jacqueline (Angelica Panganiban), is uncomfortable 

with reconnecting with Grace. Having no children of their own, Jacqueline gives her blessings for Edward to
