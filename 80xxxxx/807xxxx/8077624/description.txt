Convert videos to DVD and watch on any DVD player. Automatically burn to DVD after conversion. Excellent and fast converter.

Convert AVI, MKV, MOV, Divx, WMV, MTS/M2TS, MPG, MP4 and more to DVD.

Create menus & chapters, cut videos, total customization with live preview.


Edit your project with the various settings and features listed below:
  -Create menus with videos and music
  -Preview window / Integrated video player for live preview
  -Cut unwanted parts of the video
  -Add chapters - automatic or custom
  -Many subtitles formats supported, convert soft or hard-coded
  -Convert NTSC to PAL and vice-versa
  -Merge function - like for CD1 + CD2
  -Rotate video, modify brightness and contrast settings
  -Compatible with any disks
  -ISO output available
  -NTSC + PAL region formats
  -16:9 widescreen + 4:3 fullscreen
  -Burn multiple copies
  -VSO burning engine included
  -Various SD resolutions available
  -Custom output size
  -Burn to DVD/USB key or store on PC

Additional/Advanced features:
