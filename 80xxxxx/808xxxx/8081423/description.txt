What if Columbus had never discovered America? And if the Indians themselves, discovering Europe? ... Igor Baranko, through this alternate history, tells a universal fable about fate and love. Four Winds is a proud Lakota warrior who, through pride, will remove the daughter of the chief of the Pawnee tribe to make her his wife. Her act triggered the jealousy of a former mistress, a Heyoka who talks to snakes and which will cause the death of Princess Pawnee. This is a killing by mistake Four Winds will realize how much he loved her. In despair, Four Winds will find the Paiutes, the masters of time to changes the world and return in the past, as he prepares to take the girl ... But the world they live in the world is the second attempt. The Paiutes have already had to react to the arrival of demons covetous to the pale skin who crossed the great water ...
