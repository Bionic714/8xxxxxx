You Will Go to the Moon (aka Going to the Moon)- 1971 edition - by Mae and Ira Freeman, and Lee Ames

You Will Go To The Moon is a work of children's literature written by Mae and Ira Freeman and illustrated by Robert Patterson.   A second edition was published in 1971, with new illustrations reflecting NASA's Project Apollo. This was reprinted in England in 1973 under the title Going to the Moon.

The writing is unusual in that it is written in the second-person future tense.
