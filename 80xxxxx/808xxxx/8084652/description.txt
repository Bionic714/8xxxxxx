One by one, the headless horseman continues his ride of death and revenge against those responsible for taking his life. Ty and Brian discover a way to outwit the horseman's curse. But still guilty of their sins, will they elude his wrath? Or learn the hard way that there is no escape from the headless horror? From the minds of Ralph Tedesco, Joe Brusha, Raven Gregory and written by Dan Wickline (Call of Wonderland, 30 Days of Night), this is the one horror series you do not want to miss!
