Advanced SystemCare Professional (formerly Advanded WindowsCare) is a program designed to optimize your PC, very fast (extremely fast) and easy to use.
It shows the basic information about your computer (operating system, processor, physical memory and graphics card). Simply pressing a button, analyze your PC by showing the problems and issues related to optimizing found the processor, memory, network and operating system configuration.
Once found errors, Advanced SystemCare lets you repair and optimize the performance of Windows and even restore the default configuration.
