It is not like any place you've been before...

Pymm is the land of the most mind-blowing math puzzles ever! It is also the land inhabited by humans, elves, dwarves, minotaurs, dragons, and wizards - all trying to solve mathematical riddles and puzzles. As you wander through these eighty-plus puzzles, may the spirit of the great dragon-slayers be with you, because you will face some mighty tricky questions.
