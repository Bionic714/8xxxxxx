Ski Dubai

Technology Documentary hosted by Matt Rogers, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Really Big Things series in 2007 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

A trip to the Middle East to see the world's tallest building and biggest indoor skiing venue. Also: building and installing a 50-foot fossil banner.
