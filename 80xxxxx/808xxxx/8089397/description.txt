Omerta - City of Gangsters is a tactical simulation of tensile fighting that takes us into the twenties of the last century , to the days of Prohibition . 
Gangster life is busy .

The player begins the task of fresh immigrant with big dreams , which start with a small touted gradually rises in the hierarchy of criminals and gang expands its territory . 
Can control 15 characters with different personalities and RPG developments . 
A busy life in Atlantic City is trying to keep a historical master the city streets are broken on maps twenties . 
Singleplayer will last about 15 hours.

Features:

* Conquer a historically accurate Atlantic City with 20 unique districts
featuring real-world landmarks in a story-driven campaign or sandbox
freeplay game
* Strategic real-time gameplay for planning coups, expanding
your territory, extorting the competition, and bribing
the authorities
* Lead your henchmen into elaborately planned turn-based tactical
combat as you pull off bank heists, robberies, street battles
and more
