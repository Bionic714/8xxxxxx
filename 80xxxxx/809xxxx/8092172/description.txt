Album Info


Trunk Muzik 0-60 is the second extended play of American rapper Yelawolf. It was released on November 22, 2010 through Interscope Records in the United States. It features guest appearances by Gucci Mane, Rock City, Rittz, Bun B and Raekwon, and it has six tracks which were featured on his last mixtape entitled Trunk Muzik. Production was handled by WillPower, Jim Jonsin, and Drama Beats, among others.
