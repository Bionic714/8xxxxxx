Conventional Weapons is a compilation album being released sequentially as singles by the American alternative rock band My Chemical Romance, between October 2012 and February 2013. It was announced on September 14, 2012 by band member Frank Iero through the band's official website. The album consists of ten unreleased songs that were recorded in 2009, prior to the making of the band's fourth studio album, Danger Days: The True Lives of the Fabulous Killjoys. Five singles (each containing two songs) will be released once a month from October 2012 to February 2013.[1]
