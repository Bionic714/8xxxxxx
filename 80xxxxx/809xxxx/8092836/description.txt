BBC Northern Ireland documentary following Viscount Crichton, son of the sixth Earl of Erne and heir to the historic family home of Crom Castle in County Fermanagh, as he faces the ever increasing challenge of keeping a castle despite growing bills. Under the watchful eye of his fiercely private father, Lord Erne, and with the dedicated assistance of manager Noel Johnston, he juggles his family heritage and responsibilities with life as a property expert in London. With unique access, cameras follow the Viscount as he opens his home for weddings, tours and TV filming in a determined bid to keep the castle for the Crichtons.
