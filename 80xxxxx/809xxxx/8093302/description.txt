THE CELIBATE RIFLES: THE TURGID MIASMA OF EXISTENCE + ROMAN BEACH HOLIDAY


The Celibate Rifles are an Australian punk rock band.
They formed in 1979.
 
For a full career description see: 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Celibate_Rifles

This CD combines two albums:

- 'The Turgid Miasma of Existence'(1986, Hot Records #1024)
- 'Roman Beach Party'(1987, Hot Records #1030)

Scans of bookletpages are included.

The music files are all in FLAC format (no compression).
