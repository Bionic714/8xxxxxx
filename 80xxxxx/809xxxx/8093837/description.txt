Happy Groundhog Day to my fellow Americans! And hello to all my friends here at TPB! Please enjoy another large set of some excellent collected editions, chosen by me from my last 24 years of comic book reading joy.

Please seed, so others can enjoy it too.

150 trade paperbacks, hardcovers and graphic novels from DC, Marvel, Image, Dynamite and other fine publishers!

(You can probably still grab my first two collections ... 101 different books were in the first and 24 were in the second.)
