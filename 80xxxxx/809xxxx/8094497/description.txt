DVD - Maharishi on Education - 1973 (PAL)

Maharishi at the American Assoc. of Higher Education - DVD

By Maharishi Mahesh Yogi

Color, 45 minutes

Recorded in 1973

In March 1973, more than 3,000 prominent educators met in Chicago to explore the relationship of the quality of life to higher education. Maharishi was the keynote speaker and gave one of the clearest, most systematic and comprehensive explanations to date of the significance of his teaching for education.
