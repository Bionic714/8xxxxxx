B'z  is a Japanese rock duo, consisting of guitarist, composer and producer Takahiro "Tak" Matsumoto and Koshi Inaba, as vocalist and lyricist.

B'z is one of the best-selling music artists in the world, having released 46 consecutive No. 1 singles, 25 No. 1 albums and sold more than 80 million records worldwide.[1] In 2007, B'z became one of the first bands from Asia to have their handprints and signatures put up in the Hollywood's RockWalk.
