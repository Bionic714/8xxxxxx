Title: Jack The Ripper
Genre: adventure / detective / 1st-person
Released: 29th January 2004
Developer: Galilea Games
Publisher: The Adventure Company
Description:
Immerse yourself in an incredibly realistic world of thrills and suspense. Terror ruled the fog-covered streets of east London as Jack The Ripper committed murders of such a vicious nature, that people feared for their lives. Who is this killer that seems to vanish in the mist of darkness? Rise to the challenge and let your instinct help you solve the mystery. Journey through a world with undercurrents of danger as you attempt to solve the case of the world's most elusive serial killer.

Jack The Ripper belongs to the next generation of adventure games: fully 3D characters, real-time 3D animations, and 3 different game phases: exploration, discussion and puzzles.

How to play:
1) Run setup_jack_the_ripper.exe and install the game;
2) Launch the game using main executable or shortcuts (DRM free).

! Warning
Windows 7/Vista users: character voices don't work properly, the sound drops out while they speak. Fortunately, the game has subtitles.
