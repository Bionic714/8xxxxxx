Mens Health Magazine - India January 2013


Men's Health India is a lifestyle magazine that offers health, fitness, nutrition, sex, style and grooming advice to men. While there is an accent on fitness, it is more famous for it's 'how to' sections that answer questions most men have, but don't know who to ask. It is India's largest-selling men's magazine since its launch in 2006, and is the biggest men's magazine in the world.
