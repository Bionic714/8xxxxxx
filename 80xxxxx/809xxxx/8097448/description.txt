Forum Runner allows you to browse your favorite forums on the go!
Upload attachments from your camera or from your library, receive push notifications of private messages and subscriptions, and more. Supports forum moderation for forum admins on-the-go. View and vote on polls, view attached images, and more!
If you're not sure whether a particular forum is included in Forum Runner, you may want to check with the site to confirm.
Currently, vBulletin, phpBB, XenForo, IP.Board, and myBB are supported.
