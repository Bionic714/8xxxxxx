
Written by Dorothy L Sayers, this story features Harriet Vane (later joined by Lord Peter Wimsey). 
The dons of Harriet Vane's alma mater, the all-female Shrewsbury College have invited her back to attend the much anticipated annual 'Gaudy' celebrations. However, the mood turns sour when a a series of malicious pranks including poison-pen messages, obscene graffiti, the destruction of a set of proofs and crafting vile effigies hit the college. Desperate to avoid a possible murder in college, Harriet asks her old friend Lord Peter Wimsey to investigate.
Features Joanna David as Harriet Vane and Ian Carmichael as Lord Peter Wimsey.
