WJSV Complete Broadcast Day On September 21, 1939, radio station
WJSV in Washington, D.C. transcribed an entire day of broadcasting
the same month Germany invaded Poland. 



Also on this day, in Europe, Sept 21, 1939, Heydrich issued
instructions to SS Einsatzgruppen stating the Jewish Poles
are to be gathered into ghettos near railroads for the future
"final goal." He also ordered a census and the establishment
of Jewish administrative councils within the ghettos to
implement Nazi policies and decrees.
