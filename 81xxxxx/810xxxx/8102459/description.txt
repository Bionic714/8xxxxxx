Shauna Grant heads the cast, playing a bad girl for once. She is expelled from her school for making out with sharp-featured Dan Stephens in the men's room and therefore enrolled in Ms. Marmelstein's strict all girls' academy. Shauna is in her early twenties, but she's still the most youthful looking of the bunch of starlets assembled on this occasion, so you'll have to suspend disbelief to buy into this particular fantasy.
