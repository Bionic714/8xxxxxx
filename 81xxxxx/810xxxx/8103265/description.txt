The Philly Kid (2012)

Dutch
Na twaalf jaar in de gevangenis doorgebracht te hebben voor het doden
van een corrupte agent, wordt de voormalige universiteitsworstelkampioen
Dillon McCabe voorwaardelijk vrijgelaten. Dillon keert terug naar zijn
vertrouwde omgeving in Louisiana en probeert uit de problemen te blijven.
Wanneer zijn beste vriend Jake achter raakt met betalingen aan de Russische
maffia, wordt deze ontvoerd en gemarteld. Om hem te redden besluit Dillon
te tekenen voor drie kooigevechten om zo genoeg geld te winnen om Jakes
schulden af te betalen.

Englisch
A former NCAA wrestling champion becomes a successful MMA fighter after
being sent to prison for killing a cop, and battles a corrupt system after
realizing he's been set up to take a big fall. Dillon McCabe (Wes Chatham)
was a natural-born fighter. But the very same night Dillon won the NCAA
wrestling title, he got mixed up in an incident that resulted in the death
of a police officer. Subsequently released from prison after 10 years,
Dillon returns to his Baton Rouge mean-street roots and learns that one of
his oldest friends has crossed a ruthless loan shark. In no time, the ex-con
is rising through the ranks of the local fighting circuit on a mission to
raise the money that will save his friend. But upon learning that the entire
system has been rigged against him, Dillon realizes that his biggest fight
is yet to come. ~ Jason Buchanan, Rovi
