Loveaholic

Director: Ping Lumpraploeng
Cast: Udom Taephanit, Wisa Saansaat, Akara Amarttayakul

Plot Synopsis
Rong, a comedy screenwriter fails to handle his marriage life when his beloved wife, Daeng, secretly has an affair with her gentle and handsome gynecologist. She did it because their marriage has become boring and monotonous. Rong happens to find out about this when he reads her secret diary.
This leads to a tragedy when Rong starts a quarrel and Dang quickly drives her car away full of anger. Later, Rong is told that his wife is dead. He is in a grief and blames himself until something creepy happens in the house. Rong notices that many things in the house are changing their places as if someone has moved them. He starts to believes that Daeng is still around, but his friends think he's crazy. All of a sudden, Daeng returns back to Rong, but he isn't sure if she's a human or a ghost!
