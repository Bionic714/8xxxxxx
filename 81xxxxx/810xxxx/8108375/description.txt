Album Info

Eternal E is a 1995 greatest hits album by the rapper Eazy-E.
This is a re-release of the 1995 and 2003 releases of "Eternal E" - a collection of Eazy-E's various tracks both solo and with N.W.A. Tracks 15 & 16 did not appear on the 1995 release, but were added to the 2003 re-release. Track 17 is a bonus track by Eazy-E's son Lil' E, that did not appear on the 2003 re-release.
