ICE 1 updated for Train Simulator 2013

The ICE for Train Simulator 2012 recreates the original ICE-1 series of trains introduced in 1991 and includes accurate controls (including automatic drive and brake system), passenger view, moveable cab blinds and more. Includes scenarios for the Ruhr Sieg Line and the Seebergbahn route.
