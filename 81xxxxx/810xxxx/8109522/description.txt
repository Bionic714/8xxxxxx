Line of Fire (2002 TV series)
From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Line Of Fire is a documentary produced by Cromwell Productions that shows us historical battles with a difference, the great battlefields of war are presented in an animated environment, providing new insights into military history's most compelling events.
