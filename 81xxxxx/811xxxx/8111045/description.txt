iPhoneography Apps
------------------

This torrent contains premium iphoneography apps. Some of the apps produce d-RAW .tiff images
which are free of jpeg compression in default camera. So the pics captured may be 20mb+ in size.

Recommended for iPhone 4 and above. All of the apps are updated as of 7/2/2013.


Requirements
============
You need to have Jailbroken iPhone with appsync installed through cydia. Just double click on any of the apps. This will launch itunes and app will appear in apps list. After that sync the iPhone as usual.
