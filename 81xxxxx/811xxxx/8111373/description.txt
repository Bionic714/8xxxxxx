Updated Munich to Augsburg route for compatibility with ICE 2.

The Munich-Augsburg route for Train Simulator is a highly-detailed recreation of the route complete with all the unmistakable stations and landmarks. 

Includes

61-kilometer/38-mile high-speed route from Munich to Augsburg, featuring the huge Munich Main Station complete with underground S-Bahn station. 

Class 101 electric locomotive with functioning AFB, PZB90 and LZB 

Class 294 diesel locomotive with functioning PZB90


Does not include ICE 3, available separately.
