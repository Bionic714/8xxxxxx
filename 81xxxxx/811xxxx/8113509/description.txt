I Love Trouble #3

Story by: Kel Symons

"LIFE ON MARS"  Thomas, a blind telepath, recruits Felicia to work with him at the Mars Corporation. Mars is a huge global conglomerate, with their dirty fingers in a lot of pies, most notably the emerging field of nation-building. With her unique talent - able to get to anyone, anywhere - Mars is looking to turn Felicia into an assassin capable of paving the way for new business opportunities.
