Vishwaroopam is a 2013 Tamil-Hindi spy thriller film written, directed and co-produced by Kamal Haasan who also enacts the lead role. The film has Pooja Kumar, Rahul Bose, Andrea Jeremiah and Jaideep Ahlawat in supporting roles, the film features soundtrack composed by Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy, with lyrics by Vairamuthu and Kamal Haasan while Javed Akhtar translated them for the Hindi version titled Vishwaroopam.
