This IP Changer is a tool to change the IP, which your Tibia Client should connect to. It will also work for all versions from Tibia 7.1 to Tibia 9.81
This IP-changer also has support for MC, and the otserv:// protocol.


Visit  
http://www.otservlist.org/
or  
http://www.tibialist.com/
to find your favourite Open Tibia Server.
