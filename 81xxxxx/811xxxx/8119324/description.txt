Horgasm A Love Story DVD is a documentary that follows Torstein Horgmo during 2 years as a world famous professional snowboarder going about his anything but ordinary life.

Horgasm A Love Story is a unique movie made by Tobias Froystad which gives a insight into highs, lows, victories and defeats of professional snowboarder Torstein Horgmo.
