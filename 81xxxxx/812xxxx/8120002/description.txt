SYNOPSIS:- Mahua (1969)
While playing his flute one day in the countryside, Brahmdesh, comes across a beautiful young woman. He is attracted to her and is told that her name is Mahua. When he is about to introduce himself, she stops him and calls him "Palash". When he clarifies that his name is Brahmdesh, Mahua tells him that they are lovers who were separated in a past life by the local Rajkumar, who wanted to marry Mahua. At this point, Mahua disappears, Brahmdesh stumbles and knocks himself unconscious, and is taken back in time when he and Mahua were lovers, pitted against a cruel Rajkumar, who has Palash beaten and left for dead; Mahua who has lost her memory and unable to recall her past. When Brahmdesh regains consciousness, he has to deal with the reality that he cannot marry Mahua even in this lifetime as his marriage is to take place with a woman named Manisha. Will the two star-crossed lovers ever get to meet in this lifetime
