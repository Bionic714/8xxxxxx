Star Wars: Union (1999)

Publisher: Dark Horse Comics
Publication Date: November 1999 - February 2000
Country: United States
Language: English 

Notes: 
This title concluded the story told in the Timothy Zahn duology, Specter of the Past and Vision of the Future. It showed the wedding of Luke and Mara that was promised in those novels. As of 2006, it was also the latest point on the Star Wars timeline ever depicted in comics.

Number of issues cataloged: 4
