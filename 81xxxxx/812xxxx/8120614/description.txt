Cast The First Stone is a Raleigh, North Carolina based band. Their musical style is a mix of metal, hardcore, experimental and pop. You should check them out if you like unique catchy music that doesn't hold back from breaking it down in the middle of the song with their bone-crushing heavy sound. They're melodic style heaviness brings something to the table that you can't easily turn away from. They have played with many bands such as A Day To Remember, Alesana, God Forbid, Bury Your Dead, The Chariot, He Is Legend, BrokenCyde, Emmure, My Children My Bride, White Chapel, Stick To Your Guns, Shai Halud, In Fear And Faith, A Skylit Drive, For The Fallen Dreams, Born Of Osiris, Dr. Acula, After The Burial... the list goes
