Guitar Tab Player is simple and very fast tab reader and player. It let you watch and play your tabs and music notation, simple and fast. Support ANY guitar pro files (including Guitar Pro 6). Just type what you want to play and go along with it!
 Because details matter. Correctly shows and plays downward and upward glissando. Sees difference between legato slide and shift slide. Correctly shows and plays  transition of  techniques  (downward glissado -&gt; legato slide -&gt; bend) . 

- You can switch pages with swap gesture.
- Long tap for additional menu.
- Click on measure counter to go to the selected measure.

You can change screen orientation throught the menu.
