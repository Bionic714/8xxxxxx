Tages var en popgrupp i Sverige under 1960-talet. I konkurrens med bland andra
Hep Stars ansågs/anses de ofta ha varit en av de bästa svenska popgrupperna
från den eran.
Gruppen spelade mycket eget material, framför allt skrivet av bandmedlemmen
Göran Lagerberg, samt en del covers. 
http://sv.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tages

Tages was a Swedish pop group formed in the early sixties near Gothenburg.
The band released a number of singles and LPs in their native Sweden to
considerable success, making the Swedish Top Ten more than a dozen times. 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tages_(band)

Original bandmembers:

Tommy Blom, vocal, guitar (substituted by Örjan Ramberg at several occasions)
Danne Larsson, rythm guitar, vocal
Göran Lagerberg, bass, vocal
Anders Töpel, lead guitar
Freddie Skantze, drums -66

Additional bandmembers:
Tommy Tausis, drums 66-67 
Lars Svensson, drums 67-69
Anders "Henkan" Henriksson, producer. On some recordings he plays piano/organ
