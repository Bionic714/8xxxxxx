Running Time: 88 min
Genre: Drama
Country: South Africa 
Language: Afrikaans 
Subtitles: English (hardcoded)
Release Date: 2012 (South Africa)

A promising young singer is traumatised by her near death by strangling and the murder of her parents. She takes refuge on her relations' farm in the Karoo, and resorts to silence as a way of protecting herself. However things change when she meets the local minister and slowly begins a process of rehabilitation.
