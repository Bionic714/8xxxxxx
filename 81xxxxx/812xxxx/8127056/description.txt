February 9, 2013
Ring of Fire 
Hosted by Robert F. Kennedy Jr., Mike Papantonio and Sam Seder


On Ring of Fire:  Congresswoman Jan Schakowsky will give us the latest on the debt ceiling battle in Washington.

Dan Petegorsky from the Campaign for a Fair Settlement joins us to talk about why the Justice Department is so afraid to put Wall Street bankers in prison.

Author and Nation contributor Ari Berman will tell us why Republicans want to gut the Voting Rights Act.

And Pardiss Kebriaei from the Center for Constitutional Rights will discuss the human rights violations being committed by way of US drone strikes.
