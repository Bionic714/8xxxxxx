Bloodhound Gang is an American band, beginning as a rap group but gradually changing its genre throughout the years. Their songs usually have humorous and off-beat, satirical lyrics that often deal with sexual subjects and contain many puns and innuendos.
They are best known for their hit singles "Fire Water Burn", "The Bad Touch", "Foxtrot Uniform Charlie Kilo", "Along Comes Mary", "Uhn Tiss Uhn Tiss Uhn Tiss" and "The Ballad of Chasey Lain". Formed in 1991,[1] Bloodhound Gang has sold more than 6 million albums.[1][2]
