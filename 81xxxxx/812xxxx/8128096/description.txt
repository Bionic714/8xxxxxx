A 1 hour single-episode special based on the Dragon Ball manga series. 
Originally airing in Japan between episodes 175 and 176, the special is based on an extra chapter of the manga series. 
It depicts an alternate version of the future in which Goku dies from a virus and a teenage Trunks tries to defeat the androids ravaging Earth.
