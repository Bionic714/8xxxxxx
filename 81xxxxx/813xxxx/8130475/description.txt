The Block All-Stars Series 6 Episode 4

The Block is Back - in the all new All-Stars format. The Block is back to it's root is Sydney, New South Wales.

Phil and Amity are stepping back in time 10 years... and back onto The Block!

Mark and Duncan The tradie mates are back, but how will they keep up with their much-younger contestants?

Dan and Dani's win-at-all-costs attitude rubbed their Block neighbours the wrong way in 2012. Now, they're determined to make amends.

Four all star couples are given with the toughest renovation challenge they have ever faced. In just six weeks they will have to completely make over four heritage listed homes in one of the suburbs best streets.

In the race to the finish line, the teams find that completing heritage listed rooms is far harder than they ever imagined. They will not only be judged on their design prowess but their ability to restore the rooms to their former glory.
