The life of Bhishma Pitamaha is the story for this film. It includes his birth to Santana, how he became Bhishma from Devavrata, bringing Amba and Ambalica in Swayamvar for the marriage of his brother Vichitravira, and his role of Kaurava kingdom and Mahabharata war and his demise. It means the entire Mahabharata in short format.
