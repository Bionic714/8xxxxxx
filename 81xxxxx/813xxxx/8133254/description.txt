Macmillan Childrens Dictionary 
by C. Barraclough
Macmillan | May 2001 | ISBN-10: 0333953037 | PDF | 96 pages | 12.4 mb

The Macmillan Children's Dictionary combines clear, vibrant photographs with graded puzzle-type activities to bring a new dimension to vocabulary resources. The topicbased approach provides a meaningful context to reinforce memorization.
The Macmillan Children's Dictionary is intended mainly as a classroom resource which will help primary-age children consolidate and enlarge their vocabulary through guided practice. It can also be used as a personal reference resource al home with the aid of the alphabetical wordlist.
