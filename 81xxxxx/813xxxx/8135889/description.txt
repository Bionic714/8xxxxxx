This is the F40PH California Zephyr updated 21 December 2012 to work with the updated Donner Pass.

The F40PH for Train Simulator recreates the Amtrak Phase III colour scheme and includes Amtrak Super-Liner Phase II and Phase III passenger cars including coach, diner and sleeper, and baggage cars. Drive the included scenarios for the Donner Pass or select Quick Drive and drive the F40PH wherever you want.

Donner Pass, available separately, is required to play the included scenarios.
