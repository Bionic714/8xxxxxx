description
The program is designed BusinessCards MX for creating and designing business cards. With the help of this program you can design and print professional business cards easily and quickly.
The program interface is designed so that even novice users have no problems with using the program. The design process is based on the placement of elements accessible in the program, such as text, figures, images and the background, and freely shifting them on the prepared project.
The program uses templates of business cards, which we can prepare ourselves or use the model cards offered by the program.
The program has about 750 business cards templates.
