Profile

Stephen "Stevie" Ray Vaughan (October 3, 1954 ΓÇô August 27, 1990) was an
American guitarist, singer-songwriter, and record producer. Often referred
to by his initials SRV, Vaughan is best known as a founding member and leader
of Double Trouble. Together with drummer Chris Layton and bassist Tommy Shannon
they ignited the blues revival of the 1980s. With a career spanning seven years
Vaughan and Double Trouble consistently sold out concerts while their albums
frequently went gold.
