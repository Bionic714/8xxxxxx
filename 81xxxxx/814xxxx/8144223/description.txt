Enemy of the Mind - Independent Feature Film made in Austin, Texas

A hardened cop chases the shadow of a killer whose deadly trail he believes started with the murder of his wife Abby. With each new victim, Detective Frank del Toro must find a way to stop him, even at the cost of his job, his reputation and his sanity.

Starring Manny Rey, Allison Wood, Ed Hattaway, Jennifer Joseph, Chris Osborn

Directed by Brandon Stephens

Written by Manny Rey and Brandon Stephens
