Cabin Pressure - Series 4 (2013) BBC Radio 4 

    
http://www.comedy.org.uk/guide/radio/cabin_pressure/episodes/


Cast:

Carolyn Knapp-Shappey ..... Stephanie Cole
1st Officer Douglas Richardson ..... Roger Allam
Capt. Martin Crieff ..... Benedict Cumberbatch
Arthur Shappey ..... John Finnemore
Captain Hercules "Herc" Shipwright... Anthony Head



Written by John Finnemore

1. Timbuktu (Wed 9th January 2013)
Hooray hooray, it's Birling Day once more, where the crew traditionally swap their dignity for cash! But where have all the camels gone? And why is Arthur reading a book?

2. Uskerty (Wed 16th January 2013)
Carolyn and Martin get into an argument, and Douglas and Arthur get to play with an airport.

3. Vaduz (Wed 23rd January 2013)
It's a bad time for Carolyn to take a holiday, as the crew of MJN Air have to face a real live king and a mythical fax machine.

4. Wokingham (Wed 30th January 2013)
When his mum falls ill, Martin has to deal with a sister, a van and a moustache. Meanwhile, Carolyn and Douglas become locked in a game that can never end.

5. Xinzhou (Wed 6th February 2013)
It's cold, it's dark and there's no food on board. What better time for Gertie to decide to fall to bits? And what worse time for Arthur to try out his maths skills?

6. Yverdon-Les-Bains (Wed 13th February 2013)
Love is in the air, as Douglas and Herc fight it out over the fruit tray, and hope springs eternal, as Martin has the interview of his life.
