If you don’t know die Ärzte (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ärzte), they’re a great German punk band from Berlin. They’re very popular in Germany, Austria and Switzerland, especially since their lyrics are pretty funny, as long as you understand German, at least. I tried to download their discography on TPB, but couldn’t find one that was very complete, so I compiled it from elsewhere and bring it to you now.

I have most of their most important stuff here, including some singles, B-sides from EPs and a few random songs. Everything’s tagged fairly well, though only the live albums are divided into separate folders (sorry, but that’s my personal preference). FYI, I tried to avoid duplicate songs by only labeling them as being on their original albums:
