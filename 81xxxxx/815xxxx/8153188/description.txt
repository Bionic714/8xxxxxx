Storyline:
Stavros is a shopkeeper. His wife has left him and refuses to return - his mother has already suffered a stroke and Stavros have to watch her 24 hours a day. The favorite pastime of Stavros and three friends who also are shopkeepers in the neighborhood, is the daily count of the Chinese, who are setting up shop in their opposite.A job that has no end because the Chinese seem to multiply by the day. Counting is stopped only if an Albanian pass .Then they beting if the dog bark the Albanian or not.That's the second favorite occupations. So pass their time, loose and fun, watching the life changing outside their shops. However Stavros is constantly worried. Unexplainable unhappy. At nights he can't sleep but he can not understand why. Until one day a passer Albanian recognizes Stavros mother as his own lost mother ...
