La Grande to Hinkle
See the Union Pacific fight and win as it takes on the Blue Mountains of eastern Oregon - the toughest challenge UP faces! Loaded with spectacular cab rides, Volume II hits all the highlights of this dramatic mountain district from La Grande to Hinkle.

Scale the Blue Mountains from La Grande through Hilgard and Motanic to the summit at Kamela. Ride the cab of a new GE Dash 8. Get an engineer's view from a helper set on the rear of a UP freight. Experience Meacham Creek Canyon, the Umatilla River, Pendleton, and much more all in this exciting video!

The result of meticulous and superior production techniques, this outstanding video is 60 minutes of colorful action and full Hi-Fi stereo sound. It's a rich story told as only the award-winning Video Rails can tell it. A story you'll be proud to own!

    Enjoy dramatic cab rides on a new GE Dash 8-40C
    Feel the power as SD-40-2s, C36-7s, 8-40-Cs and SD-60s take on UP's roughest helper district.
    Thrill to exhilarating pacing scenes of the newest SD-60.
    Watch Trailer trains, container trains, double stacks, grain trains, soda ash trains, auto rack trains and AMTRAK all take on the terrain.
    Don't miss your chance to experience the Blue Mountains as never before.
