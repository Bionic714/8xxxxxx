Recorded live at the Paris Pavillion, Paris, France on November 29, 1979.
Naturally, they were all blouse-wearers and long-haired exponents of the pro-rock scene at the time but, once you rise above the inevitable fashion statements, the live performance, supported by back-projected ævideoÆ on a handful of tracks, is outstanding.
