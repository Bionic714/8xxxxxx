Stepherd proudly presents: Beyonce Discography 2003 - 2012

Beyonce Giselle Knowles-Carter, often referred to simply as Beyonce, is an American singer, songwriter, dancer and actress. Born and raised in Houston, Texas, Knowles enrolled in performing arts schools and was first exposed to singing and dancing competitions as a child. She rose to fame in the late 1990s as the lead singer of Destiny's Child, one of the world's best-selling girl groups of all time.
