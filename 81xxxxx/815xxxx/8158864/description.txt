Description:

Easy Answer allows you to easily answer phone calls and intelligently control the speaker phone.
Easy Answer supports the following features:
ΓÇó Answer incoming phone call by shaking device.
ΓÇó Answer incoming phone call by placing device close to ear.
ΓÇó End call by shaking device.
ΓÇó End call by placing phone upside down.
ΓÇó Automatically enable the speaker phone when phone lies flat.
ΓÇó Automatically disable the speaker phone when phone is not flat.
ΓÇó Automatically enable the speaker phone when phone is away from your ear.
ΓÇó Automatically disable the speaker phone when phone is close to your ear.
ΓÇó Adjust the amount of shaking required to answer / end calls.
ΓÇó All features can be enabled or disabled.
Yes, there are other apps on the market that do the same thing so why choose mine?
ΓÇó This application has been carefully tested and works.
ΓÇó Efficient. It will not burn through your battery.
ΓÇó Easy answer is only active on phone calls and will not be silently running in the background at other times.
ΓÇó Does NOT require internet access or access to your contacts.
