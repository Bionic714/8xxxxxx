Outer Space Trilogy - Documentary - Avi


Three part TV series about space. 


Part One follows Nasa specialists and others as they investigate the possible dangers to the earth from asteroids, solar storms and space bacteria. 

Part Two is a general history of space exploration.  

Part Three follows a shuttle crew as they prepare for a mission to the International Space Station.
