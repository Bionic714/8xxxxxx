Title: Traitors Gate 2
Genre: adventure / puzzle-solving / 3rd-person
Released: 28th October 2003
Developer: 258 Productions
Publisher: Nordic Games
Description:
The world has seen all kinds of weapons of mass destruction: nuclear, chemical and biological. Now the threat is digital. Traitors Gate 2 is an adventure game in which you have to destroy a computer virus that threatens to shut down all communications in the world. You play as Raven, an operative, and your mission is to find the virus, copy it and then destroy the research lab where it is located.

Get ready to explore an ancient Babylonian location encompassing more than 30 chambers and rooms filled with puzzles and enemies. The game combines stealth, exploration, espionage, and gadgetry with an intense storyline. Do your best and save the World from this digital threat!
