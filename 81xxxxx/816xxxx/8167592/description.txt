  Highborn, you're treated to a strategy game that  offers  an  intelligent,
  tactical experience without the frenetic play of a traditional  RTS.  Move
  your forces around an overhead map to secure spells  and  combat  support,
  then dive into combat to deploy those advantages against  an  unsuspecting
  enemy. All the fun and fighting is set against an irreverent tale,  filled
  with   wacky   heroes,    witty    dialogue    and    wild    assumptions.



   Features: 
   ~~~~~~~~~
  * A New Strategy: A unique blend of strategic movement and combat caters
    to a variety of gameplay styles and offers lots of replayability.
  * Many Styles of Play: Unlock hero characters for access to different
    abilities, play styles, and strategies.
  * Achieve Greatness: More than 20 achievements to unlock, including
    judicious use of turns, using units as bait, and making it through an
    entire mission without capturing support structures.
