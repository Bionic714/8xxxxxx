Title: Bridge Project
Genre: construction / simulation / puzzle-solving
Released: 15th October 2012
Developer: Caipirinha Games
Publisher: Chronic Logic
Description:
Bridge Project is the successor to the original and award-winning Bridge Builder simulation. All amateur designers and structural engineers finally have a new challenge on their PCs. The gameplay has been subject to much tweeking and development with new up-to-date materials, exciting scenarios and a variety of tasks waiting to be solved. Build stable bridges in a variety of landscapes with detailed environments. Take on the task of constructing a huge variety of bridges: suspended, folding, stone, steel, wood, car or railway bridges. Enjoy the enhanced graphics and the new, improved physics engine which puts your bridges to the test.

Minimum system requirements: Windows XP/Vista/7, 2.4GHz Core2Duo or equivalent CPU, 2 GB RAM, 1.3 GB HDD space, 3D Graphics card with 256 MB RAM (minimum GeForce 8600GT or similar card).

How to play:
1) Run Setup.exe and install the game (you will be asked to enter a serial number, just write whatever you want);
2) Launch the game using main executable or shortcuts.
