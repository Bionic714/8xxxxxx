What should have been a typical work day for Cindy (Samantha Saint), has turned into the ultimate day of "Guess Who". For Cindy has received her fourth gift this month from her secret admirer. With the help of her best friend Carina (Alektra Blue), they will turn this office upside down trying to figure out who is Cindy's secret admirer. Could it be the office bully Rex, or maybe the heart throb salesman Daniel, or maybe even the kinky accountant Karen. Follow Cindy along as we find out who her secret admirer really is; it's sure to be a funny, sex-filled adventure.
