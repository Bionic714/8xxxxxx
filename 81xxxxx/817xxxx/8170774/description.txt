Chasing the Dead #4

The mystery of Sue's horrific journey comes to an explosive conclusion, as she reaches her final destination. Sue must escape an undead army in her struggle against the Harvester, and to save her daughter she must confront her mysterious torturer face-to-face in a battle till sunrise.
