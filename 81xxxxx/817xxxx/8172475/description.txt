Ola and the Janglers, svensk popgrupp bildad i Stockholm 1962. Från början
bestod bandet av: Ola Håkansson (sång), Christer Idering (gitarr), Johannes
Olsson (orgel), Lennart Gudmundsson (bas), och Leif Johansson (trummor).
Gruppen tog särskilt starkt intryck av två brittiska musikgrupper, Manfred Mann
och The Zombies. Deras första låt som kom att hamna på Tio i topp var också en
cover på Zombies "She's Not There".
Claes af Geijerstam ersatte Idering som gitarrist och Åke Eldsäter ersatte
Lennart Gudmundsson som basist 1965. 
http://sv.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ola_%26_the_Janglers

Ola & the Janglers were a garage rock and beat group, founded in Stockholm,
Sweden in 1962. Its lead member was Ola Håkansson. The group's 1969 hit "Let's
Dance", a cover of the Chris Montez song, reached #92 on the Billboard Hot 100.
This was the first entry on that list by a Swedish group/artist.
Between March 1965 and October 1968 they had 13 entries to the Swedish top
chart “10 I topp”. They reached first place with the songs “Love Was On Your
Mind", "Poetry In Motion", "Alex Is The Man" and "Let's Dance".
After disbanding some of the members formed Secret Service who had some hits
in the 80s, "Oh Susie", "Ten O'Clock Postman" and "Flash in the Night".
