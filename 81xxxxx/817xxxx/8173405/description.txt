---------------------------------------------------------------
SCOTT WESLEY BROWN DISCOGRAPHY
---------------------------------------------------------------

This Scott Wesley Brown discography may or may not be complete.  Included are the following 11 releases...

 * 1973 - Scott Wesley Brown
 * 1978 - Songs And Stories Live
 * 1981 - SWB
 * 1982 - Signature
 * 1984 - Kingdom Of Love
 * 1987 - The Language Of Jesus Is Love
 * 1988 - Sparrow
 * 1988 - To The Ends Of The Earth
 * 1998 - Out Of Africa
 * 1999 - Mission Of Praise
 * 2010 - Songs From The Valley

Scott Wesley Brown is an American CCM singer and songwriter. He recorded for Sparrow Records for much of his career.  His songs have been covered by Petra, Pat Boone, Bruce Carroll, Sandi Patty, Amy Grant, and others. Brown has also worked with organizations such as Promise Keepers and Campus Crusade for Christ, and was a worship pastor in San Diego.
