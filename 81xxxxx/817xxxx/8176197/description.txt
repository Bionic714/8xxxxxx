The Who are an English rock band formed in 1964 by Roger Daltrey (lead vocals,
guitar, harmonica), Pete Townshend (guitar, vocals, keyboards), John Entwistle
(bass, brass, vocals) and Keith Moon (drums, vocals). They became known for
energetic live performances which often included instrument destruction. The
Who have sold about 100 million records, and have charted 27 top forty singles
in the United Kingdom and United States, as well as 17 top ten albums, with 18
Gold, 12 Platinum and 5 Multi-Platinum album awards in the United States alone.
