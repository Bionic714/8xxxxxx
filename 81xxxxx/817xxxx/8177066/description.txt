Rebecka Törnqvist (born 26 April 1964 in Uppsala, Sweden) is a Swedish jazz and
pop vocalist. Her debut album A Night like This was released in 1993 and sold
over 100,000 copies. She was nominated for five Swedish Grammys in 1993. This
was followed by Good Thing in 1995, which got a platinum record and got her a
Swedish Grammy Best Female Pop/Rock Artist of the Year in 1996
Good Thing topped the Swedish album chart and the title track became a huge
hit. Some of the musicians on the album are Esbjörn Svensson, Peter Asplund,
Per "Texas" Johansson and Max Schultz.
