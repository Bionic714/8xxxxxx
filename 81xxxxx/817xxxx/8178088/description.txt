Toto is an American rock band formed in Los Angeles in 1976. The band's current
lineup consists of Joseph Williams (lead vocals), David Paich (keyboards),
Steve Porcaro (keyboards), Steve Lukather (guitars, vocals), and Simon Phillips
(drums). Bass player Nathan East is currently touring with Toto as a guest
musician, as Mike Porcaro is too ill to tour. Toto is known for a musical style
that combines elements of pop, rock, soul, funk, progressive rock, hard rock,
R&B and jazz.
