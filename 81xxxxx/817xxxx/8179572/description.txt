An android application to send your phones mobile internet signal via Wifi to other devices (ie: laptop, tablets, etc.) 

***THIS APPLICATION REQUIRES ROOT ACCESS***

To use simply put the .apk on your sd, and run from your file manager on android.
This application may not be the most recent, however it is fully functioning.
