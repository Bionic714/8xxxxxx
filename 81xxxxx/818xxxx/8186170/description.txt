Death on the Staircase (original title: Soupçons) is a French 2004 TV miniseries in 8 parts documenting the private life and public trial of Michael Peterson, accused of murdering his wife.

In 2013, a follow-up episode was released under the name "Death on the Staircase - The Last Chance".

This torrent contains web-rips of the original 8-part miniseries as well as the newly released follow-up episode (labelled as episode #9 in the file list). The video and sound is watchable, but don't expect cinematic quality as these are web-rips.
