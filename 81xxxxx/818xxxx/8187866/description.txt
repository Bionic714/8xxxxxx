Installation:

Windows:
1.Run S-CCPRO_-280WU-___EN-32BIT_.exe to launch the software installer.
2.Follow the on-screen instructions to accurately complete the installation.
3.Uninstall Nikon Message Center (recommend).
4.Run Camera Control Pro 2.8 and enter the serial number when ask.
5.Run S-CCPRO_-021200WU-___EN-32BIT_.exe from update folder
6.Uninstall Nikon Message Center (recommend).
7.Run Camera Control Pro 2.12 and enter the serial number from update folder when ask.

**************************************

digital SLR functions from a personal computer, and includes advanced features
such as "Viewer" that provide substantial improvements in operation.
