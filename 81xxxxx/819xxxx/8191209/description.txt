Meet the Ninya Baginya, ninja and superhero in the small town of Arlandash, California. Now meet the formerly straight-laced Elias, who has become a stoner, womanizer and general man of leisure. now sit back and laugh as these two old buddies attempt to rekindle their friendship. The result is a decadent series of ridiculous mishaps and the creation of a whole new hero, with a surprisingly smokey power.
