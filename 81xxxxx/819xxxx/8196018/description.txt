Identity

Identity is a British police procedural drama television series starring Aidan Gillen and Keeley Hawes, airing in the UK during JulyΓÇôAugust 2010. Concerning identity theft, the series was created and written by Ed Whitmore, a writer most noted for his work on the BBC's Waking The Dead and the acclaimed ITV mini-series He Kills Coppers. 
The Identity Unit led by D/Supt Martha Lawson, the founder of the unit, specializes in cases concerning identity fraud by outsmarting, hunting down and unmasking the modern day Jekyll and Hydes. 

Cast:
Aidan Gillen as DI John Bloom 
Keeley Hawes as Detective Superintendent Martha Lawson 
Holly Aird as Tessa Stein 
Elyes Gabel as DC Jose Rodriguez 
Shaun Parkes as DS Anthony Wareing 
Patrick Baladi as Assistant Commissioner Hugh Wainwright
