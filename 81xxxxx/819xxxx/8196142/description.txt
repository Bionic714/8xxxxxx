This recipe collection is designed to let people, regardless of their cooking experience, get the most meat for their money. You need not waste a morsel of chicken or suffer the after Thanksgiving turkey hum-drums. With this book, you can even put squirrels on the dinner plate, using a wide variety of recipes to suit every palate. The recipes included are suitable for any poultry or "redneck chicken," as squirrel meat is known by the author's family.
