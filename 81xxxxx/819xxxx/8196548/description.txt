North of 60


North of 60 is a mid-1990s Canadian television series depicting life in the sub-Arctic northern boreal forest. It first aired on CBC Television in 1992 and was syndicated around the world. Wikipedia
First episode: December 3, 1992
Final episode: December 18, 1997
Genre: Drama
Networks: Canadian Broadcasting Corporation, CBC Television
Writers: Hart Hanson, Wayne Grigsby
