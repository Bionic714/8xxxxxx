The Game of Thrones Quotes fan-made app is a perfect way to relive your favorite Game of Thrones moments by reading the most memorable quotes from this smashing TV hit. 
You can choose quotes from the main characters, mark them as favorite, post them onor tweet them to share with your friends. 
With almost 100 most humorous and touching citations from all the characters of this famous series (citations from books not included), you will find yourself drawn into the world of the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros all over again.
