description
CCleaner is a tool that helps you to keep your system running smoothly doing a thorough cleaning that improves overall performance and increases the free disk space.
CCleaner removes temporary files, files that "hung" after installations and lists of recently used files. Also empty the trash on your PC and removes traces of browsing.
Before deleting, CCleaner lets you specify which items you want to delete and which prefer to keep.
CCleaner also includes a Windows Registry Cleaner that scans for invalid entries.

It runs on Microsoft Windows 8, 7, Vista and XP. Including both 32-bit and 64-bit.
