The Cahill Files is a series of two books and 3 e-books. Clifford Riley, the author of Rapid Fire, is the author of all the books. The titles of the books are:

Operation Trinity(May 8, 2012)

The Submarine Job (June 5, 2012)

The Redcoat Chase (July 3, 2012)

The Houdini Escape (August 7, 2012)
