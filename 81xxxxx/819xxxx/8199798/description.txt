The Art of the Matrix


HQ PDF | 488 pages 

The Art of The Matrix goes beyond the film. But you cannot be told what this book isΓÇªyou have to see it for yourself! First published in February 2001, almost two years after the release of the now landmark film (March 31, 1999), this almost 500-page (including gatefolds), five pound, one-of-a-kind book with over 1,000 color and black-and-white illustrations, is the pre-production archive of the script, artwork, and conceptual drawings. Over 26,000 copies have been sold to date and acclaimed by reviewers and fans of The Matrix worldwide.
