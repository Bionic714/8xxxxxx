Band name: Hillsong (Australia)
Album name: Zion
Released: 2013

"Zion is the third studio album by Australian Contemporary Christian & Alternative Rock band Hillsong United. Production for the album began in March 2011 in Sydney, Australia. The album is released on February 22, 2013 for Australia[1] and February 26 Internationally. The album is available in standard and Deluxe Edition (CD + DVD) plus digital."
