The Mormons Are Here

Arts Documentary hosted by Alan Yentob, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC The Culture Show series in 2013 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Alan Yentob talks to South Park creators Trey Parker and Matt Stone about their comedy stage musical The Book of Mormon. Already a huge hit in the US, it is now opening in London's West End. This Culture Show special reflects on the duos extraordinary career and reveals how music has always played a crucial role in their creative output - from early student films through to South Park and Team America: World Police.
