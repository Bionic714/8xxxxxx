How to Grow Your Own Garden Bedding Plants for Fun and Profit is a short course on how to start bedding plants for your garden. The purpose of this book is to provide you a method to grow your own bedding plants. This is not a book for the beauty of plants. Its purpose is to provide you with good inexpensive bedding plants for your garden.

This method can be used for any plant from your flower needs to the finest tomato plant. You can start any plant from seed with this method. Grow your own tomatoes. The book takes you from zero information on growing bedding plants to having the plants in your garden.
The most valuable information in the course is the pictures. Each picture is worth about 10 pages of information.
