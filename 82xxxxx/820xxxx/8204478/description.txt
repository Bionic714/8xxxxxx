Every comic book of the Archie Comics Sonic The Hedgehog universe up to February 2013. More than 400 comic books, including the Sonic, Sally, Tails, Knuckles and Sonic Quest miniseries, the full run of the Knuckles the Echidna and Sonic X titles, all 10 Specials, 15 Super Specials, the Free Comic Book Day issues from 2007-2012, issues 1-245 of the main title and 1-49 of Sonic Universe, plus a Map of Mobius, storyline reading order, and a CBR reader installation program so you can read them.
