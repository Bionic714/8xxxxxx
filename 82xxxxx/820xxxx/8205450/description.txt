Four-issue series for epic Comics in 1992 written by Steve White & Andy Lanning and illustrated by Phil Gascoine.

The 'Dreadlands' are Earth's future wastelands, inhabited by nomadic scavengers...but underneath the Dreadlands lies a military/scientific complexe where wonderous new technologies are being developed that may save the Earth - including time travel...!


Praise be unto the creative team and original scanners.
