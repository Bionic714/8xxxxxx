Storyline


James Harvey is a ghost doctor hired by Carrigan Crittendon to rid 
Whipstaff Manor of spirits so she can find a treasure trove rumored to 
be hidden there. But her plan backfires when James's daughter Kat 
befriends Casper, the friendly phantom who inhabits the place along 
with The Ghostly Trio, who disdain humans.
