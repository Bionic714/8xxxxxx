Storyline: 

Jean-Baptiste Grenouille, born in the stench of eighteenth century Paris, 
develops a superior olfactory sense, which he uses to create the world's 
finest perfumes. His work, however, takes a dark turn as he tries to 
preserve scents in the search for the ultimate perfume
