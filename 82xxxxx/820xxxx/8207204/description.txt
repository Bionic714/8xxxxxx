Ravi Shankar and Ali Akbar Khan - In Concert 1972

"In Concert 1972 is a double live album by sitar virtuoso Ravi Shankar and "master of the sarod" Ali Akbar Khan, recorded in New York City in October 1972 and released in early 1973. The album appeared on Apple Records and was co-produced by George Harrison. It was the final Shankar-related release on The Beatles' label."
