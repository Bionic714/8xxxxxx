Second instalment of La Santes music comes packed with raw oldschool sounds. The time gap between current and previous album is small but the sound got more refined and La Santes style matured.
Chleb z Kokain─à (Polish for Bread with Cocaine) will be a very welcome release for all those who follow oldschool electronic sounds. The music is not however an attempt to bring past musical genres back to life, it rather attempts to create an laternative evolution path.
