Steelgrip Starkey and the All-Purpose Power Tool - yup - was a six-issue limited series created by Alan Weiss and published by Epic Comics in 1986. Positioning itself as a modern-day folk tale in the spirit of Paul Bunyan, John Henry, and Rosie the Riveter, it chronicles the adventures of Steelgrip Starkey as he embodies the virtues of honest labor.
