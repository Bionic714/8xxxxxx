Story


Simpleton Raj Verma alias Raju return home one
day to find that his sister is publicly raped by gangster 
Beharry and one of his wrestlers also Raj's childhood 
friend Duryodhan. When Raj intervene he is beaten, publicly 
humiliated and left for dead, and then he is taken to the 
hospital and survive by a miracle. After recovery Raj swears 
to take revenge on Duryodhan and Beharry and is trained to 
become an expert wrestler by a man named Baba Thakur. But 
the question remains will Raj be able to cope with these 
trainings and if he does will he be able to take on 
Duryodhan who is already an expert in this field?
