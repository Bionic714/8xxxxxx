Fairy Mahjong is a highly innovative and addictive 3D skill-based puzzle game. Combining outstanding levels, colorful graphics and great audio effects it is one of the most original and fun games on the Android.
Fairy Mahjong combines mahjong and 3-matching game-plays with accurate touch controls in a 3D environment.

- Beautiful 3D graphics
- Easy, normal and hard game modes
- Great soundtracks and audio effects
