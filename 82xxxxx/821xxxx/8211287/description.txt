Dutch
De moordenaar Ryan Weaver wordt op Kerstavond samen met enkele andere gevangenen met een vliegtuig overgebracht naar de gevangenis.
Ryan weet samen met een andere gevangene de bewakers te overmeesteren, en de gehele crew (op Teri Halloran na) te vermoorden.

English
Ryan Weaver romances beautiful women before he kills them. Ryan is arrested, convicted,
and is part of a Christmas Eve transfer of prisoners on a transcontinental 747 commercial flight.
Other airlines at JFK may have holiday passengers waiting on stand-by,
but this jumbo jet takes off with a lot of empty seats.
Ryan and another prisoner overpower and kill their guards.
The few civilian passengers are herded into a "pantry"
on the plane and are not seen again until the end of the movie.
Ryan eliminates the other prisoners and all the crew members except Teri Halloran.
The remainder of the movie follows a "cat-and-mouse" game between Ryan and Teri on the 747.
