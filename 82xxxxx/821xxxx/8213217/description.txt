Budget On The Go

The perfect companion app of YNAB (You Need A Budget) for Desktop. All of our mobile apps support Cloud Sync for real-time, automatic and pain-free syncing of all of your devices wherever you have an internet connection. There's no more guessing when it comes to spending decisions since your budget is always up to date.

Our mobile apps make it so quick and easy to add a new transaction, your budget can know about your latest purchase before you leave the store! Multiple devices can be connected to the same budget making it easy for the entire household to stay in sync.
