“Sven-Ingvars in Frödingland” is the Swedish dance band Sven-Ingvars studio
album from 1971, in which all songs is music set to Gustaf Fröding poems.

Gustaf Fröding, (August 22, 1860 - February 8, 1911) was a Swedish poet and
writer, His poetry combines formal virtuosity with a sympathy for the ordinary,
the neglected and the down-trodden. It is highly musical and lends itself to
musical setting.  Gustaf wrote openly about his personal problems with alcohol
and women and had to face a trial for obscenity for that cause. 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gustaf_Fr%C3%B6ding
