For her ailing son to be healed, GRACE (Angle Locsin) needs to reconnect with EDWARD (Dingdong Dantes), the biological father.

This daunting process of saving her son's life slowly destroys their respective current relationships.

Edward's wife JACQ (Angelica Panganiban) is becoming paranoid of this returning old flame of her husband.

On the other hand, Grace's ever supportive boyfriend TRISTAN (Zanjoe Marudo) gradually doubts his significance in her life.

Helmed by the unclaimed director Ruel S. Bayani, One More Try tells of a very moving story that tests the measure of relationship and love for something & someone you hold dearly, how far are you willing to go and let go?
