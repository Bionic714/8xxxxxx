Information
------------------------------

Massive Engines
Chris Barrie hosts an enthralling look at some of the world's most incredible machines as he explores some of the world's biggest engines, including monster trucks, giant diggers, rockets, trains, supertankers and earthmovers. Marvel at these monstrous mechanical creations as Discovery Channel takes you through ten exciting episodes of engineering ingenuity. In Massive Engines, Chris tells us the story of the development of engine power across a range of ten different types of machine. Constantly on the move, he combines great historical anecdotes with hands-on demos and test drives to give fascinating insights into how these great machines, old and new accomplish extraordinary feats.
