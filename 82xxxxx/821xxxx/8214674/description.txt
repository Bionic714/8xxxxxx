The GE 44 for Train Simulator, developed by DigitalTrainModel, is available in Southern Pacific livery and features opening cab doors and windows, cab and dial lighting.

The locomotive is also Quick Drive compatible, giving you the freedom to drive the Southern Pacific GE 44 on any Quick Drive enabled route for Train Simulator. Also included are scenarios specifically for the Donner Pass route (available separately and required to play these scenarios).
