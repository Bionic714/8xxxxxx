One Piece 3D: [color=red]Straw Hat Chase[/color][/b] is the [b]11th[/b] One Piece film of the series, and is set before the timeskip. This is One Piece's first movie release to be in 3D CGI and second One Piece film since Movie 3 to be a double-feature. The film was featured with "Toriko 3D: Gourmet Adventure", both being billed as the "Jump Heroes Film". The double-feature was released on March 19, 2011.
