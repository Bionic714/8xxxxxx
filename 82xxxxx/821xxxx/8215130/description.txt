SpongeBob SquarePants: Employee of the Month is an adventure game based on the television show SpongeBob SquarePants. The game is broken down into four playable chapters as well as an intro and a finale.


Intro
SpongeBob wins 'Employee of the Month' award again, making him 'Employee of the Year'. As a prize, he receives two tickets to Neptune's Paradise, a local theme park which Mr. Krabs had won tickets free in the mail.

Chapter 1: Employee of the Year

Chapter 2: Hitting Rock Bottom

Chapter 3: Back to Square One

Chapter 4: Bottoms Up!

Finale
