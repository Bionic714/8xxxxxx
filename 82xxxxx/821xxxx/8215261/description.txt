Wiki Taxi

It is an application that helps you to access Offline Wikipedia Database.
The database should be downloaded by you
Follow these steps

1) Copy The files in any location with sufficient space for database(Mine database is around 14 GB)
2) Open the Wiki Taxi executable file
3) Download appropriate database as your need.
4) Open the database and enjoy

Very usefull for people who don't have internet connection all time and want to have informative archive and datdabase
