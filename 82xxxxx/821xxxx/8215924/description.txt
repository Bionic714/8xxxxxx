This is a complete discography for Bob Dylan...for the most part. Other than the unnecessary numerous compilations, 1 important live album is missing, 1984's "Real Live", because I didn't have it on hand, so sorry about that. Other than that 1 flaw, this is about as complete of a Bob Dylan discography as you can get, unless you also want the numerous bootlegs out there.

I had to put the "Studio Albums" folder in an .RAR file since I had way too many folders, so sorry about that.

Everything does indeed have a CBR of 320, enjoy!
