The Wedge is a weekly music program airing on Canadian music TV station MuchMusic.


Since 2011, the show is hosted by Damian Abraham, lead singer of the band Fucked Up.


The Wedge primarily focuses on music videos, but will periodically have live performances, interviews, and panel discussions.


The Wedge is by and large associated with alternative music, although it often hosts music from other genres as well.


February 27, 2013 episode.

There was not an episode of The Wedge on Feb. 20, 2013. It was pre-empted as MuchMusic was airing The 2013 Brit Awards on a constant loop all night.  The Wedge came back on Feb 27.
