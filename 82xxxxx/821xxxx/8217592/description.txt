Azad (Anil Kapoor), is a mob enforcer working for Don Lalla Jamal Khan (Anupam Kher). He rescues Meena (Meenakshi Sheshadri) from a brothel, thereby inviting the wrath of Bhau (Paresh Rawal), a rival Don. He vows to help Meena achieve her dream of becoming a singer and enlists music Director, Dhiren (Govinda) to help her. Things become complicated when both men fall for Meena. Meena falls in love with Dhiren and this causes Azad to loose his mind in jealousy. Meanwhile, Bhau's men violently pursue Meena and Azad and want them dead at any cost. Will Azad be able to free Meena from the danger? Can Dhiren and Meena be united?
