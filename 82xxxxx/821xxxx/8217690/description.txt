Storyline

Frank, Joe, Marvin and Victoria used to be the CIA's top agents, 
but the secrets they know just made them the Agency's top targets. 
Now framed for assassination, they must use all of their collective cunning, 
experience and teamwork to stay one step ahead of their deadly 
pursuers and stay alive. To stop the operation, the team embarks on an 
impossible, cross-country mission. But when you're considered "Retired, 
Extremely Dangerous," an impossible mission is just another day at the office. 
Based on the cult DC Comics graphic novels by Warren Ellis and Cully Hamner.
