Girls

Genre: Comedy, Family
Year: 2013
Country: USA
Runtime: 30 min
Producer: Lena Dunham
Cast: Christopher Abbott, Adam Driver, Lena Dunham, Spencer Ebba, Vonn Harris, Jemima Kirke

.: Plot :.
Created by and starring Lena Dunham ("Tiny Furniture"), the show is a comic look at the assorted humiliations and rare triumphs of a group of girls in their early 20s.
