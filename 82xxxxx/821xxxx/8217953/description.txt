Killing Lincoln
President Abraham Lincoln will go down in history as the man who ended slavery in America. But just 4 days after his victory Abraham Lincoln was dead, murdered by a rebel sympathizer.. Relive the agony and the ecstasy of a man who would die for his cause. Based on Bill O'Reilly's bestselling book, produced by Ridley Scott and presented and narrated by Tom Hanks, this is National Geographic Channel's first ever, dramatized-documentary.
