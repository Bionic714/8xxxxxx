O yeah - sure the Country and Western stars could rock!!! If the cowboys were a bit more reserved than the  real rockers, listening to this compilation will still get you warmed up.

The C&W music of the early 1950s deeply influenced Rock n Roll (and the Rockabilly scene as well). But from the mid-1950s C&W singers were admittedly kept moving more by the Rock and Rollers.

This 10 CD-Box gives for the first time a good overview of the Rock C&W scene. No fewer than 250 choice titles are sure to get those hips swinging. 
(membran-online.de)
