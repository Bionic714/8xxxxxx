These ten CDs offer 200 of the wildest Rock & Roll songs of the 1950s - and not a note of boredom among them. Loud and fast, was the motto of the performers on this box. Experience how you won't be able to tell the difference between the future Soulstar Otis Redding and Little Richard - and many more rarities guaranteed!
