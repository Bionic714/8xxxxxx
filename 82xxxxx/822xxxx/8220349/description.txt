Rules of Engagement


Genre: Comedy, Romance
Year: 2013
Country: USA
Runtime: 22 min
Producer: Tom Hertz
Cast: Megyn Price, Oliver Hudson, Bianca Kajlich, David Spade, Patrick Warburton

.: Plot :.
Rules of Engagement enters its fifth season this fall during the 2010-2011 season on CBS. The series is a comedy about different phases of the male/female relationship as seen through the eyes of a newly-engaged couple Adam (Oliver Hudson) and Jennifer (Bianca Kajlich), a long-married pair Jeff (Patrick Warburton) and Audrey (Megyn Price), and a single bachelor on the prowl, Russell (David Spade). However, Russell obtains a relationship of his own as he tries to make the professional relationship with his assistant Timmy (Adhir Kalyan) more personal.
