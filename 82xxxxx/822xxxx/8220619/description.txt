Description:
-------------
Forza Motorsport 4 - another of the main Xbox-race please us not only more extensive fleet of vehicles, but also a number of interesting innovations. For example, the game will support the controller Kinect - its camera can track the position of the head in space, and allow the player, for example, look to the right of the car. Forza Motorsport 4 is an extension of the legendary autosimulator for the Xbox 360, the development of which are carried out by Turn 10 Studios. The publisher of the game, as always, is a Microsoft Games Studios.

Forza Motorsport 4 gameplay is a lot like the gameplay of the previous parts. The game takes the form of one-person, high-quality chassis and detail of the world, realistic slip while riding a car.
It is possible to change the color of any car, and added more options to their beautiful design compared to Forza Motorsport 3. The game will be several modes: single on passing, ordinary pen, multiplayer online. Single mode will include more than 200 different events, including the race in a circle, oval, drift, drag, paddock for a while, and more.
Forza Motorsport 4 will have full support for Kinect, you can even say all the control of the game will be based on this technology. Gesture control will allow gamers to look around in the cockpit of a car while driving by simply turning the head!
As in the previous section, Forza Motorsport 4 will include a large amount of content XboxLive, and the options that will be available to players in the network. Gamers will be able to create new designs for cars, give or sell this "virtual goods" of the game to other players.
To all this, players will be able to transfer their profiles ForzaMotorsport 3 in the fourth. Depending on the level of the game, which they have in the third part, and bought a car, all of this may already be available in Forza Motorsport 4!

Note: You can play without Kinect.
