Footfall is a 1985 science fiction novel written by Larry Niven and Jerry Pournelle. 

It was nominated for both the Hugo and Locus Awards in 1986, and was a No. 1 New York Times Bestseller.

The book depicts the arrival of members of an alien species called the Fithp that have traveled to our solar system from Alpha Centauri.

The aliens are intent on taking over the Earth.
