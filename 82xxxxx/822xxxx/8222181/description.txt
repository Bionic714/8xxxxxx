Storyline

In a world in which Great Britain has become a fascist state, 
A masked vigilante known only as "V" conducts guerrilla warfare 
against the oppressive British government. When "V" rescues a 
young woman from the secret police, he finds in her an ally with 
whom he can continue his fight to free the people of Britain.
