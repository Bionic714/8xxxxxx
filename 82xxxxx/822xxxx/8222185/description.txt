Ep: Time and Punishment 

Air Date: Mar/03/2013

After months of keeping it under wraps, Ramona reveals her secret engagement to Karen, but is stunned by her response. With romance back in her life, Drita decides to celebrate by upgrading both her car and her breasts. When Karen gets propositioned by her smooth studio partner sparks start to fly. Meanwhile, runaway bride, Love, reconnects with the one who got away. While Ang tries to bring everyone together for a holiday party, Ramona invites some of the women to a fabulous diamond and fur party where she announces her news to the world-- only to be blindsided by the group's shocking reaction.
