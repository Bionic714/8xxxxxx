Mitt liv som hund AKA My Life as a Dog

In My Life as a Dog, the frequent collision of comedy and drama is breathtakingly real, and Anton Glanzelius is terrific as Ingemar, a smart 12 year old with a philosophical bent. He puts his own misfortunes and those of his terminally ill mother up against greater disasters, reminding us of the theory of relativity in a human dimension. As his mother deteriorates, he's sent to the country to be cared for by his uncle and aunt, where he meets all sorts of crazies and encounters adventures like most 12 year olds.
