----

They are provocative and angry.

They protest bare-breasted and go to war against male domination, dictators, prostitution and religions!

These warriors are the new face of feminism, their bodies have become their weapons. And they are ready to pay the price of this fight by prison or exile. From the flight of Inna Schevenko, 22, FEMEN leader, to that of Aliaa EL Mahdy, 21, threatened to death for talking against sexism in Egypt by posing nude on the Internet, this movie tells the rejoicing and sometimes violent story of a movement which has become planetary.
