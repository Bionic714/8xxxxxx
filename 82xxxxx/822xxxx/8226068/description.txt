Imagine Your Perfect Day! This is a story about Sandy, a happy go lucky girl who always has something good to say. As she comes across her neighbors and friends, some don't share her positive attitude. So with a little help from Sandy and their own imagination, we get a sexy insight on what some would consider their perfect day!
