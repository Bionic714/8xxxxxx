AU/UA
CONTEMPORARY POETRY OF UKRAINE AND AUSTRALIA
EDITED BY LES WICKS, YURY ZAVADSKY AND GRIGORY SEMENCHUK

The anthology includes poems of Australian and Ukrainian authors and their translations into Ukrainian and English correspondingly
