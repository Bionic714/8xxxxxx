Taboo: Exorcism's
Many cultures around the world believe evil can manifest in demonic spirits that enter a person's body. For some who believe, the only solution is an exorcism. In Phoenix, Arizona, a battle for the souls of those said to be possessed is being waged, not by priests, but by five teenage girl exorcists extract a demon from a man who believes his bloodline is cursed. One minister in Jacksonville, Florida is trying to battle as many demons as she can. Aka Devils and Demons
