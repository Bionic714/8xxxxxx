Water is critical to our survival. Nothing is more important than sourcing for water when one is in a disaster or survival situation. Donald Langley's work is set to be the definitive manual to creating your own water filtration system on the go!

Learn how to:

- Survive and 'create' drinkable water with nothing more then some simple tools around the house.

- How plastic bags can save your life and filter water for you in a survival situation!

- The 'power charcoal method'. Knowing how to utilize this method could mean the difference between life and death for preppers and survivors.
