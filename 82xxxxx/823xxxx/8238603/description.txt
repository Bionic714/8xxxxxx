 Philip Carter, IQ and Aptitude Tests
ISBN: 0749449314 | edition 2007 | PDF | 192 pages | 4 mb

IQ and Aptitude Tests contains 400 brand new practice questions together with answers, explanations, and a guide to assessing performance. The questions are organized into four IQ tests together with verbal, spatial, and numerical aptitude tests. These include tests of word meanings, grammar and comprehension, advanced verbal aptitude, logical analysis, mental arithmetic, numerical sequences, and number problems. Aptitude tests and IQ tests are increasingly encountered in recruitment, selection, and assessment procedures.
