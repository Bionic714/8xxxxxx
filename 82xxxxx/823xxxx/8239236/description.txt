This is an interesting documentary/reality show which the BBC produced.
They sent a guy into a few countries on Bush's "Axis of Evil" list to see how things really were inside each one. 

The series begins with North Korea, which is fascinating in and of itself, but the list also includes Iraq, Iran, Syria,Libya and Cuba. 

"The Bush regime claims that North Korea, Iraq, Iran, Syria, Libya and Cuba are part of an "axis of evil".
In a remarkable travelogue, reporter Ben Anderson, armed with a hidden camera and a tourist map, visits all six rogue states and tries to find the reality of life in some of the most repressive regimes in the world."
