Suave conman Ralph Gorse longs to permeate the upper crust of 1930s Society, and his stylish good looks and striking sexual magnetism soon help him gain entry into this privileged world. Once inside, Gorse uses his charms to seduce a series of wealthy women, stopping at nothing, not even murder, to further his ambitions and desires for wealth and power.
