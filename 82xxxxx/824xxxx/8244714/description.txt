This route expansion is around 45 miles long and includes the city centre, the scenic Severn Beach line with its four-mile tunnel under the River Severn and the Avon gorge. 21 highly detailed stations along the route are modelled.

The docks, coaling and shunting yards, freight areas and sidings of Bristol's unique rail network provide drivers with an enthralling combination of railway activities, and 15 enjoyable scenarios are included, ten using default stock, and five using stock from Just Trains, as well as four Free Roams.

This version includes the service pack released 31/01/2013
