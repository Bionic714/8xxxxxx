Lake is a German rock music band that formed in the early 1970s.
Lake Live ... On The Run is the first double live CD Lake recorded and released in 1982. Songs on CD 1 include: 1. On The Run 2. Red Lake 3. Hopeless Love 4. The Final Curtain 5. See Them Glow 6. Black Friday 7. Between The Lines 8. Jesus Came Down 9. Paradise Way
Songs on CD 2 include: 1. Rocky Mountain Way 2. Glad To Be Here 3. Scoobie Doobies 4. Celebrate 5. Living For Today 6. Jamaica High 7. Key To The Rhyme 8. Chasing Colours
Bit rate is 192 Kbps MP3 format.
