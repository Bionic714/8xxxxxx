A great collection of overtures to classic (and not so classic) Broadway shows, drawn from the original cast albums in most cases. The shows are:

1.  *Show Boat(Jerome Kern)
2.  *Of Thee I Sing (George Gershwin)
3.  *On Your Toes (Richard Rodgers)
4.  *Oklahoma! (Richard Rodgers)
5.  Finian's Rainbow (Burton Lane)
6.  South Pacific (Richard Rodgers)
7.  Miss Liberty (Irving Berlin)
8.  Fanny (Harold Rome)
9.  Candide (Leonard Bernstein)
10. Goldilock (Leroy Anderson)
11. Redhead (Albert Hague)
12. Gypsy (Jule Styne)
13. The Fantasticks (Harvey Schmidt)
14. Bye Bye Birdie (Charles Strouse)
15. Twentieth Century (Cy Coleman)
16. Merrily We Roll Along (Stephen Sondheim)

*Not Original Cast Recording
