Winamp is a multimedia player made by Nullsoft. It supports
numerous audio and video formats. It also plays streamed video and
audio content, both live and recorded, authored worldwide. It has
an extremely customizable media library, and allows you to rip and
burn your favorite music CDs. Winamp has thousands of skins and
plug-ins that allow you to change the look-and-feel of the player
as well as add new features. The player also has access to
thousands of free songs, videos and online radio stations from
SHOUTcast Radio and AOL Radio with XM
