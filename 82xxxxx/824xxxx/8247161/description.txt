Description:                               =
============================================

Experience the dazzling action of the hit sequel like never before in Luxor 2 HD, a beautifully enhanced version of the marble shooting game. Wield your winged scarab once again in this hit sequel where chains of colored spheres are working their way through ancient Egypt. It's up to you to stop them before they reach the pyramids as you work your way up from a simple farm hand to the Pharaoh of Egypt. Will you succeed?  Play 88 gorgeous levels of marble-shooting madness. Enjoy 25 stunning backgrounds with thrilling new special effects. Collect Ankh coins and gems to earn extra lives or points, surround yourself in amazing sounds, and enjoy two different aiming mechanics. With 13 power-ups including the new Net and Dagger, three stimulating play modes, and a spectacular HD presentation, you are certain to be awed.
