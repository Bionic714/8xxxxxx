ChallenBall is a new concept in puzzle games. Play in either Challenge Mode, with 48 levels, and discover the hidden and amazing game story or play without pressure in the Relaxed Mode, choose your own difficulty and have tons of fun. The story will bring you to a magic world where Leonardo Da Vinci and Zhang Heng will show you the game mysteries. A Secret Message is waiting for you only if you achieve the final level.
