Ozzy Osbourne - Speak of the Devil movie was released Jul 17, 2012 by the Eagle Rock Entertainment ltd. studio. This DVD was filmed at Irvine Meadows, California in 1982. Ozzy Osbourne - Speak of the Devil video The show was part of the delayed continuation of the "Diary of a Madman" tour following the tragic death of Randy Rhodes in March 1982 in a flying accident during the early part of the tour. Ozzy Osbourne - Speak of the Devil film The show features guitarist Brad Gillis who was brought in to replace Randy Rhodes. Ozzy Osbourne - Speak of the Devil review The concert is a perfect snapshot of Ozzy's live performances in the 80s with dramatic staging and masses of energy with a set list based around his first 2 solo albums and a few Black Sabbath classics. Ozzy Osbourne - Speak of the Devil DVD Ozzy has always been one of heavy metal's great frontmen and, despite the difficult circumstances, this show captures him at his absolute best.
