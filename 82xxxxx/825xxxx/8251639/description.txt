Help save these cute little pets from the cruel wizard in Webbies! These bunches of cuddly friends spend their days carelessly playing in the woodland. Unfortunately, Hurgen, the bitter wizard doesn't like their frolics and devises a plan with Alfred the spider to trap all the food in the forest within Alfred's webs, dooming our lovely creatures. Foil the wizard's plan by untangling all the nuts and fruits in the forest and feeding our cute little pets!
