Light the Fuse is a live album by The Rolling Stones, released in 2012. It was recorded at the Phoenix Concert Theatre in Toronto, Canada. The album was released exclusively as a digital download through Google Music on October 16, 2012. The concert was performed in front of an audience of only 1,000.
