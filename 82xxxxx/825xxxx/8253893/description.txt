A clique of teenage girls drift apart after the group's leader| Alison DiLaurentis| goes missing. Her body is found and the girls are reunited at her funeral.
All four begin receiving text messages from the mysterious 'A'| who seems to know many of the group's deepest secrets| some of which only the late
Alison had knowledge of. The girls join forces against A's scheming and try to uncover the mystery of Alison's death.
