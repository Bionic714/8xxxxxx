.: Plot :.
FX has ordered The Americans, a drama about two KGB spies posing as Americans in the early 1980s, the network announced Friday.

The series, executive produced by Justified's Graham Yost and created by Falling Skies' Joe Weisberg, centers on the two spies, whose arranged marriage evolves into something more real, compromising their mission during the Cold War.
