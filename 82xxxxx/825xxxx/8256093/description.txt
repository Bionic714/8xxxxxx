Penelope 1966 Film



Penelope is a 1966 comedy film directed by Arthur Hiller and starring Natalie Wood, Ian Bannen, Peter Falk, Jonathan Winters, and Dick Shawn. Wikipedia
Release date: November 10, 1966 (initial release)
Director: Arthur Hiller
Running time: 97 minutes
Music: John Williams
Producer: Arthur Loew, Jr.

Comedy about a flighty, bored, kleptomaniac wife of a banker who robs her husband's bank of $60,000.
