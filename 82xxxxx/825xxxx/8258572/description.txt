Beauty and the Beast

Genre: Drama, Fantasy
Year: 2013
Country: USA
Runtime: 42 min
Producer: Gary Fleder
Cast: Kristin Kreuk, Jay Ryan, Jason Deline, Alex Paxton-Beesley, Nicole Gale Anderson, Chris Anton, Peter Apostolopoulos, Austin Basis

.: Plot :.
Beauty and the Beast will be loosely based on the 1987 CBS series starring Ron Pearlman and Linda Hamilton.
Jennifer Levin and Sherri Cooper will write and executive-produce alongside Paul J. Witt and C. Anthony Thomas, Ron Koslow and Bill Haber. ABC is also developing a Beauty and the Beast project, planned as a more traditional take about Belle being imprisoned by the Beast in his enchanted castle.
