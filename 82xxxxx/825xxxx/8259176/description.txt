Patton tells the tale of General George S. Patton, famous tank commander of World War II. The film begins with Pattons career in North Africa and progresses through the invasion of Europe and the fall of the Third Reich. Side plots also speak of Pattons numerous faults such his temper and tendency toward insubordination, faults that would prevent him from becoming the lead American general in the Normandy Invasion as well as to his being relieved as Occupation Commander of Germany.
