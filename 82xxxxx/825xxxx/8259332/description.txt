Pencils, Giant Bolts, Pickles and Inflatables

Technology Documentary hosted by Zac Fine, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Factory Made series in 2010 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Find out how lead gets inside a pencil, why a blazing inferno is needed to make a bolt, what kind of cucumber is needed to pickle into a pickle, and what keeps a 15-foot inflatable from falling down flat?
