Nick Robinson - The Origami Bible
North Light Books | 2004 | ISBN: 1581805179 | English | 149 pages | PDF | 175.92 MB

In this guide, author Nick Robinson focuses on mastery of the intricate technique of origami, easily showing readers how designs are created, the creative process involved, and how existing works can be adapted into new designs.
Readers will learn the spectrum of skills needed for the art form, from choosing the right paper to building 12-fold designs or 24-fold models. With step-by-step instructions, along with a helpful flip-out back flap that provides a key to symbols used in the book, The Origami Bible is packed with projects, including CD cases and hats, elephants and tadpoles. It is the essential resource for all origami enthusiasts, whether complete beginners or intermediate and advanced artists.
