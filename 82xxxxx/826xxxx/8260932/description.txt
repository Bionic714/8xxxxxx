"Bruane Brenn" (translating to English as "the bridges are burning") is a song by Norwegian heavy metal band Kvelertak. It is a track of the band's second album Meir. The single was premiered on January 8 on BBC Radio 1's "Rock Show" with Daniel P. Carter and was released on iTunes and Spotify in Scandinavia on January 21 and elsewhere on January 28. The artwork was created by Seldon Hunt.
