  Contemporary Nutrition Seventh Edition is designed for students with       
  little or no background in college-level biology, chemistry or             
  physiology. It will provide students who lack a strong science             
  background the ideal balance of reliable nutrition information and         
  practical consumer-oriented knowledge. With their friendly writing         
  style, the authors act as the student's personal guide to dispelling       
  common misconceptions and to gaining a solid foundation for making         
  informed nutrition choices.
