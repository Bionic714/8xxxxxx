=-- STORYLINE --=

Recruited to assist Montreal police in their 
desperate search for a serial killer who assumes 
the identities of his victims, FBI profiler Illeana 
Scott knows it's only a matter of time before the 
killer strikes again. Her most promising lead is a 
museum employee who might be the killer's only 
eyewitness.[/code]
