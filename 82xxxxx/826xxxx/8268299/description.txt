World's Most Dangerous Roads Series3

Travel Documentary hosted by Adrian Dunbar, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Celebrities tackle some of the world's most infamous roads.


1)  Madagascar 
Angus Deayton and Mariella Frostrup take on Madagascar's roads at the end of rainy season.

2)  Georgia 
Hugh Bonneville and Jessica Hynes take a road trip through Georgia.

3)  Bolivia 
Phill Jupitus and Marcus Brigstocke head to Bolivia for a high-altitude road trip.
