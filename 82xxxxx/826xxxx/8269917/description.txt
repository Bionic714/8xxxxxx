Produced by George Martin is a feature length
profile of Sir George Martin, Britain's most
celebrated record producer.
The films talk about his childhood,

working on orchestral music,

Together George Martin and The Beatles revolutionised
pop music and recording techniques forging probably
the greatest producer / artist collaboration ever.
