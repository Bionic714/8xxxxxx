The Legion of Super-Heroes are gearing up to celebrate the anniversary of the founding of their team. Meanwhile, Brainiac 5 is called in by Rond Vidar, a head of the Time Institute as well as an honorary Legionnaire, to assist in helping someone from an alternate universe that seems to be Ferro Lad. Ferro Lad is having visions from several alternate realities that are placing his mind in turmoil. Brainiac 5 tries his best to help cure him of his predicament while Shadow Lass and Mon-El find themselves fighting against a Khund war ship and are able to defeat it. Eventually, Brainiac 5 learns that it's not exactly Ferro Lad but his twin brother and he finds a cure for his malady. Once this occurs, Ferro Lad fades away, perhaps to his rightful time and space.
