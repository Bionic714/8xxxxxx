Smash

Genre: Comedy, Drama
Year: 2013
Country: USA
Runtime: 42 min
Producer: Theresa Rebeck
Cast: Debra Messing, Jack Davenport, Christian Borle, Megan Hilty, Katharine McPhee, Raza Jaffrey, Brian d'Arcy James

.: Plot :.
Follows a group of people, including Debra Messing as a put-upon lyricist and Katharine McPhee as a spirited ingenue, as they come together to put on a Broadway show about Marilyn Monroe.
