Joan of Arc (1999) (Eng)(NLsub)

Nederlands:
Het relaas van een eenvoudige boerendochter en de zoektocht die haar
veranderde in één van de meest geruchtmakende personen aller tijden.
Ze hoorde stemmen en ging erop uit om Frankrijk te herenigen.

English:
Ten years before her death, Joan hears voices. Six years later, from the
village of Domremy, she begins her mission to unite France under King Charles.
First she leads a defense of Vaucouleurs against the Burgundians, then obtains
safe passage to Charles, the Dauphin. He uses her, as the embodiment of the
mythical "Maid of Lorraine," to raise an army, and he sends her to the rescue of
Orléans. After Charles is crowned, Joan leads a disastrous campaign in Paris,
where her brother dies. Then she's the victim of Charles's manipulations: she's
captured in Burgundy, sold to the English, examined by Bishop Cauchon, found a
heretic by the Inquisition, and burned at the stake.


Met o.a.: Leelee Sobieski, Chad Willett en Peter O'Toole
