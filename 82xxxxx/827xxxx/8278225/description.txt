Top Of The Lake is a drama series created by Jane Campion and Gerard Lee. The series is set in New Zealand and is a powerful and haunting story. A twelve-year-old girl who is five months pregnant is found standing chest deep in a frozen lake and refuses to say who the baby's father is. After she suddenly disappears Robin Griffin finds herself being tested to the limits as she tries to investigate the case.
