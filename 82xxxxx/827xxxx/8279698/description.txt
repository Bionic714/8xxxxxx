Disc Cover is slick and intuitive software to create CD and DVD labels, covers, tray inserts, folding booklets, slim cases, super jewel boxes as well as mini-CD labels, business card CDs, VHS wraps and more.

Disc Cover video tutorials.

Over 250 template sets and 1,000+ clipart images (40,000+ with Extras Pack) provide a great help for a quick start. Import your data and images from iTunes, Aperture, iPhoto, Finder, iDVD and many other sources.

Print disc labels, covers, tray inserts and more on paper from the most popular manufacturers: Avery, Neato, Memorex and others. Or, print your designs directly on the discs using HP, Epson, Canon and other printers. With Lightscribe, Labelflash and DiscT@2 support you can laser-etch the design on a disc.
