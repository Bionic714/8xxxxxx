No Chicken Soup Today! v1.0.0
Requirements: 2.2
Overview: Control Mother Hen in this quest to escape her fate: become chicken soup. 


In this 2D platform game use a combination of force and angle to overcome the obstacles that you will find on 30 levels of pure fun.

* Lots of ice cubes, chilli sacs and big knives.
* Find the chick at each level.
* Escape from the kitchen before time runs out.

Can you save Mother Hen and her little chicks? Remember there will be "No Chicken left behind"
