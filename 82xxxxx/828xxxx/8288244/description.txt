World in Conflict (also known as WiC or WIC) - a computer game in the genre of real-time strategy, Developed by Massive Entertainment and published by Sierra Entertainment for Windows. The game was released in September 2007. [1] March 10, 2009 the creators of games released complement World in Conflict: Soviet Assault, where the player has been given a long-awaited chance to play the single-player campaign for the USSR.

The game is set in 1989 in an alternate reality. The economic difficulties of the Soviet Union, began to threaten its integrity, resulting in the Soviet Union went to war against a number of capitalist countries. Initiate World War III. [2]
