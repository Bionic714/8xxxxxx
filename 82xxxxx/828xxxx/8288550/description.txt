Review:

 Etage Noir label-boss. Austrian musician Parov Stelar is back as part of a duo and has a new sound to boot.
 Maybe it was the influence of his recent albums that featured remixes of his work, but there's definitely a bigger electronic element than before.
 Highlights include the Herb Alpert goes to Ibiza-isms of "La Calatrava", the bonkers Daft Punk-goes electro-swing title track, the Dave Brubeck vibes of "Menage A Trois" and the glamorous bluesey-in-places, "At The Flamingo Bar".
