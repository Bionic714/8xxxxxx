To help boost her newspaper's circulation, slinky journalist Danielle (Lauren Hays) finds her next story in the form of seductive male gigolo Gage (Mark Ritter). Fascinated by his web of anonymous sexual encounters, Danielle's obsessive jealously ultimately threatens to blow her cover.
