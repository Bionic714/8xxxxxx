Japanese Homestyle Dishes: Quick and Delicious Favorites -epub

Japanese Homestyle Cooking will enable you to prepare simple, authentic dishes to enjoy within the comfort of your own home. Learn the secrets behind preparing exquisite sushi and sashimi with an inexpensive bamboo sushi mat and a little practice and patience.
 The recipes are presented in a simple format that begins with a list of essential ingredients and proportions, a simple step-by-step guide to preparation, cooking and timing, and include both the Japanese and English name for the dish.
 With its full-color photographs throughout and layflat concealed spiral binding, this Periplus cookbook is attractive and easy-to-use.
