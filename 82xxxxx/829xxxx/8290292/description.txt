American rockabilly and country singer and guitarist. He is best known for the rockabilly songs he recorded in the 1950s for the Sun Record Company, such as Ubangi Stomp, Rock n Roll Ruby and Uranium Rock.

In 1980, while preparing for another European tour, Smith died of a heart attack at 47 years of age.
