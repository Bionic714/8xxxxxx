Navigon Iphone & Ipad Europe v.2.4 Updated: Mar 22, 2013


Your Iphone/Ipad Must Be JailBreaked To Use This Build 


Requirements: Compatible with iPhone, iPod touch, and iPad. Requires iOS 5.0 or later. This app is optimized for iPhone 5.


With NAVIGON you can turn your iPhone or iPad into a fully functional mobile navigation system. Thanks to a faithful adaptation of the menu functions, the app fits in seamlessly with the iOS operating concept and can be installed on any iPhone 3GS, iPhone 4, iPhone 4S, iPhone 5 or iPad device (the navigation function is only available on the iPad WiFi +3G)



Languages: English, Arabic, Bokmål, Norwegian, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Dutch, Finnish, French, German, Greek, Hungarian, Italian, Polish, Portuguese, Romanian, Russian, Slovak, Spanish, Swedish, Turkish


What's New in Version 2.4

Voice instructions can be broadcasted through the car's speakers. This function requires the iPhone to be connected with the car's bluetooth handsfree kit.
Wikipedia POIs can be downloaded for free and transform the app into a travel guide.
Glympse functionality has been further improved.
Additional Foursquare functionality.
Easy switch between car- and pedestrian navigation.
