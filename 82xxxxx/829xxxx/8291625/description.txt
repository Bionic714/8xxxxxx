Story about 3 friends, 2 guys and 1 girl, who live at the island Ibiza. Each of them choose a different way in life: first as free person who lives life by the day, second as a more planned spiritual one and the last one doesn't know what to do, until finding a new life in Africa and helping the poor people there. As the seasons turn and Ibiza changes 3 characters keep coming back to each other, until the moment that some things happens and only one of them survives.
