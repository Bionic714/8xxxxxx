Ayalum Njanum Thammil (Malayalam: α┤àα┤»α┤╛α┤│α╡üα┤é α┤₧α┤╛α┤¿α╡üα┤é α┤ñα┤«α╡ìα┤«α┤┐α╡╜, Between him and me) is a 2012 Malayalam romantic thriller film directed by Lal Jose, produced by Prem Prakash and distributed by Century Films. Written by brothers Bobby and Sanjay, the film stars Prithviraj, Pratap Pothen, Narain, Samvrutha Sunil, Rima Kallingal and Remya Nambeesan in the lead roles.[2][3][4][5]

Ayalum Njanum Thammil is a candid and honest story of a committed senior doctor and an irresponsible junior doctor and through their relationship, a picture of the medical profession is drawn vividly. The soundtrack and background score are composed by Ouseppachan while Vayalar Sarath Chandra Varma pens the lyrics for the songs. The film released on 19 October 2012 to widespread critical acclaim. The film went on to be a blockbuster in the box office. It won four Kerala State Film Awards, including the awards for Best Popular Film, Best Director and Best Actor (Prithviraj).
