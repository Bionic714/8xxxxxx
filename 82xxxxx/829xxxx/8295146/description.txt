This Hit for Haiti match was a charity fundraiser held at the Tennis Gardens in 2010. The money raised would go directly to International Red Cross as part of the relief efforts in the aftermath of the Haiti Earthquake of 2010. $1 mn was finally raised.

Participating in the match was Roger Federer, Pete Sampras, Andre Agassi and Rafael Nadal. The match was exciting and intense at times. You get to see a bit of aggressiveness on the part of Andre Agassi as he had bit of a fight with Pete Sampras.
