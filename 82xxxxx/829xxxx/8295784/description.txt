The package contains:

BR Standard 5MT
BR 6MT Clan
BR 7MT Britannia

All loco's TS2013 spec
Quick Drive enabled
Weather FX
Driver selectable lamps

NOTE: for the loco's to appear in Quick Drive, the Mk 1 Coaches need to be installed. The Mk1 Coaches are available separately as part of the IHH Bonus Content 2 BR Carriage Stock package.
