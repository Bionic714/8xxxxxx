"MTV Unplugged" presents former James Gang member Joe Walsh accompanied by R&B great Dr. John. Recorded at the National Video Center in New York City, Walsh performs such hits as "Desperado" and "Rosewood Bitters." All involved come together for renditions of "Cinnamon Girl" and "Let The Good Times Roll." Recorded 12/14/89 at National Video Center, New York City Set List: 01. Desperado [Walsh] 02. Rosewood Bitters [Walsh and Rosas] 03. Repetition, Repetition, Repetition [Shear] 04. Cinnamon Girl [all] 05. Life's Been Good [Walsh/Dr. John/Rosas] 06. Let the Good Times Roll [all]
