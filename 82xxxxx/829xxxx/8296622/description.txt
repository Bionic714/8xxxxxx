Don't Raise the Bridge, Lower the River is a British-made comedy film produced by 

Walter Shenson starring Jerry Lewis and was released on 12 July 1968 by Columbia 

Pictures. It was based on Max Wilk's novel of the same name with the original 

Connecticut locale moved to Swinging London and Portugal.

 
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Don%27t_Raise_the_Bridge,_Lower_the_River


This video was recorded from USA television station WTIC-TV from Hartford 

Connecticut on their digital sub-channel 31.2 where a Standard Definition digital 

television network service called "Antenna TV" is broadcast. You will notice the 

WTIC-TV FOXCT logo at the beginning of this video and an occasional FOXCT logo 

displayed during the film.


There are no commercials included in this video.
