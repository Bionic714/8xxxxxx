My Friend Irma is a comedy film directed by George Marshall and is most notable as 

the film debut of comedy team Dean Martin and Jerry Lewis. The film was released 

on August 16, 1949 by Paramount and is based upon the CBS radio series My Friend 

Irma that first aired in 1947.


This motion picture is in black and white.
