Hard Drive: 3 GB
DirectX Version: 9




Hawken is a FPS based online multiplayer game modes in which you command strands: the famous giant fighting robots that are very popular in Japan and familiar to any fan of the famous title Heavy Gear - launched in the 90s.

This is the first game in Adhesive Games, a small independent studio based in the United States, which did not spare investments to produce a game with beautiful graphics and gameplay worthy of main category titles.

The game consists of a mixture of action - at very fast pace - and strategy. It was designed to keep players quite busy, running around the battlefield with specific goals, and detonate any enemy who dares cross his path.
