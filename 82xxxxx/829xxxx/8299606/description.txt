On the most evil night of the year, a beautiful blond woman and ruggedly 
handsome archeologist hitman find themselves trapped in a forest haunted by 
bizarre flesh eating Native American demon zombies. As they flee the woman's 
crime boss husband, who seeks to murder them for their illicit love affair, 
their car is destroyed in a head-on collision with one of the demons, a Stone 
Man. The blond woman and assassin must escape the perils of the dark and evil 
forest, on foot, without becoming demon food or getting themselves slaughtered 
by the woman's vengeful, fat cat husband. 

This is one of those films that belongs in the "so bad it's good" categories.  I 
laughed like hell during the film, and it it had had any kind of budget, we 
might have been looking at another Evil Dead or Feast hit.  As it stands, the 
one liners, corny character acting, and overall goofiness of the film make it 
worth a view.
