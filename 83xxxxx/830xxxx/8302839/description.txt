Fathom Vol. 2: Into the Depths

English | CBZ | 13 Issues

Genre: Fantasy, Science Fiction

Written by Written by J.T. Krul and Michael Turner
Art by Koi Turnbull

Worlds collide when the might of the bloodthirsty Admiral Maylander and the United States military declare war on the Blue civilizations nestled deep under the ocean's surface. Following the classic events of the original Fathom series, the second thrilling volume finds underwater biologist Aspen Matthews struggling to accept the startling reality of her true identity, all in the midst of an all-out war between the humans and the Blue! Collecting for the first time the sold out Fathom: Beginnings and Zero issue, as well as issues #1-#11, Fathom Volume 2 is a must for any Aspen, Fathom, Michael Turner, or comic book fan!
