Aqua Fish is a shining 3D arcade game based on classical Pac Man game. You must collect all treasures placed in 6 different underwater worlds. But be carefull, you are not alone, there are some fish that will try to stop you at all cost. You can beat them using some special bonuses like, Freeze, Haste, Invulnerable or Extra life.
