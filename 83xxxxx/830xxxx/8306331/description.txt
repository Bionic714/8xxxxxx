The Queen and I is a 2008 documentary film about Farah Pahlavi, the former Queen/Empress of Iran. The film was produced and directed by Iranian-Swedish filmmaker Nahid Persson Sarvestani. It was shown at Sundance Film Festival in 2009, where it was nominated for the Grand Jury Prize.

The film follows the former Queen/Empress and the director, a former communist, as they share ideas and concerns about the country they were both forced to leave after the revolution.
