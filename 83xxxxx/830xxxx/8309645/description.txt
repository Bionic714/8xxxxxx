The Day the Universe Changed (subtitled "A Personal View by James Burke") is a British documentary television series written and presented by science historian James Burke, originally broadcast in 1985 by the BBC.

Again, these versions are NOT the Science Channel versions. They are the original ones, as first aired in 1985.

The quality is improved over the set I posted before (8240369) ...

Why? some MVGroup people uploaded yet another version of the originals in mkv format. I figured XviD/AVI is more useful so I converted them. The mkv files had bitrates varying from under 1.5k to over 3k. I randomly chose 2k as a decent bitrate for these files.
