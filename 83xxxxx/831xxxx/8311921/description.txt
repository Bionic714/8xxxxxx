 Kodaka Hasegawa, a high-school student, returns to his hometown after several years away. He attempts to blend in with his class but his blond hair and glaring eyes make him seem like a hoodlum to his classmates, leaving him friendless. One day, he witnesses his classmate Yozora talking to herself and, after some embarrassment, the two form the Neighbor's Club in hopes of making friends and leading fulfilling lives. However this unfortunate duo only attracts equally unfortunate members leading to even more unfortunate results.
