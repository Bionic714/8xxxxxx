On September 11, 2001, Osama Bin Laden shocked the world with the attacks on the Twin Towers and the Pentagon, the United States, giving voice to radicalism and intolerance. This documentary chronicles the life of one of the most mysterious and controversial men of the XXI century, trying to answer questions such as: what took the rich boy, heir to a fortune millionaire at 10 years of age, considered to be the biggest terrorist of all time?
