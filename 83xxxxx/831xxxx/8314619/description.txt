Sameer Malkan's entertaining and moving drama focuses on Ajay (Saif Ali Khan) and Dev (Ashkay Kumar), a pair of two-bit con men whose endless moneymaking schemes involve everything except actual work. When an attempt to steal a young man's wallet ends up getting him killed, the two friends' guilty consciences lead them to his village and family, where their attempts to help out and redeem themselves result in a growing web of lies. As they become part of the family, and Ajay falls for a beautiful local girl (Sonali Bendre), both must decide whether or not to reveal the awful truth.
