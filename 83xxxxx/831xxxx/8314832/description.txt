Live at the Cardiff Capitol Theater is a live concert recording of Lynyrd Skynyrd during a European tour in support of their third album, Nuthin' Fancy. It was released by Geffen Records. This concert took place at The Cardiff Capitol Theater in Wales on November 4, 1975.
