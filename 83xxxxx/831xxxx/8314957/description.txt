Moonquake - Moonquake (1973)

Canadian trio from Montreal, formed as backing band for Michel Pagliaro in the early 1970s before striking out on their own. They disbanded in 1976 after having released two albums. This is there first and the best one.

Members:
- Jack 'Geisinger' Aegust (bass, lead vocals)
- Hovaness Hagopian (guitar, vocals)
- Derek Kendrick (drums, percussion, vocals)
