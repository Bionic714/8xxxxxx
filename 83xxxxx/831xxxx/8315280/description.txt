:~Storyline~:

Captured international assassins are locked up inside 
a high-tech bunker known as the Killing Chamber. To break 
out of this concrete hell they must duel each other, fight 
deadly ninjas and battle against gangs of masked maniacs. 
And... if they survive this, they will have to confront 
Snakehead: the lethal, deranged top dog who will stop at 
nothing to kill 'em all!
