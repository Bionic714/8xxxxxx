Hotels are a place of romance and mystery--no one knows what goes on behind closed doors. No one, that is, except for Tony, a long-time waiter at the exclusive Hotel Valentine. Tony has seen his share of wild guests, like hot swinger couple Nikita Denise and Randy Spears, and lovestruck coworkers, like gorgeous Elexis Monroe. But nothing prepares Tony for his encounter with the ethereal, mysterious Stephanie Swift, who steals away from her "real life", only to steal Tony's heart. Will the star-crossed lovers confess their secret desires? Or will Stephanie return to jilted lover Rocco Reed? An old fashioned tale of love at first site, starring Stephanie Swift, Tony De Sergio, Elexis Monroe and Rocco Reed.
