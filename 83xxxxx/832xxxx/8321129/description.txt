Lou Cifer & the Hellions were born in 1995 when members of different German Rockin' groups (namely Lou Cifer, Bill Zebop and Dave Hill from Mess Of Booze and Hal Hellion from the Ton-Up Rockers) came together to realize their own ideas of Rock'n'Roll, so forming a new studio band. The end of the year saw the release of "ArresTed", their 4-track 7" EP on Fifty-Seven Records with only selfpenned songs.
