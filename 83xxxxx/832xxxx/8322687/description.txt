On This Perfect Day is the debut album by Arjen Anthony Lucassen's side project Guilt Machine, released in August 2009.

From Wikipedia:

The music deals with "the destructive psychology of guilt, regret and the darkest form of secret -- the secrets we hide from ourselves.", and the music ranges "from dark and heavy to atmospheric and melancholic."

Track list:

1. Twisted Coil
2. Leland Street
3. Green and Cream
4. Season of Denial
5. Over
6. Perfection
