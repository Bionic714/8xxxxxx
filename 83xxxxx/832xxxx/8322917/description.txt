Pappa Ante Portas is a 1991 German comedy film


Heinrich Lohse is a manager who increasingly tends to loose control. After ordering typewriting paper and erasers enough for 40 years to come, his boss forces him to retire. Confronted with this new situation, Lohse's family reacts in shock. It turns out Heinrich's wife Renate and his son Dieter were quite comfortable with the absent husband and father and don't want to see their situation disrupted.
