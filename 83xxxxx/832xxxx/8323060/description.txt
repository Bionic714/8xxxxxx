Inspector Rex S2 Ep16 - Deathly Secrets

Deathly Secrets - The body of a young woman medical student is found in a forest just outside Vienna. She has been killed by an arrow. During the investigation, Brandtner and Rex discover that the medical student had knowledge about a doctor's negligence and was threatening to go public with the information. Stars Gedeon Burkhard, Heinz Weixelbraun and Martin Weinek. (From Austria, in German) (Rpt) PG
