Based on the true story of 'Operation Bernhard', which was the secret Nasi plan to
destabilise the British economy by flooding Britain counterfeit sterling notes.
In the tradition of 'Inglourious Basterds', this is the story of five allied soldiers
who against all the odds go undercover behind enemy lines in an attempt to capture
the forged millions for themselves.
