Documentary which takes a look at the career of the American pop rock band Evanescence. Founded by singer Amy Lee in Little Rock, Arkansas, Evanescence have enjoyed a number of high-charting singles and albums following their breakthrough hit 'Bring Me to Life'. This documentary makes use of archive footage and interviews with the band members to bring their story to life.

 02. Formative Days
 03. A Very Close Pair
