The Class 280-157 Consolidation for Train Simulator, developed by Smokebox, is available in Alabama, Tennessee and Northern Railroad livery and features detailed controls and gauges, opening cab doors, windows, ventilator hatch and storm window, detailed internal and external animations and expert driving mode for sophisticated locomotive controls.

The locomotive is also Quick Drive compatible, giving you the freedom to drive the Class 280-157 on any Quick Drive enabled route for Train Simulator. Also included are scenarios specifically for the Horseshoe Curve.
