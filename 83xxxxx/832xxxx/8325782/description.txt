Nowhere Left To Run is a 2010 short film starring British pop rock band McFly, which was shot over three days.[1] The film was first screened through the group's official website on 13 October 2010. The film features the band playing versions of themselves, with a vampire-themed twist. The film features seven songs from their upcoming album, Above the Noise. The film was released on DVD on 29 November 2010 in the UK.
