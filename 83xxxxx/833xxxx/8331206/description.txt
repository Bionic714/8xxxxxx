Inspector Rex S2 Ep17 - Poison Gas

Poison Gas - Jewel thieves arm themselves with nerve gas which they intend to use during the course of a multi-million dollar robbery in which many people could die. However, before the robbery takes place a tramp comes into contact with some of the gas, collapses and dies. It is now a race against time, and Alex and Rex go undercover in an effort to find out more. Stars Gedeon Burkhard, Heinz Weixelbraun and Martin Weinek. (From Austria, in German) (Drama Series)
