Zion and Bryce Canyon National Parks

Travel Documentary hosted by David Michael Gee, published by Questar in 2009 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

1)  Zion and Bryce Canyon National Parks 
By foot, shuttle, and horseback, this program gives you access to the scenic and geological wonders of Zion and Bryce Canyon National Parks. In Zion, embark on a breathtaking ascent of Angel s Landing. In Bryce Canyon, climb sheer cliff walls, tube down the Virgin River, and gaze upon thousands of beautiful and bizarre rock spires known as hoodoos.


2)  Secrets of the Southwest 
In this land made famous by Georgia O Keeffe, you will discover the secrets of the Southwest along famous Route 66 and the Grand Circle, an area that boasts the highest concentration of national parks in the country, including the grandest canyon of them all. Discover how Monument Valley in Utah and Gallup in New Mexico became cinematic locations, jet-ski in the brilliant blue waters of Lake Powell, and explore the spirit of the Navajo in Canyon de Chelly.
