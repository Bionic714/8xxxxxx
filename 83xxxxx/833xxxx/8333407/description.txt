Wallace Stevens - The Collected Poems (Alfred A. Knopf, 1971).

ISBN: 9780679726692 | 534 pages | PDF

This definitive poetry collection was originally published in 1954 to honor Stevens on his 75th birthday and won the Pulitzer Prize for Poetry in 1955. The volume contains:

* "Harmonium" (1923)
* "Ideas of Order" (1936)
* "The Man With the Blue Guitar" (1937)
* "Parts of a World" (1942)
* "Transport to Summer" (1947)
* "The Auroras of Autumn" (1950)
* "The Rock"
