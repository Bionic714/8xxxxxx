Now in their 30's, best friends Leanne, Stacy and Carmen find themselves single and somewhat emotionally lost. After trying to heal through traditional methods, the trio decides a road trip is in order. Comedy - and Hot Sex - ensues as each tries drastically different approaches to get back in the saddle!
