Welcome to the Punch
2013 Film
Welcome to the Punch is a British thriller released on 15 March 2013 by Momentum Pictures in the UK and Ireland. 
Initial release: March 15, 2013
Director: Eran Creevy
Screenplay: Eran Creevy
Producers: Ben Pugh, Brian Kavanaugh Jones, Rory Aitken
Genres: Adventure film, Action film, Crime Fiction, Thriller
