The model:

- fully accurate and highly detailed model and cab
- realistic and working gauges
- high quality sounds
- accurate performance and speeds
- full operational dynamic brakes
- Cablight
- illuminated numberplates
- windows can be opened and closed
- individual wiper control. All wipers may be activated seperately
- two versions of the former Lehigh Valley locomotives 7640 and 7642
 

Scenarios:

Four scenarios for the Horseshoe Curve are included.
