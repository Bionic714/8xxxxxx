This is the Train Simulator 2013 version

This classic steam collection, licensed by the National Railway Museum,  brings you five LMS Blue Streamlined Princess Coronation Class locomotives with appropriate tenders as well as a set of Just Trains LMS Blue Coronation Scot Mark 1 carriages.

The high quality models feature accurate physics, smoke and steam effects, and realistic sounds. The package also includes three scenarios for the Newcastle to York route.
