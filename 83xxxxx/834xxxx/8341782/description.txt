Dutch with hard-coded English subs!

The bizarre story takes place in Amsterdam-West, where a virus turns people into 
bloodthirsty zombies. Although much blood is flowing and many limbs chopped off, 
there is a lot to laugh at in this bizarre horror comedy. 

An excellent Dutch entry into the horror genre.  Has been posted here before but 
without subtitles, leaving non-Dutch speakers perhaps not understanding what was 
going on.  This is an excellent indie film.  Think Dawn of the Dead + Evil Dead 
slapstick.  Well worth the time invested watching it!  So crack open a can of 
Heineken, Orangeboom or Konink (maybe with a Ketel Een chaser) and enjoy Dutch 
horror at its finest!

Enjoy!
