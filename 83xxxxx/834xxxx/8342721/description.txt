Four adventurous teen girls hit the road on a wild ride of fun, self-discovery and sexual liberation in Sweetheart's sixth installment of Lesbian Hitchhiker. When the van breaks down, the girls go their separate ways to look for help, encountering an array of gorgeous older lesbians who will help the girls in more ways than one. Will they reunite and explore their feminine desires with each other or get lost in a haze of womanly lust?
