The Legion of Super-Heroes learn that Shrinking Violet has actually been a Durlan actress named Yera, though she was innocent of any wrongdoing. The Legion try to find the real Shrinking Violet, who is trapped on Imsk by some rebellions who do not want the presence of the United Planets on their planet. They kidnapped Shrinking Violet to steal all her secrets of the Legion of Super-Heroes so they could have an upper-hand in their inevitable battle. The Legion defeats the rebel Imskians and save Shrinking Violet's life. Colossal Boy, having been in a relationship with Yera instead of Shrinking Violet all this time, proposes to her and she accepts.
