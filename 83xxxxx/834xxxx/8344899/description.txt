Super Mario Red Scare started in mid-September using portion of Supernova Mario World Engine. The game is for one of MFGG's minigame competition #11 entry.

Story:
Bowser is getting tired of the old ways. Now, he started to use his new communist army to take over the whole Mushroom Kingdom and share the wealth with his allies. Now it's Mario to fight back the aggressive Koopas once and for all.
