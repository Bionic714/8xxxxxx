This is (for the most part) a complete discography of the British Heavy Metal band, Iron Maiden.

Other than including their 15 Studio Albums, I've also included all of their Official Live Albums, Extended Plays and Compilations. Also, as a bonus, I've thrown in 4 bootlegs I happen to have, most are pretty easy to find though. Lastly, I've included Vinyl Rips of the albums "Iron Maiden", "The Number Of The Beast", "Powerslave" (my all-time favorite Iron Maiden album), and "Live After Death", all in a 24-96 FLAC format. Enjoy!
