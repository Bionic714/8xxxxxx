A long lost piece of electric jazz-funk from Harold Vick under the alias "Sir Edward." Released by Bernard "Pretty" Purdie's defunct-yet-irresistibly-funky Encounter Records label. Originally Released in 1973. Highly recommended and dangerously groovy!
