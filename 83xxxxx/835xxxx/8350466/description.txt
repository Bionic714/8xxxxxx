March is the debut album of singer-songwriter Michael Penn, released in 1989.
It featured the singles "No Myth", "This and That", and "Brave New World". In 1990, "No Myth" peaked at #22 on the Billboard Hot Adult Contemporary Tracks chart, #5 on the Mainstream Rock Tracks chart, #4 on the Modern Rock Tracks chart, and #13 on the Billboard Hot 100. "This and That" reached #10 on the Modern Rock Tracks chart. "Brave New World" reached #20 on the Modern Rock Tracks chart and #26 on the Mainstream Rock Tracks chart.
