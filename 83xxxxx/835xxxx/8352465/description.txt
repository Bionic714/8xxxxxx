Beautiful Jesse Jane is the elusive agent working for the most covert assassin agency in the world. A mysterious woman determined to engage in bad behavior for all the right reasons, she is convinced that her brutal missions are helping those in danger. Once partnered up with the seemingly innocent BiBi Jones, business turns unusually dangerous, leaving them to fear how they'll make it out alive.
