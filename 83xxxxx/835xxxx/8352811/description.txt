A glorious event brings powerful warriors to Tirrus Dow castle for a chance at the grand tournament purse. As the festivities unfold, an unwelcome predator casts its shadow over the realm. Sir Tristen Dow's newly knighted brother is kidnapped during the chaos. Desperately searching for his missing brother, Tristen is ensnared by unfortunate battles and apparent wizardry. A mysterious duo has been seen wandering the land, leaving dead knights in their wake. With the help of devoted friends, can the knight find his brother and restore peace before evil completely consumes the land?
