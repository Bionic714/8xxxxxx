GAME OF THRONES Season3 Episode 2

Episode 2 Title: Dark Wings, Dark Words  Directed by: Daniel Minahan  Written by: Vanessa Taylor  Original air date: April 7, 2013 

The third season of the epic fantasy drama television series Game of Thrones premiered in the United States and Canada on March 31, 2013 on HBO.  HBO renewed the series for a third season on April 10, 2012, nine days after the second season's premiere. Production began in July 2012.
.
Like the other seasons, the third season will consist of ten episodes. It is based roughly on the first half of A Storm of Swords, the third of the A Song of Ice and Fire novels by George R. R. Martin, of which the series is an adaptation
.
Sansa says too much; Shae asks Tyrion for a favor; Jaime finds a way to pass the time; Arya encounters the Brotherhood Without Banners.
