Bellydance Bootcamp, Basic Technique Warm-Up Vol. 1 
Fahtiem
 
Join multi-award winning, world renowned performer and instructor Fahtiem in an innovative and exciting presentation of "Fahtiem's Basic Technique Warm-Up." This aerobic warm-up will teach you body movement discipline layered over a consistent weight change technique. Learn the fundamentals of mind-body awareness through belly dance movements in a fun and easy to follow format.
