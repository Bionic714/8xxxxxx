Release Date: 14 March 2013

Language: Malay

Genre: Comedy / Romance

Running Time: 1 Hour 34 Minutes

Director: Rashid Sibir

Cast: Scha Al-Yahya, Elly Mazlein, Rusdi Ramli, Jehan Miskin, Ruminah Sidek

The best cabin crew award is back this year. Twelve cabin crew were shortlisted to compete with each other in order to get the pride, including Dania and Tania. Tania was once received the award. She is still popular among the crew and passengers. Dania knows Tania has been using Remy, a pilot to attract everyone's attention. Hence, Dania decides to reveal the truth and the 'war' between two girls begins...
