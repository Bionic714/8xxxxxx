Story 1: As The Sky Burns

The Prophet tries to coerce the Legion of Super-Heroes to join him in his endeavor in fighting a threat called Omen. The Legion of Super-Heroes refuse him because of the damage he has caused on his travels and so they fight, with the Prophet tipping the scales in power. The battle rages on while back in the Legion Headquarters, Brainiac 5 is trying to find a cure for Danielle Foccart, the Invisible Kid's sister, for her malady. The Legion are able to finally defeat Prophet when Invisible Kid tells Shadow Lass to use her darkest shadow on his eyes. As soon as they defeat Prophet, Omen arrives.

Story 2: Monarchs Of All They Survey

Queen Projectra and her husband, Karate Kid, try to enjoy their honeymoon.
