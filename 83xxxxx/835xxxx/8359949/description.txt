Superhero Network Entertainment Group Presents:


PARAGON 1B (THE NARRATIVE VERSION)


"Benjamin Byrne is a teenage boy who exists in an overly idyllic world. After years 

of depression over the loss of his Father, Ben attempts to commit suicide. He 

unknowingly falls through a doorway into our world. Lost and alone, Ben sleeps in a 

homeless shelter earning money ingesting experimental medicinces. When the side 

effects kick in, Ben's system restarts imbuing him with super powers and special 

abilities. With the assistance of Latisha, a volunteer at the shelter, Ben embarks on 

a journey to uncover why this world cannot become the unspoiled world that he is 

from."
