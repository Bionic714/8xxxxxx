Bloc Party - Discography from 2004 to 2012
------------------------------------------


Contains studio albums, EP's, singles, remix albums, b-sides and live material

- Studio Albums (320 kbps)
- EP's (256 to 320 kbps)
- Singles (192 to 256 kbps)
- Remix Albums (160 to 320 kbps)
- B-Sides (128 to 320 kbps)
- Live & Acoustic (under 256 kbps)


Notes/disclaimer:

- Bitrate for studio albums is guaranteed 320 kbps; everything else varies
- Album art and meta-data are correct, but there are certain songs that lack meta-data. This was as a result of transferring from iTunes music folder.
- The remix album 'Intimacy Remixed' is missing
