After the Science Police receive a blackmail message that they will kill the President of Earth if they don't receive a certain amount of credits, they call in the Legion of Super-Heroes to help. While one group of Legionnaires act as the President's bodyguards, some Legionnaires are working undercover as Science Police officers because they believe that someone in the SP may be the actual culprit. They eventually learn who the person is and are able to defeat him.
