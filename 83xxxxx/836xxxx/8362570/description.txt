Trainer Jeremy Cioara takes you through everything you need to know for the ICND1 v2 exam, 100-101. You'll learn about network components, configuring and managing Cisco devices, troubleshooting, security, and much more. This series follows the exam objectives and includes topics Cisco added to the exam in 2013, including IPv6 and updated Cisco IOS.

Passing the ICND1 exam earns you CCENT certification and puts CCNA Routing and Switching well within your reach. ICND1 is now the only prerequisite for CCNA Security, Voice, or Wireless, and CCDA. Get started today!
