The Soup Book By Sophie Grigson
2009 | 352 Pages | ISBN: 1405347856 | PDF | 36 MB



This title features delicious soup recipes for the whole family, with editor-in-chief Sophie Grigson. Discover over 150 tried-and-tested soup recipes organized by ingredient. From pasta and noodles to meat and winter vegetables, turn surplus food in your fridge into mouth-watering meals. Handy tips on preparation and cooking times, freezing advice, nutrition information and 'cheap eat' symbols will help you choose the best soup to whip up. Step-by-step instructions make cooking simple, guiding you through all the essential techniques from how to puree to making stock. It comes in a portable compact format, and is perfect for taking with you to the farmer's market.
