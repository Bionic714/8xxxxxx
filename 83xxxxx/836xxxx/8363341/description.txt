Awaken

Genre: Drama, Romance, Sci-Fi
Year: 2012
Country: USA
Director: Daric Loo

.: Plot :.
Alexs dreary life is reignited when he meets Rachel, the woman of his dreams. The only thing that stands in the way of their perfect, serendipitous relationship is that Rachel has recently passed away.
