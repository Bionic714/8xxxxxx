About band:
-----------
Get Thy Rock! (G.T.R!) band was founded in Kharkiv, Ukraine in mid-2012 and quickly took its place on the Kharkiv music venues. By taking the classic Hard Rock and bringing new vision to it, the band was able to create some extraordinary tunes with a powerful sound within the genre.

In april 2013 Get Thy Rock! finished recording debut EP "My Choice", which includes 4 songs and became band first step in creating their own style.
