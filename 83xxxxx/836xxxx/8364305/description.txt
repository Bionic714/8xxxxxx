Métal Hurlant Chronicles is an English-language Franco-Belgian television science fiction anthology series based on the popular comics anthology magazine Métal Hurlant, known in the United States as Heavy Metal. 

All the episodes are a self-contained story taking place on a different planet with a different cast, linked together only by the idea that a meteorite, the "Métal Hurlant", passes the planet in question during the events of the story. 

The series premiered on French television on October 27, 2012 on France 4. Broadcast rights for various European countries such as Germany, Austria, Luxembourg were bought by Sony Pictures Television.
