Secret Burger Media presents 
The Current
								
A film of a 10,000 foot descent across hundreds of miles of snow, sand, 
mountains, and desert.  The story of our connection to water and the Southwest's 
growing desire to stop the current.

Produced and Directed by
Greg Cairns
Stephen Witherspoon
