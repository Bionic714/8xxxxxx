Belly Dance Explosion - the fire within that will never extinguish! Amira and the Jewels teach and demonstrate step by step belly dance choreography. The music and dance is a master piece of creativity and brilliant imagination. It is designed for the absolute advanced dancer, but can be a great challenge, motivation and inspiration for the intermediate dancer who aims high. It is an intense energy, fusion, powerful and empowering dance that will ignite the fire that will never be extinguished. Amira Mor is an internationally renowned belly dancer, choreographer, producer, and certified fitness instructor as well as the director of the Jewels of the Desert Dance Company.
