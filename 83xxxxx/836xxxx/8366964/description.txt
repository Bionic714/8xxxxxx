Leo Wright - 1962 - Suddenly The Blues (2012)

I'm not really a fan of flute or guitar in jazz but this one is a highly enjoyable record. Leo Wright certainly deserves to be better known among the 60's musicians.

Track list:
01. A Felicidad
02. Greensleeves
03. Gensel's Message
04. The Wiggler
05. Tali
06. Dionysos
07. Sassy Lady
08. Willow Weep For Me
09. Suddenly The Blues

Personnel:
Leo Wright - flute, saxophones
Kenny Burrell - guitar
Ron Carter - bass
Rudy Collins - drums

Enjoy and share!
