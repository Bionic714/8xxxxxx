Englisch
One of the US Air Force's most modern tactical aircrafts, a F-100 with a new laser guidance
system, crashes into the sea near Malta - a region where the Soviet forces are highly
present, too. The CIA immediately sends out their best secret agent Ken Tami to salvage the
system before it falls into enemy hands. To ensure his loyalty, they bring his two young sons
to a nearby hotel on the island. Ken Tami's tough opponent is KGB agent Andrei.
