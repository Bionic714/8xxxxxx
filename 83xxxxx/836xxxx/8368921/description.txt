Manual Step:

1.) Start game, finish Tutorial.

2.) Buy or Build whatever you want your money and Donuts will go down, but you can still buy without a problem.

3.) If you buy expensive Items you will get Minus Donuts (Example: i have 5 donuts, i buy a house for 600 donuts.. now i have -595 Donuts, but purchase is successful)
