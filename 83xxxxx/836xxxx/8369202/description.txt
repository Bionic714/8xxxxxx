Tiger Lillies - 2000 - Bouquet Of Vegetables

Bouquet of Vegetables - The Early Years is the final missing piece of the Tiger Lillies cannon, our first recordings from 1989/91 which were only released on the cassettes, 'Bouquet of Vegetables' and 'Little Death'. Now back in the land of the living on CD we are proud to present some of those early songs such as 'Palace of St James', 'Wrong Attitude' and 'Marseille'. (tigerlillies.com)
