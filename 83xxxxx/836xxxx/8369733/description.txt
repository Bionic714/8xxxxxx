
Warpath is a first-person shooter (Death match/Capture The Flag) video game developed by Digital Extremes,who previously developed Pariah. The game was originally being developed as a sequel to Pariah, but since Pariah was a commercial flop, Warpath continued development as a whole new game. 


Released: July 18, 2006 

Genre:    First-person shooter

Modes:    Single-player, Multiplayer 

(up to 32 players; bots included for offline play)
