The Surprise of Haruhi Suzumiya (English) (PDF)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This torrent contains both parts of The Surprise of Haruhi Suzumiya by Nagaru
Tanigawa. In addition, it also contains The Untold Story of Haruhi Suzumiya, a
booklet contained with the limited editions of both parts. All three were trans-
lated into English by ultimatemegax, et al (ultimatemegax.wordpress.com).

Because this story actually forms a trilogy with The Dissociation of Haruhi
Suzumiya, that book has also been included for completeness. It was translated
by the Baka-Tsuki Project (baka-tsuki.org/project).

I will not be posting the other books in the series. If you would like to read
them, I would suggest purchasing the English versions from Yen Press
(yenpress.com/haruhi-suzumiya-novel), or Kadokawa Shoten if you clicked on the
English torrent by accident or something. Or if you won't buy them, I suggest
this torrent: thepiratebay.se/torrent/5698043.

The official English release of Dissociation will be June 18, 2013, and for
Surprise, November 19, 2013. If you like these books, please consider buying
them when they come out. Or if not, SEED OR DIE.
