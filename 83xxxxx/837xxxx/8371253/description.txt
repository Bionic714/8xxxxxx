Dark Extra Extar - Star Wars stories
Publisher:  Dark Horse
Publication Date:  March - June 2000, May - July 2001, and February - May 2002

Dark Horse Extra was a series of monthly four-page comic anthologies released by Dark Horse Comics. Each issue contained four single-page stories relating to ongoing Dark Horse titles. The stories would usually be told over the course of several issues. There were several Star Wars stories in it before the Extras were ended with issue 50 in 2002.
