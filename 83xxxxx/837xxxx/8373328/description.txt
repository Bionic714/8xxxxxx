SYNOPSIS:- Zameen Ke Tare (1960)
Tingu and Mangu are friends. While Tingu is the son of a sweeper, Mangu belongs to a rich family and stays with his father and step-mother. Tingu's father is a drunkard who beats Tingu and his mother, when drunk. One day Tingu's mother dies. Being a child, everyone tells him that his mother has gone to the House of God. As he cannot live without his mother he decides to go to the House of God and bring her back. Mangu also accompanies him as he also wants to meet his biological mother.

Both of them leave their house quietly and begin their journey to the House of God. On their way, they keep on asking for the address of the House of God. But, no one gives them a satisfactory answer. During their unique journey, they ecnounter a dacoit, a truck driver, a juggler and many other interesting people. In an interesting way, they reach the highest temple of God which is on the Himalayas. Whom did they meet there? Were they able to meet and get their mothers back? All this is and more is revealed in the touching climax of this movie.
