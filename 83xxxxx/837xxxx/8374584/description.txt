Seven Deadly Sins is a book about how Corey Taylor of Slipknot and Stone Sour quit his use of drugs and explains how his life before the death of Paul Gray was all Drugs, Sex and Drinking. This book is basically the biography of Corey Todd Taylor. This book has more than just learning about how Corey Taylor turned his life around after the death of Paul Gray but it also helps all the Slipknot/Stone Sour fans who are entering the world of Golden Gods.
