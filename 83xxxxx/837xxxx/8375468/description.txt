Fly Nation is a segment oriented show, co-hosted by Naoto & Yoshi Aoki, Nick Pujic, April Vokey, Paul Castellano, Josh Nugent and Thibaut Millet.  Within each episode, tag along with the co-hosts to various fly fishing destinations around the globe including all corners of Canada, a few visits to the US, Argentina & Iceland. Fly Nation features fly fishing adventure at dream destinations & at everyday urban locations.  Join the pursuit of just about everything with fins, ranging from trout & salmon to muskie and gar.
