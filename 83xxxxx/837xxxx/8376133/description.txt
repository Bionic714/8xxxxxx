It Takes a Man and a Woman is a 2013 Filipino romantic comedy film and the third and final installment in A Very Special Love film series. Cathy Garcia-Molina return as the director of the film and Carmi Raymundo as the head scriptwriter of the movie. Sarah Geronimo and John Lloyd Cruz also reprise their roles as Laida Magtalas and Miggy Montenegro respectively

Synopsis

The films follows the life of Miggy (John Lloyd Cruz) and Laida (Sarah Geronimo) after their break-up which occured in the second film. Miggy, is now in a relationship with Belle (Isabelle Daza) while Laida is now a fiercer woman after living in the United States. They try to co-exist in the same company while Laida tries to oppose Miggy's business decision.
