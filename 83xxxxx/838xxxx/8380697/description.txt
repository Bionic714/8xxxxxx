The band was formed in the spring of 2008. In the beginning it was a studio project which included only two members - Sigmund (vocals, guitars, bass) and Cosmic Drone (keyboards). The band was called Odium. It recorded the demo "Decay" which included three songs and recieved many positive opinions. In autumn of 2008 the line-up was formed and the band changed its name to 
Inner Missing.
