This is my own rip.

Although there is another OK upload of this movie, it has artifacts during many scene changes (conversation between the mom and the babysitter starting at 5:15, which you can compare to the Sample.mkv), and a glitch where 11 frames are dropped at 48:05, and the filesize is much larger than it needs to be.

In addition, IMDB lists the runtime of this movie at 1h 42min, and the DVD runs 1h 41min, while the HDTV showing runs 1h 44min and has pure film cadence.

The DVD is 30fps, and is pure video with no film cadence.  If you alter the HDTV video to 25fps, the DVD audio fits exactly (once the starting offset is correct).

So, I don't know what frame rate this movie should be shown at, but it is smoother in some scenes at 25fps.  So, that's the version here,  but there is also a 24fps audio and batch file (rename it from Make24fpsVersion.txt to run) to re-mux the movie to 24fps if you want that.
