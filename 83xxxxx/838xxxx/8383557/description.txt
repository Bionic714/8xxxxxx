Paramore is the self-titled fourth studio album by the American rock band Paramore. It was released on April 5, 2013 through Fueled by Ramen.[8][9][10] It is their first album without co-founders Josh and Zac Farro.[11][12] It is the band's most critically acclaimed album to date, and has been praised for its diversity in music by reviewers.
