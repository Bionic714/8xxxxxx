Live at Hammersmith '82! is a Duran Duran limited edition DVD/CD 2-disc live album, released by EMI on 7 September 2009.



The show was recorded during The Rio Tour at the Hammersmith Odeon in London, UK on 16 November 1982. This was the third of three shows performed at the Odeon on the tour.

The concert was originally recorded by the BBC TV for transmission in the UK, which features on various audio albums including Planet Heart and the King Biscuit Flower Hour show.

The live cover version of Steve Harley's "Make Me Smile (Come Up and See Me) " is also available on the compilation albums Girls On Film "The Collection" and The Essential Collection. The track was also released as the B-side to "The Reflex" single in 1984.
