A naturally talented dancer, Eva has been extensively trained in classical music and Hungarian folk and Gipsy dances. She brings a passionate and impeccable interpretation of Middle Eastern music to her bellydance performing and teaching. Inspirational teacher, well established in South West London, she teaches all levels from beginners to professionals and performs at a variety of venues including festivals, theatres and corporate events.
