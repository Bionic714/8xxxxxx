 M4A Information
 ===============
 -Bitrate: 192kbps VB
 -Codec: AAC
 -M4A is better than MP3. It offers higher compression, making the files
  smaller; and the compression is lossless, meaning no information has been
  removed in order to make the files small. Also, M4A is as widely supported as
  MP3. The AAC codec was designed as a replacment for MP3.
