Israel: Facing the Future

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by John Ware, published by BBC in 2013 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

John Ware journeys to Israel for a fresh look at how it has responded to the changes sweeping the region in the wake of the Arab Spring. He meets Israelis from all walks of life to go beyond the news cliches and analyse what is next for the world's only Jewish state as both the religious and the secular battle over its future.
