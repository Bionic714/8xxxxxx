Carry out the rescue mission in the new version of well-known strategy series Rescue Team 3. A devastating tornado damaged greatly wonderful Greenfield Island. Fortunately a ship has arrived and brought the rescue team. You are the leader of the team and you will be in charge of rebuilding the Greenfield Island after the horrible disaster. In this new version of the famous series you will face with more challenges and the innovative graphics will make rebuilding of the beautiful city even more exciting.
