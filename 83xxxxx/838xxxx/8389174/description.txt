1001 Erotic Nights 2: The Forbidden Tales is a follow up to the successful 1001 Erotic Nights released in 1982. This time out, though the formula is the same (period costumed performers relay and act out some sexy stories in front of the camera), we've got Francois Papillion playing the horny sultan who wants to find the right woman. His pal, played by the legendary Jamie Gillis, decides for him that he should hold a contest to eliminate the most eligible woman by putting them to the ultimate sex tests.
