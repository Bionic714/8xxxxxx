Swamp Thing, a fictional character, is a humanoid/plant creature elemental in the DC Comics Universe, created by writer Len Wein and artist Berni Wrightson.

This is the fifth Swamp Thing series and the first not under the Vertigo imprint since the second series switched over at Vertigo's founding in 1993. A New 52 series written By: Scott Snyder (#1 - 18); Jeff Lemire (#12) Charles Soule (#19)
