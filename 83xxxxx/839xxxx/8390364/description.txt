"Awanai Tsumori no, Genki de ne" will be the 18th single from SCANDAL. It is also used as a theme song for the upcoming film, "Ore wa Mada Honki Dashitenai Dake."


Artist: SCANDAL
Song: Awanai Tsumori no, Genki de ne (I Don't Intend to See You Again, Take Care)
Genre: Rock
Release date: May 22, 2013
