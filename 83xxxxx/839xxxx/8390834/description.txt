2013 release from the Bakersfield, CA-based Metal band. Topple The Giants is their first studio album since 2007. Topple The Giants is the band's first release in six years, after the release of Kill the Headlights and is the first with guitarist Tim Fluckey on lead vocals and new guitarist Marc DeLeon. The EP includes new music, along with older tracks re-recorded with the new line-up.
