Description: A beautifully photographed and well written film based on the 1832 novel of the same name by famed French author Stendhal. Directed with great care by Armando Crispino who is best know for his horror film AUTOPSY. Even though this drama seeks to attain high-brow status, Crispino can not resist tossing in exploitative elements such as copious amounts of nudity and scenes of brutally torture. Also stars Pier Paolo Cappoi, Evelyn Stewart, Antonio Cantafora, Patrizia Valturri, and Mara Venier. (1974-Itlay) In Italian, with English subtitles
