The Need for Speed

Technology Documentary hosted by William Hootkins, published by Discovery Channel broadcasted as part of DC Extreme Machines series in 2000 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

From the British speed heroes of the 1930s to the first man to break the sound barrier; man is constantly obsessed with pushing the boundaries of speed, often at the ultimate cost - their own lives. This is a celebration of our adrenaline-fuelled passion to go faster than ever before. So sit back, buckle up and prepare to be overwhelmed by the need for speed.



6)  The Ultimate Speed Machines 
"Ultimate Speed Machines" looks at the most advanced extreme machines on the planet, beginning with arguably the most sophisticated of them all, the human being. Watch track champion and 200m world record holder Michael Johnson in action and take a look at a new British bike made of carbon fibre that promises to smash all speed records. This exciting programme also looks at the fastest downhill skier, free faller, fastest boats, trains, cars, motorcycles and aircraft, making it a must for all speed lovers!
