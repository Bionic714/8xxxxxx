"Yung Rapunxel" (stylized as #YUNGRAPUNXEL) is a song by American rapper Azealia Banks, taken as the lead single from her upcoming first studio album Broke with Expensive Taste (2013). It was originally set to be released on March 26, 2013. However, on March 28, 2013, it was announced that the single would drop on April 16. The first single from the album was originally planned to be "Miss Amor/Miss Camaraderie". Nevertheless, on January 28, Banks acknowledged that the first single was going to be "Yung Rapunxel". The original release date, which was February 12, was delayed to March, and then again, delayed to April 16, 2013. The song interpolates vocals from the 2001 hit No More Drama by R&B singer Mary J. Blige.
