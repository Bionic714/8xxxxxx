This is an archive of Pali and English Theravada Buddhist texts in HTML format that may be viewed alone or with an Android app such as:
 
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.yuttadhammo.buddhisttexts

Put the BuddhistTexts folder on your SD Card and it should work with the above app.

Texts included:

Digha Nikaya
Majjhima Nikaya
Samyutta Nikaya
Anguttara Nikaya
Dhammapada
Jataka
Visuddhimagga
