This is part of my Gods Collection, basically a set of files that are from superstars of the Trance, Techno and generally Rave scene. The names that really get people to fork out for the tickets. Putting together collections of old or rare sets from around the world. While one of two of these are already on the site, I really think putting them together by artist like this will make for some torrents that hang around for a long time.

Okay, most folks probably haven't even heard of these guys. And to that, I say shame. Everyone that listens to techno, trance, pretty much anything electronic these days should know the foundations that it was built on - and it was sets like these. This part of the pack contains thirty hours of the oldest sets that I could muster up. Fantazia, Amnesia House, AWOL, Helter Skelter, Elevation Together and some amazing sets from Future Myth.

Do yourself a favour, download it, listen. Consider it like flipping through your best friends baby photos!.. Some of the sound quality isn't amazing, but a lot of these have been dubbed off cassettes - and screw you if you don't even know what that is!
