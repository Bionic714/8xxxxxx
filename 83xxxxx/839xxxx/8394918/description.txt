This is part of my Gods Collection, basically a set of files that are from superstars of the Trance, Techno and generally Rave scene. The names that really get people to fork out for the tickets. Putting together collections of old or rare sets from around the world. While one of two of these are already on the site, I really think putting them together by artist like this will make for some torrents that hang around for a long time. The bitrate shown in the header is probably an average, these are from many sources over many years.

Tiesto, surely the name itself resonates with awe. Tiesto never ever fails to deliver an exceptional set - even when he is late (as I experienced once at a rave) the organizers shuffled the lineup around, and when Tiesto finally came on a few sets later, he made a quick apology and then got on with the mixing. He is that well known he became the first DJ to perform at an Olympics - the opening ceremony of Athens no less! Half of Gouryella along with Ferry Corsten, the man behind the Magik and In Search of Sunrise CDs, he is certainly bringing trance to the mainstream. His remix of Delerium's Silence stayed on the UK top 10 for four weeks. One last note before the tracklist - Flight 643. Either you know it or you don't, which means either enjoy it, or find out what it is and then enjoy it. Below, just shy of 70 hours of the finest.

I am missing a set from 1999 which I REALLY want. It is from Amsterdam and goes for 12 hours (no, you read right). If anyone has it, please for the love of all that is good, PM me!
