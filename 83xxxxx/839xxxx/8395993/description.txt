--------------------------------------------------------------
FIREFLIGHT DISCOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------

This discography may or may not be complete.  It contains the following 8 releases...

 * 2002 - Glam-rok
 * 2004 - On The Subject Of Moving Forward EP
 * 2006 - The Healing Of Harms
 * 2008 - Live At Flevo
 * 2008 - Unbreakable
 * 2009 - Unbroken And Unplugged
 * 2010 - For Those Who Wait
 * 2011 - Stay Close - Single
 * 2012 - Now

Fireflight is an American Christian rock band formed in Eustis, Florida in 1999. The band is signed by Flicker Records. They have played in Shoutfest, Revelation Generation, and have been featured on The Scars Remain tour with Disciple, Family Force 5, and Decyfer Down.
