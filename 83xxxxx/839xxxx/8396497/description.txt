Henri Pachard churns out a grimy little grinder with a lot of impact that was overlooked by many critics. Ginger Lynn is working as a receptionist in a whorehouse fronting as a massage parlor when she is busted in a police raid. She is hauled off to prison where she must deal with a horny warden (Thomas), a toughie hellcat-type (Nichols) and a backstabbing cellmate (Raven). The film is light at times and dangerously serious at other times. Raven is especially wicked in her role while Ginger plays the naive newbie perfectly.
