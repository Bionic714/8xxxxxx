AKVIS Plugins Collection 

Software for processing graphics and digital images - retouching, painting, restoration. Retoucher-program for photo restoration and photo retouching. The program will help remove scratches, spots, dust, stains and other defects to remove unnecessary details, the text, to reconstruct missing parts of pictures, delay holey space and increase the ragged edge.

- AKVIS ArtSuite
- AKVIS ArtWork 
- AKVIS Chameleon
- AKVIS Coloriage
- AKVIS Decorator
- AKVIS Enhancer
- AKVIS LightShop
- AKVIS Magnifier
- AKVIS MultiBrush
- AKVIS NatureArt
- AKVIS Noise Buster
- AKVIS Refocus
- AKVIS Retoucher
- AKVIS Sketch
- AKVIS SmartMask

The program is available in two versions: as an independent program (standalone)  and as a plug-in to a photo editor.

Don't integrated with CS6, run Start Menu/Programs/Akvis
