[[INCLUDES]]


-- Project 64 1.6

-- "The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time" & "The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask"            

   ROMs

-- Glide64 Graphics Plugin (Pre-Installed)

-- HD re-textures for both ROMs (Pre-Installed)


[[GRAPHICS SETTINGS]]


-- Open Project64

-- Goto "Options" on the menu bar and choose "Configure Graphics Plugin..."

-- A window titled "Glide64 Settings" should pop-up, If not, goto "Options" -- 

   "Settings..." -- and where it says "Video (graphics plugin):" click the drop

   down menu below it and choose "Glide64 'Final' Date: Dec 29 2011" then click the 

   OK button.

-- Go to the "Texture enhancement" tab in the "Glide64 Settings" window and make  

   sure the "Use alpha channel fully" box , both "Compress texture cache" boxes and 

   the "Save texture cache to hard disk" box are checked, and that all other boxes 

   in that tab are unchecked. Also, make sure that the "Format" drop down menu is 

   set to "Rice Format"
