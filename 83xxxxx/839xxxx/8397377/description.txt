A Story Of Jesus, Told And Sung By Johnny Cash

The Gospel Road is a double album and the fourth gospel album and 44th overall album by American country singer Johnny Cash, released on Columbia Records in 1973 (see 1973 in music). It is the soundtrack to the film of the same name released by Twentieth Century Fox. The movie and the soundtrack tell the story of the life of Jesus. It peaked at No. 12 on the country album charts; the single released from it was "Children"/"Last Supper" (Columbia SP 4-45786, March, 1973). The movie was released on March 31, 1973.
