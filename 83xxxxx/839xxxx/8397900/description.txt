'Jeremy Brood'

Fantagor, 1989 (this version), 65 pages

Written and Illustrated by Jan Strnad and Richard Corben

Top fantasy writer Jan Strnad, has woven a classic science fiction tale of a futuristic, dystopian world, which is familiar and yet disturbingly altered. It is brought to life by the astonishing skills of Rich Corben, illustrated in his renowned hyper realistic airbrush style.
