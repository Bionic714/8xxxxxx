'Anomaly 3'

Another financially disastrous endeavor from the Sprawling Anomaly Publishing Empire, 1971, 57 pages

Written and Illustrated by Richard V. Corben, Kenneth Smith, Jan S. Strnad, Herb Arnold, Bob Kline, R.G. Krenkel

Content: The Castle of the King, An Allegory; Profile - Robert Kline; R.G. Krenkel, A Portfolio; Folio - Herb Arnold; Bog; Untitled; Survivors of the Suicide World, Episode Three: A Brief Encounter At War


'Anomaly 4'

Bud Plant, 1972, 36 pages

Written and Illustrated by Richard V. Corben, Robert L. Kline, Jan S. Stran

Content: Alice In Wonderlust, Leander and the Fat Queen, Encounter At War
