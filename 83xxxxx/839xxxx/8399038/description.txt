Zeca Afonso

José Manuel Cerqueira Afonso dos Santos,was an icon among Portuguese left-wing activism due to the role of his music in the resistance against the Portuguese dictatorial regime.


He is considered in many Post Graduate Thesis as one of the best songwriters of the XX century.
