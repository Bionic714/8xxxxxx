This is nearly the entire collection of West End Games (which went bankrupt) for Star wars Role Playing game. (often called "D6" as oppose to Wizard of the Coasts "D20") Core books, Galaxy guides, source books, adventures, miniatures.  Including some odd, homebrews books I found.

WEG40148 X-Wing Rogue Squadron Sourcebook, was never printed and the author's only copy was found to be on a corrupted CD.  Sadly this has been lost and will likely never be found
