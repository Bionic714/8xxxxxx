The True Story: Series 1
The True Story is a fascinating six-part documentary series revealing the real-life events that inspired some of Hollywood's most famous blockbusters. Uncover the actual stories and characters behind classic films including




6)  Casino 
Behind Martin Scorsese's masterful direction and Robert De Niro's larger-than-life performance lie's the very real story of the high-stakes battle between the mob and the law for control of Las Vegas. We interview key figures on both sides of this colourful crime story and detail how the FBI was finally able to overcome the Mafia's formidable grip on Sin City.
