The 6th of the 7 "Road" movies, "Bali" was the only film in the series to be filmed in color. It's one of the better films in the series but, as with all of the "Road" pictures, younger viewers won't get many of the topical gags or recognize some of the name actors in bit roles. Virtually all the "Road" pictures were sendups of various film genres, and this one takes on South Seas adventure-romances. Hope handles the jokes (and asides to the audience) whilst Bing croons a less-than-memorable collection of tunes by Burke and Van Husen. Lamour is gussied up in some colorful costumes in what was essentially her last "Road" movie.
