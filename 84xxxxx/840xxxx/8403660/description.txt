Ecouter is the debut -crowdsourced- self titled album by this new and energetic ensemble. Playing various instruments and always looking for new fresh ways to express music, the 3 core members of Ecouter: Nikola Ragusa, Amelie Brodeur and Nathalie Spehar are excited to have you listen to this album. Music includes music from Tango Master Astor Piazzolla including his famous, "Adios Nonino". Plus fresh new music by American Composer Marc Mellits, Merge Left! 

Album Release March 2013
