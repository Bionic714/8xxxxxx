Aradia of Las Vegas brings her 29 years of Middle Eastern dance experience to this video for Intermediate to Advanced dancers. She provides sizzling combinations to use for Egyptian, Lebanese and Turkish Oriental Dance. Including: An Introduction about the style and it's defining element. Combinations facing the camera. Combinations with her back to the camera so you can follow along as you would in a dance class. Performance of each style. Total running time is 1 hour 20 minutes. Filmed in High Definition, packed full of information and technique, with an easy to follow format. A must have for all Belly Dance enthusiasts!!
