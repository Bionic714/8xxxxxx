The movie is a combination of 4 independent loops: "Das vogelnde Klassenzimmer" (scenes 1-2), "Hurra, die Mose brennt" (scenes 3-6), "Resi's Fick-Theater" (scenes 7-17) and "Die Pinsel von der Liebesinsel" (scenes 18-22). The segments are tied together by some schoolgirl-report-style interviews on the street, with supposed members of the public.
