'Alvar Mayor - Death and Silver'

4 Winds Publishing, 1989, 56 pages

Written by Carlos Trillo
Illustrated by Enrique Breccia

An age of discovery. An era of Apocalypse. A hero of shadows. He is the son of a conquistador who has forsaken the Spanish to embrace the ways of the Incas. To his own people he is an outlaw. To the Incas he is a stranger. His story is one of the most celebrated comic stories in the world.
