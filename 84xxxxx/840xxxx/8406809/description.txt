The True Story: Series 1
The True Story is a fascinating six-part documentary series revealing the real-life events that inspired some of Hollywood's most famous blockbusters. Uncover the actual stories and characters behind classic films including




Part: 7: Jaws 
Documentary examining the shark attacks in New Jersey in 1916, believed to have influenced Jaws. At the heart of the story lies a mystery: were sharks really the culprits? The Jersey Shore shark attacks. The year was 1916. America was in the grip of one of its longest-running heatwaves. Naturally, this sent thousands of people flocking to the nearest rivers and beaches, looking for ways to keep cool. In all, five people were attacked on the Jersey Shore. Only one survived.
