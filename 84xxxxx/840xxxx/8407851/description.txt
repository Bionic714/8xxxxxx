Trophy wife Kylie Ireland is tired of being ignored by ambitious, cheating husband Nick Manning. To stave off her loneliness, she invites younger sister Lindsey Meadows to move in. But when Lindsey brings home new boyfriend Tyler Knight, Kylie can't deny an overwhelming attraction to the gorgeous, African-American stud. Is the older redhead doomed to stay in a loveless marriage, or will she give in to her sexual temptation? When Lindsay's life takes an unexpected turn, Kylie knows it's now or never. Will she give up her pampered lifestyle with a man she doesn't love, or finally give in to her forbidden lust? Michelle Lay and Aaron Wilcox costar in this tale of deception, forbidden desires and true love.
