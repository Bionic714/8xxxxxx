Clash: Encounters of Bears and Wolves

Nature Documentary hosted by F. MURRAY ABRAHAM, published by PBS broadcasted as part of PBS Nature series in 2010 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

The wilds of Yellowstone National Park are a world of predators, scavengers and opportunists. In this vast and complex kingdom, two dominant predators reign supreme the grizzly bear and the wolf. Though these two fearsome hunters would normally rule their ranges uncontested, in Yellowstone they must share resources, or face starvation. Now, join NATURE and discover who will triumph in Clash: Encounters of Bears and Wolves.
