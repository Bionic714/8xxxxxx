Score is a WordPress and BuddyPress theme that is ideal for reviewing any kind of content.

The theme is integrated with the powerful GD Star Rating plugin to bring you the following review features seamlessly:

-Both the post author and visitor can rate posts
-Users can rate posts from the top of the review, via the comments or both
-Sort reviews by date, title, site and user ratings
-Vote up/down other users comments
