The SW1500 is a classic example of a US yard switcher, and the different colour schemes and variants included in this add-on make it ideal for all your switching operations in Train Simulator.

The SW1500 Switcher is in the following liveries: Union Pacific, Southern Pacific, BNSF, Burlington Northern, Norfolk Southern Lines, Norfolk Southern Horse, Frisco, Western Pacific, Southern and Canadian National.
