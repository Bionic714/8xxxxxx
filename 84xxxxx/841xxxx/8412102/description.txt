Summer Palace, is a 2006 Chinese film and the fourth feature film by director Lou Ye. The film was a Chinese-French collaboration produced by Dream Factory, Laurel Films, Fantasy Pictures and Sylvain Bursztejn's Rosem Films. Wikipedia
Initial release: 2006
Director: Lou Ye
Running time: 140 minutes
Cast: Lei Hao, Xiaodong Guo, Yihong Duan, Lin Cui, Ling Hu, Chi Le, Xianmin Zhang
Genres: Coming of age, Period piece, Drama, Chinese Movies, Romantic drama, World cinema, Romance Film, Political drama
