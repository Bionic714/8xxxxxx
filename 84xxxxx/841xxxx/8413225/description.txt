Special investigator Nick Terry, frustrated by seeing criminals set free due to the regulations that seemed to hinder the police force, put on a costume and armed himself with high-tech gadgets (including a blacklight ray that projects darkness), transforming himself into the Owl. His girlfriend, Bella Wayne, assists him as his sidekick Owl Girl.

This is a neat collection of all the original owl stories, without all the extra stories in the various books he appeared in!
