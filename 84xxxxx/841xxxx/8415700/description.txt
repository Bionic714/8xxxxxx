Gaza's Property Ladder

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Seyi Rhodes, published by Channel 4 broadcasted as part of CH4 Unreported World series in 2013 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Reporter Seyi Rhodes and producer Daniel Bogado travel to Gaza to reveal what must be one of the world's most unlikely property booms. In this war-torn territory, 'Location, Location, Location' means finding an apartment in one of the highly sought after areas that are usually not shelled or hit by missiles. As a result, ordinary families are being priced out by a dramatic rise in rental costs, while estate agents and property developers make huge profits.
