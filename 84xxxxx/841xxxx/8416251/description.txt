Subrosa's first full length video has been a 4 year project and the work the team put in definitely shows with 39 minutes of footage filmed at famous spots all over the world. It features full parts from Hoang Tran, Simone Barraco, Lahsaan Kobza, Scott Ditchburn, Mark Mulville, Kyle Hart and Joris Coulomb, along with clips from the rest of the Skeleton Crew.
