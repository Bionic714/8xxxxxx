The Eurovision Song Contest 2013 will be the 58th annual Eurovision Song Contest. The contest will take place in Malmö, Sweden, following Loreen’s win in the 2012 Contest with the song “Euphoria”. This is the fifth time that Sweden will host the Contest, the last time being in 2000. The dates set for the two semi-finals are 14 May and 16 May 2013, with the final taking place on the evening of 18 May 2013.
