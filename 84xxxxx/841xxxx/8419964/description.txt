My Kitchen Rules Australia Series 4 Episode 46 Grand Final

When Australia's favourite home-cooking show returns for its fourth series in 2013, the table will be set for a few extra guests.

It's an all Queensland battle in tonight's Grand Final as Dan and Steph vs Jake and Elle. As they each prepare a five-course meal for 20 guests, the pressure is on to impress the judges. Who will walk away with the $250,000?
