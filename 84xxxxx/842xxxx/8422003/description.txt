Description :
Horror-action on the subject of the famous writer and filmmaker Clive Barker. A game in which entwined the darkest and insane elements of his work. Past and future lost in the African desert city of Al Khali mixed, when evil has invaded our world through spatial faults. The detachment of special purpose "Jericho" in possession of both conventional and paranormal warfare techniques, given the task to locate and destroy the source of evil that engulfed the city. But to do so they have to make a chilling journey through time, plunging ever deeper into the gloomy past of the city. Unremarkable town meet strangers in several forms: the smoking ruins of the Nazi occupation, the Arab city Crusader, a majestic Roman settlement and the futuristic landscape of the fourth millennium.
