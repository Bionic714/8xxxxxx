Koch Brothers Exposed

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Robert Greenwald, published by Brave New Foundation in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

A hard-hitting and revealing investigation of the 1% in America at its very worst. Koch Brothers Exposed is the latest film from Acclaimed Director Robert Greenwald (Wal-Mart: The High Cost of Low Price, Outfoxed, Iraq For Sale). From environmental pollution to their efforts to dismantle social security for working Americans, the Koch Brothers have launched a large network, attacking American values. 

Produced by Brave New Foundation.
