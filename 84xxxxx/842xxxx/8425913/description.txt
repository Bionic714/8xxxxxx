Beaming with Health: The Simple Guide to Feeling Good By Mim Beim
1993 | 192 Pages | ISBN: 0733303072 | PDF | 2 MB



A guide to health care through the use of natural therapies, particularly herbal remedies. Contains an alphabetical listing of illnesses and treatments, the common and botanical names of herbs, and a bibliography. The author is a professional naturopath who is heard regularly on radio station JJJ.
