Costume designer for the Ballets Russes, Léon Bakst (1866–1924) created exotic, boldly colored apparel for a number of theatrical presentations, among them The Firebird, Cléopâtre, and Schéhérazade. This exciting collection features 30 Asian-inspired designs. Budding artists can follow the original color schemes shown on the covers, or select their own hues.
