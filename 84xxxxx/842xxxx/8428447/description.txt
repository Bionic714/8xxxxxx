The country's King of Acoustic Pop NYOY VOLANTE returns with an all OPM album "Tuloy Pa Rin" which features well-loved hits like Ipagpatawad Mo, Bakit Ba Ganyan, Sa Isip Ko, Tuloy Pa Rin, Sana Dalawa Ang Puso Ko and many more. It also includes 3 original songs- Pikit and Miss Kita. The album has a bonus CD- minus one versions of the songs for an ultimate sing-along experience.
