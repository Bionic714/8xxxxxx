Guests: Kim In-kwon, Lee Kyung-kyu, Ryu Hyun-kyung
Venues: Seoul City Hall (Jung-gu, Seoul)
Mission: Receive the higher score in Superpowers Karaoke

Running Man is a South Korean variety show; a part of SBS's Good Sunday lineup, along with K-pop Star. This show is classified as an "urban action variety"; a never-before-seen new genre of variety shows. The MCs and guests complete missions in a landmark to win the race. It first aired on July 11, 2010 with 142 episodes aired.
