Space: 1999 - Classics Remastered #9

In this pulse pounding issue of SPACE 1999 CLASSIC, The Commander and Mal are trapped in a life and death situation while they try to return to the Alpha after a fatal explosion. Later as the crew descends to the embattled and seemingly deserted planet of Xeron they get more than they bargained for while looking to resupply and end up in yet another situation where they must fight for their lives.

Written by Andrew E. C. Gaska
Art by John Byrne, Gray Morrow, Miki
