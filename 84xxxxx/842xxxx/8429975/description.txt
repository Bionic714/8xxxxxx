Yeh Dil Maange More a famous hindi comedy stage play, originally written by Kedar Shinde in Marathi, adapted and directed by Paritosh Painter and Presented by Kaustub Trivedi and Swapna Pradhan. Starring Tanaaz currim, Paritosh Painter, Kamya Punjabi, Ninad Kamat, Imran Khan and Kashyab Sampura. Produced by Vikram Pradhan.
This is a hilarious play about the story of a couple whose marriage is on the rocks and by sudden change of fate they have new neighbours who happen to be old time time friends. Both husband and wife get attracted to their neighbours spouses and this makes for a lot of funny twists in the situation. Watch this play to laugh out all your woes and find out what happens to the two couples and their marriages.
