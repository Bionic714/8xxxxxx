English
Our entire way of life depends on electricity. So what would happen if it just
stopped working? Well, one day, like a switch turned off, the world is suddenly

us why? Now, 15 years later, life is back to what it once was long before the
industrial revolution: families living in quiet cul-de-sacs, and when the sun
goes down lanterns and candles are lit. Life is slower and sweeter. Or is it?
On the fringes of small farming communities, danger lurks. And a young woman's
life is dramatically changed when a local militia arrives and kills her father,
who mysteriously - and unbeknownst to her - had something to do with the blackout.

coming-of-age journey to find answers about the past in the hopes of
reclaiming the future.
