Riff Raff was a punk rock band from Santa Cruz, CA consisting of a revolving cast of musicians. The players on this album were: Troy Lewis, Paul Netto, Thom White, and Josh White.

This was unfortunately their last release before disbanding permanently in 2006.

Other releases were: Kustom Made Hell, Demo, Angels of Old, Speed Kills.

Material was released on FSA recordings and Half-Pint Records.

Members played in First to Fall and Moon-Eater.
