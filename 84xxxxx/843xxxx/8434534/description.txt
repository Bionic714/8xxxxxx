Seethamma Vakitlo Sirimalle Chettu, also known as SVSC, is a 2013 Telugu drama film, written and directed by Srikanth Addala.[8] The film stars Venkatesh, Mahesh Babu, Anjali and Samantha, with Prakash Raj, Jayasudha and Rohini Hattangadi in supporting roles. The film was produced by Dil Raju under Sri Venkateswara Creations. [9]

The film was touted to be the first multi-starrer in contemporary Telugu cinema.[10] The film was launched on October 6, 2011 and filming began on January 18, 2012 in Visakhapatnam.[11] The soundtrack of the film, scored by Mickey J Meyer, was released on December 16, 2012. The film was released worldwide on January 11, 2013 to rave reviews from the critics and turned out to be a blockbuster.[12]
