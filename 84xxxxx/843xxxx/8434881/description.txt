Meitantei Conan/ Detective Conan/ Case Closed

Eighth Detective Conan movie 


Original Airdate: April 17, 2004
Running Time: 108 minutes

The eighth movie was about a poisoning case on an airliner, and since the pilot and co-pilot were also affected, Conan Edogawa and Kaitou Kid were forced to take care of the lives of the passengers.
