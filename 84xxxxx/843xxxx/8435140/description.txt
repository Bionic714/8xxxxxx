The new British band "invade" the music business, and Big Time Rush are afraid they are going to end their career. For save their carrer, they decide to hire new managers, but disagree on who they are going to take, so Kendall and Carlos go with the first and James and Logan with the second. At the end, they understand that they have to do their job like "Nobody's Around" and sing that song. Meanwhile, Katie is trying to find a best friend to talk about boys, but Mr. Bitters has turned the Palm Woods into a businessmen living place, so along with Buddha Bob they try to kick them out.
