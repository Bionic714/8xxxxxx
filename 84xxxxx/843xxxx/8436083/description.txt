AutoEye 2.0 is an automated image enhancement solution that rebuilds lost detail, color and enhances digital photos to bring out amazing clarity and color fidelity. AutoEye 2.0 runs as a stand-alone application and as an Adobe Photoshop plug-in.

AutoEye 2.0 has patented IVIT (Intelligent Visual Imaging Technologies) algorithms that are designed to analyze the pixels in your photo and determine the best enhancement solution and method to bring out the color and sharpness necessary to make the photo look fantastic.
