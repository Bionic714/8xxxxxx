Description:
Welcome to Hong Kong, the city where the flickering light of neon lights intertwined with everyday life, a city whose exotic locations and seething streets hide one of the most powerful and dangerous criminal organizations in the world - Triad.

In this game you have to open world try on the role of Wei Shen, undercover cop who is trying to destroy the Triad gangs from the inside. Prove your value on the way to the tops of the organization, taking part in violent criminal operations, and do not let the criminals to reveal themselves. Torn between loyalty to a police icon, and gangster code of honor, you have to gamble and not get lost in the blurry boundaries between truth, loyalty and justice.
