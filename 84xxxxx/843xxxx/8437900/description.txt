La Sante brings you raw oldschool EBM sounds. Vananaba is third release of La Sante. It brings certain changes to the style, most notably the live drums were introduced (or at least ones that sound more live than we are used to in electronic world)
As usual the songs are dynamic and there is a lot of melody. You are getting 6 energetic sound that are bound to be liked by old and new fans alike.
