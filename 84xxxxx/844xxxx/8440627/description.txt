


The case of Antoinette Smith, who disappeared in July 1987 after attending a David Bowie concert at Slane Castle, Co Meath. 
Nine months later her body was found by a family walking through Killakee woods in the Dublin mountains, 
and a taxi driver subsequently gave an account of a woman fitting her description travelling in his vehicle to the outskirts of Rathfarnham village with two men.
