Zebra2 v2.6

Zebra is our wireless modular synthesizer, combining a powerful modulation engine with many different types of synthesis: additive, freehand or spline-based waves, oscillator spectral effects, FM oscillators, classic synth and comb filters. The entire signal path is stereo.
