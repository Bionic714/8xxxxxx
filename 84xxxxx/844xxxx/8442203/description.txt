Jesse Jane Has Gotten Herself Into A Mess! Looking to make life with husband Manuel Ferrara more comfortable, Jesse Jane welcomes new house keeper Vicki Chase into her world. But having Vicki around proves to be more trouble than Jesse can handle when she gets caught making a mess with her side of man meat, Erik Everhard. On the sly, Vicki uses her perfect young body to tempt Manuel into filling her salacious desires. Having hard evidence of both spouses' infidelity, Vicki lets them know who is really in charge! She turns their home into a den of iniquity, inviting Tristyn Kennedy, Maddy O'Reilly and some wild boys over to desecrate every inch of the house. With Jesse blackmailed into silence, she is at the mercy of a deviant that has all the leverage!
