Swamp People is an American reality series that was first broadcast on History on August 22, 2010. The show follows the day-to-day activities for several Cajuns living in the Atchafalaya River Basin swamp in Louisiana, USA who hunt American alligators for a living.
The program's latest season, its fourth, debuted on February 14, 2013 at 9PM ET/PT.
