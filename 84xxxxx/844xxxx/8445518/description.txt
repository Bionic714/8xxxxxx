And here you go, the next book in the series about the duck detective, Inspector Canardo.

Inspector Canardo 10 - The Girl who Dreamed of the Horizon
Canardo is yet again after his old nemesis, Rasputin, who have taken over a biker gang. But Rasputin is not the only danger lurking in the desolate hotel where Canardos car breaks down. For there is nothing to do in the dusty desert area, and even pretty ladies can get bored, and dream of heading into the horizon.
