Ray Harryhausen: The Early Years
Ray Harryhausen has amazed and inspired countless filmmakers and fans around the world for over 50 years with his breathtaking motion picture effects and stop-motion animation. Presented here is a collection of his earliest work.
Leaving no doubt as to Harryhausen s seminal influence on modern-day special effects, the documentary features enlightening and entertaining interviews with the man himself
