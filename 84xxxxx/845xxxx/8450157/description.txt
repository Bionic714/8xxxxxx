The next FCUP series is 'Zero Tolerance'. Published in 1990, it was written by David Barbour and penciled by Gary Amaro and Tim Vigil.

Zero Tolerance is an independent watchdog agency chartered in the year 2017 in response to growing public unrest at reports of high-level corruption and government misconduct. Two agents, Turk and Molly, have the US President in their sights.
