WHAT IS ANSYS
ANSYS, Inc. is an engineering simulation software (computer-aided engineering, or CAE) developer. ANSYS offers engineering simulation solution sets in engineering simulation that a design process requires. Companies in a wide variety of industries use ANSYS software.
