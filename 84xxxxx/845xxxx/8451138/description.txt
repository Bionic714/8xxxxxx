5. Bizet: Carmen – Habanera (2.20)

6. Mendelssohn: A Midsummer Night’s Dream – Divertissement ACT II (4.03)

7. Puccini: Gianni Schicchi – O mio babbino caro (2.07)

8. Strauss: Die Fledermaus – Tritsch-Tratsch-Polka (2.44)

9. Rameau: Zoroastre – Entr’acte Ouverture/A mes tristes regards ACTII/Scene I (5.02)

10. Rossini: La Cenerentola – Signora Altezza ACT I (3.00)

11. Bellini: Norma – Casta Diva (6.32)

12. Balanchine: Jewels – Emeralds – Final (Faure) (3.52)

13. Cecilia & Bryn at Glyndebourne –Mozart: Die Zuberflote, Pa-pa-pa-pa (duet) (2.37)
