Meet Brenda (Mikayla Mendez) and Mark (Rocco Reed), a couple on the threshold of matrimony. Wanting to keep their relationship 'fresh' they decide to participate in a special program... Couples Camp. Hilarity ensues as they discover that it's not exactly what they expected. Great camp side stories provide a sex filled ride into the heart of what every couple runs into on their journey to find everlasting love.
