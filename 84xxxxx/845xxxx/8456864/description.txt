Superman: Under a Yellow Sun
Publisher:  DC Comics

Writer:  John Francis Moore

This prestige format one-shot reads like a novel as written by Clark Kent. It deals with the mild mannered reporter's frustrations with his job, his earning power, and his reward-less fight for truth, justice, and the American way.
