Danny Boyle: Man of Wonder Extended

Arts Documentary hosted by Mark Kermode, published by BBC broadcasted as part of BBC The Culture Show series in 2013 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

An extended version of Danny Boyle's interview with Mark Kermode, featuring additional contributions from those who have worked with the Oscar winning director.

Topics include the movie Trance, London 2012's afterglow and the highs and lows of an extraordinary filmmaking career. This is an indepth, revealing portrait of Boyle's personality which further explores his unique creative work across theatre, television and film. With contributions from James McAvoy, Vincent Cassel, Rosario Dawson, John Hodge, Andrew Mcdonald, Frank Cottrell Boyce and Underworld's Rick Smith.
