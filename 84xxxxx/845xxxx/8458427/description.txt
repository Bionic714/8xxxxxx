DER KAISER VON ATLANTIS (1943)
A One-Act Musical by Viktor Ullmann

World Premiere Recording 1994
London CD

From the "Entartete Musik" (Degenerate Music) Series

Performed by the Gewandhausorchester Leipzig
under the direction of Lothar Zagrosek

Includes complete theater piece and "Holderlin Lieder" (final three tracks)
Scans of libretto (German - English) and CD booklet.

Music written by Ullmann to a text by Peter Kien
for production in the "model" concentration camp of Terezin, 1943

The following year Ullmann was transported to Auschwitz and gassed, along with most of the composers, artists, writers, musicians, etc. of the camp.
