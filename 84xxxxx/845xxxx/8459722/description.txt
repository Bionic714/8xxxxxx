NINETY POUND WUSS DISCOGRAPHY
--------------------------------------------------------------

This discography may or may not be complete.  It contains the following 4 releases...

 * 1996 - Ninety Pound Wuss
 * 1997 - Where Meager Die Of Self Interest
 * 1999 - Short Hand Operation

Ninety Pound Wuss was an American punk rock band formed in Port Angeles, Washington in 1994. They signed to Tooth & Nail Records in 1995 and released three studio albums over the next five years which would be supported by several national tours.
