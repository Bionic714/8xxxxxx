A CAVALCADE OF KINGS (50 Episodes)

Cavalcade of Kings features the dramatization of the history of the British Monarchy. The show is an entertaining refresher of the great history of the British Isles including the battles, marriages, and other royal escapades starting with Edward the Elder in the 10th century through the reign of Queen Victoria.
