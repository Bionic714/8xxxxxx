LZR (johnny LZR) (pronounced Lazer) is a electronic Detroit musician best known as being the co-founder of the experimental punk band HUMAN EYE (In The Red Records, Hook or Crook Records, Sacred Bones Records, GONER records, CASS, Ypsilanti records, ect.).
LZR was also been a member of the Conspiracy of Owls (Burger Records) and filled the keys in the Spits for a short while.

solo albums out on KYB (Montreal), Before Common Era (Detroit), UFO factory (Detroit) and soon Skrot Up (Denmark)
