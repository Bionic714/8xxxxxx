
3D scene of calm bamboo forest comes to your Android devices. Trees and plants slowly wave with gentle wind; occasional butterflies will fly through warm sun rays. The view rotates reacting to your gestures and homescreen changes, revealing the different angles of serene forest.

You can customize the following options of live wallpaper:
- type of ground and grass;
- bamboo plants;
- butterflies or fileflies;
- path and railings;
- stone lanterns, falling leaves, sun rays, dust particles etc.

What's in this version:
Added lanterns configuration.
Added fully configurable path and railings.
