A truly weird and obscure heroine from the Golden Age:


Mother Hubbard is a mysterious witch who uses witchcraft to battle Nazis and other evil doers. She is a very powerful magic user and accesses her powers through magic rhymes. Mother Hubbard also had a flying broomstick. 

 Golden Age Appearances 
    Scoop Comics #1-3
    Bulls Eye Comics #11
    Spotlight Comics #3
