Covert Ops: Nuclear Dawn (2000) PC Game


languages: Russian, english
Version: 1.0.0.0


Description:

Game Covert Ops action: Nuclear Dawn (Chase the Express) is developed round history with the train "Blue Harvest" which was seized as a result of an impudent act of terrorism by "Army of Revelation" group. The former serviceman Boris Zugorsky directs terrorists, however, as it became clear, representatives of the international terrorism and even the staff of NATO are involved in this mysterious business. As a result the armored train goes to Europe and it needs to be stopped by all means. You play for soldier Jack Morton to whom the leading role in opposition to these ominous plans is prepared. It should be noted that Covert Ops has some options of the termination.
