
In this documentary about the Brazilian educational system, rich and poor teenagers from 5 different schools in 3 cities in Brazil talk about what happens in their schools. Why doesn't it work at all? Why is it so difficult to be a teenager? The background is a discussion of the Brazilian educational system; the story of this film is the clash between different realities and how it portrays a moment so rich and powerful in emotions.
