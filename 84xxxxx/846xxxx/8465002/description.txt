Cheech & Chong interviewed on Hannity show on Fox News Network - April 26, 2013


The comedy duo of Cheech Marin and Tommy Chong appeared on the Sean Hannity Show on Fox News to promote their new movie:


"Cheech & Chongs's Animated Movie"


Hannity asked the comedians about their views and experiences on a wide variety of  subjects. As usual. the conversation and banter was both interesting and funny.
