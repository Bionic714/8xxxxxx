This FreeBible software is no longer available from its source since a long time ago.

Here it is with the King James Version, American Standard Version, and World English Bible already installed in a portable setup.  It also includes Strong's Concordance by double clicking a word, Matthew Henry's Concise Commentary, Nave's Topical Bible, bookmarks, Rich Text notepad, a nice search function, maps and more.

Just create a shortcut to:

FreeBible.exe

and run!
