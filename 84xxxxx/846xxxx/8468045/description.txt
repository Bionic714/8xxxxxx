Men's Health (MH)
is the British edition of the 
American magazine Men's Health. 
The British magazine was launched 
in February 1995 with a separate editorial 
team and is now the best-selling monthly 
men's magazine in the United Kingdom, more 
than GQ and Esquire put together. The magazine 
focuses on topics such as fitness, sex, 
relationships, health, weight loss, nutrition, 
fashion, technology and style. The currently 
editor-in-chief is Morgan Rees; Toby Wiseman 
is the featured editor.
