Flickit is fun 2d physics arcade style game where you try to gain the highest score possible. Simply flick the player an watch as it collides with different objects, some of which will propel your player further or stop it completely. Collect in-game FlickCash to buy items that can be used in the game to gain a higher score or wear a costume, rank amount 3 other platforms and keep track of your stats.
