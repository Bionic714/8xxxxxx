Jorge Luis Borges and Adolfo Bioy-Casares, Six Problems for Don Isidro Parodi (E. P. Dutton, 1981).

ISBN: 0525480358 | 160 pages | PDF


Translated by Norman Thomas di Giovanni, and co-authored by Adolfo Bioy-Casares, this is a work of parodic detective fiction. It was originally published under the pseudonym H. Bustos Domecq [Honorio Bustos Domecq], used for several collaborative works by Borges and Bioy-Casares.
