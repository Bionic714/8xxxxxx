The Unbelievable Truth - S01 to 11 - Audio Comedy - BBC Radio 4 
67 x 30 Minute Episodes - 128K - Mp3 - Slimoo


 This radio show, hosted by David Mitchell, sees four players giving a lecture on a subject
 The majority of their speech consisting of fabricated lies. 
 However, each contestant must attempt to smuggle five strange-but-true facts past the rest of the panel, 
 hoping that no-one will notice what they have said is the unbelievable truth


 www.comedy.org.uk Review: 
 An entertaining series created by two of the people behind long-running BBC Radio 4 panel game I'm Sorry I Haven't A Clue,  specifically ISIHAC panellist Graeme Garden and producer Jon Naismith.
