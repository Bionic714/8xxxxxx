Lilyhammer Ep7 and 8 - The Babysitter/Trolls

The Babysitter/Trolls - In this two-part finale, Frank realises that his past is about to catch up with him when his doorman Arne is badly beaten in a case of mistaken identity. However, the hunt for the perpetrators is hindered by the fact that he is obliged to look after 12-year-old Jonas whilst Sigrid is away. On learning that Jonas has been kidnapped by mafia henchmen, Frank and Torgeir attempt to rescue him without Sigrid finding out that he is gone. Stars Steven Van Zandt, Marian Saastad Ottesen and Sven Nordin. (From Norway, in English and Norwegian) (Drama Series) (Final)
