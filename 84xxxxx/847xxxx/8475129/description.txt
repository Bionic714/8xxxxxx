5 x15 minute dramatisations of Roald Dahl short stories, each of them tales of horror with a comical twist. The stories in this collection are:

1) Man From the South
2) Skin
3) Lamb to the Slaughter
4)A Dip in the Pool
5) Nunc Dimitis

See also A Little Twist of Dahl for 5 more dramatisations.
