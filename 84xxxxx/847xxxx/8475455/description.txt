New Lives in the Wild

Travel Documentary hosted by Ben Fogle, published by Channel 5 in 2013 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Ben Fogle meets people who have given up the daily grind in favour of a new life in the wilderness.



3)  New Zealand 
Ben travels to New Zealand, where he meets the Long family, who live as hunter-gatherers in the Gorge River area of National Park. He helps dad Robert to hunt for rock lobsters and catch eels, while mum Catherine must climb a sheer waterfall to check for storm damage to the family's gravity-fed water pipe.
