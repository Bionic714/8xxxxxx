

Book title: New Ways to Learn a Foreign Language (1966)

Author: Robert A. Hall, Jr.

# of pages: 186


Description:

This text focuses on the nature of language learning in the light of modern linguistic analysis. Common linguistic problems encountered by students of eight major languages are examined--Latin, Greek, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian, German, and Russian. The text discusses the nature of language, building new language habits, overcoming interferences, linguistic analysis of the eight languages, and language in context. A bibliography of language texts and audiovisual aids is included.
