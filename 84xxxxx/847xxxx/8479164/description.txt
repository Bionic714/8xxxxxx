Camila Moreno (born July 8, 1985 in Santiago) is a Chilean rock and folk singer-songwriter. She became famous after the release of her first album Almismotiempo ("at-the-same-time") in 2009. In the same year, she was nominated for a Latin Grammy in the Best Alternative Song category for her single "Millones". Her voice, folk style and lyrics has been considered part of Violeta Parra's legacy, the most important Chilean folklorist.

Opmeitomsimla es el segundo álbum de estudio de la cantautora chilena Camila Moreno, lanzado en 2010 por el sello Oveja Negra.
