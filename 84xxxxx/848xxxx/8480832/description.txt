French Montana - Excuse My French [2013]


Excuse My French is the upcoming debut studio album by American rapper French Montana. The album is set to be released on May 21, 2013, through Coke Boys, Bad Boy Records, Maybach Music Group, and distributed by Interscope Records. The album features guest appearances from Max B, DJ Khaled, Mavado, Ace Hood, Snoop Dogg, Scarface, The Weeknd, Diddy, Drake, Rick Ross, Birdman, Lil Wayne, Nicki Minaj, Ne-Yo, Raekwon, 2 Chainz, and Machine Gun Kelly among others.
