
Puzzle Man is a puzzle simulator.
You'll find a variety of puzzles with different difficulty levels: easy with 24 pieces to advanced ones with 384. A challenge!
You can enjoy high quality images that, for sure, will provide you many hours of entertainment.

Game features:
- rotating pieces, just double tapping on each one.
- Autosave.
- Zoom in / out with multitouch gestures.
- Final image preview.
- Easy and intuitive.

Without doubt, this game can't miss on your android!
