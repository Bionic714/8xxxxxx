BBC Music Magazine (June 2013)

English | 124 pages | PDF | 96 Mb


BBC Music Magazine is a must for anyone with a passion for classical music. Classical music connoisseurs and new enthusiast alike will enjoy the fascinating features and reviews of over 120 new works in every issue.

In this issue: From "The Flying Dutchman" to "Parsifal", we mark Richard Wagner's bicentenary through his ten operatic masterpieces; Ashutosh Khandekar celebrates 30 years of the world's most prestigious singing competition; we dispatch James Naughtie to St Petersburg's Mariinksy Theatre to interview the great Russian conductor Valery Gergiev on the eve of his 60th birthday; and David Starkey, the celebrated historian, writes exclusively about his BBC TV series, "Music and Monarchy".
