Ukraine's AIDS Racket   2013 05  BBC  Our World

As an AIDS epidemic,mainly from infected needles,continues unabated in the Ukraine and Russia, stricken patients are faced with an indifferent health sector and a scandal over anti-retroviral drugs essential for effective treatment. 

Only a minority of patients with AIDS have an adequate source as corrupt bureaucrats and politicians skim off profits by inflating the prices of the limited quantity of the imported supply. 

A new law threatens to tighten the supply even further.
