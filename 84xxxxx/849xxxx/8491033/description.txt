
We are happy to introduce the new product in the world of puzzles!

Puzzle D3 Media is a hexagonal structure of the playing area replacing the classic puzzle. Puzzle D3 Media is a game that will help you spend quality time, rest from the daily routine and relax.

Doing puzzles promotes the development of imaginative and logical thinking, attention and concentration.

Game features:

- 2 ways to move the blocks;
- 3 levels of difficulty;
- 3 versions of the background music;
- 36 HD worlds;
- Playing for time;
- Support for a large number of devices;
- Multi-language menu.
