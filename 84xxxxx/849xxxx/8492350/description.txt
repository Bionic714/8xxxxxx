A peculiarity, broadcast by BBC Radio Scotland in 1958.

Set on a Scottish Island, the story tells of two brothers who become involved in a scientist's single handed plan to launch a satellite on a nuclear powered rocket, around the moon and back to the island. (If only NASA could be that accurate.....) The professor has designed and built the whole thing single handed. But wait, a dastardly fiend plans to steal the professors hand written plans. Can the two brothers stop the thief? All this, and Scottish country singing too. Who could resist......
