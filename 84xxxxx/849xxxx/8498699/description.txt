'Celluloid' tells the story of Malayalam cinema. The film is based on the life and

 struggles of J C Daniel, the father of Malayalam Cinema. It also takes a peek into

 the life of P K Rosy, the first heroine in Malayalam. 'Celluloid' which tries to

 revive the memories of the bygone era of Malayalam ...


Released on February 15 2013

Director: Kamal

Producer: Kamal, Ubaid

Production Co: Prime Time Cinema

Music Director: M Jayachandran

Cast: Prithviraj, Mamtha Mohandas, Sreenivasan, Tiny Tom, Chandni, Indranse, Nedumudi Venu,
 Thampi Antony, Thalaivasal Vijay, Sreejith Ravi, Chembil Ashokan, Irshad, Siddique
