Secrets of the Irish Landscape

In this major new three part series, airing as part of the RTE Goes Wild season, Derek Mooney will follow in the footsteps of one of Ireland's greatest unsung scientists Robert Lloyd Praeger.


1)  Part 1 
Derek Mooney follows in the footsteps of one of Ireland's greatest unsung scientists, Robert Lloyd Praeger, to uncover the flora and fauna of the island's landscape.

2)  Part 2 
Derek Mooney discovers how Ireland's first farmers changed the face of the region's landscape around 6,000 years ago, and also examines how the population was nearly wiped out.

3)  Part 3 
Derek discovers how the modern Irish landscape came to be, as waves of invaders and new ideas were imported into the country.
