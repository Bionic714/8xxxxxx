        Music of the Spheres is a puzzle-shooter, h. In it you can fire        
        bouncing bullets. When they bounce, a glockenspiel note is played      
        depending on the direction ofthe bounce.                               
                                                                               
        Puzzles are designed to show you as many interesting things as         
        possible, as concisely as possible. Nothing is repeated, and nothing   
        is meaningless.  Wherever possible, environments are composed to       
        embody some pattern.
