The Chickening is a run and gun platformer about a chicken who shoots lasers.. out of its eyes!

Initially created in 2009, The Chickening was finished in 2013 and being released on XBLIG, Ouya, and PC. This version is the free promo release. Feel free to copy and share it and the bonus materials.
