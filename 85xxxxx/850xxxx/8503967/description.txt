▌EN▐ "O Veneno Está na Mesa" (literally "The Poison is at the Table", meaning that it's served to be eaten, paraphrasing the expression used generally in Brazil to announce to someone that the lunch or dinner can be initiated already, calling this person to participate in the eating phase), is a documentary made by Silvio Tendler in 2011 about the use of agrotoxics in brazillian food.
