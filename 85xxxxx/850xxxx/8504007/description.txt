Freizeitkarte Project
---------------------
  http://www.freizeitkarte-osm.de/en/index.html  

   
These are GPS maps for the Garmin GPS to be used with  Garmin BaseCamp (OS X, Windows) or QLandkarte (Cross-Platform). These maps are taken from a snapshot of a country or region from the OpenStreetMap project.

OSM Snapshot: May 12th, 2013.
Region: Great Britain and Ireland
Platform: QLandkarte (Cross Platform). Download from  http://www.qlandkarte.org
