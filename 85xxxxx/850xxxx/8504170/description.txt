Saathi is a 1968 Hindi Romantic drama film        
written and directed by C. V. Sridhar. The        
film starred Vyjayanthimala and Rajendra Kumar    
in the lead with Simi Garewal, Pahari Sanyal,     
David Abraham Cheulkar, Veena and Ram Mohan as    
the ensemble cast while prominent actors such as  
Shashi Kapoor, Nanda, Sanjeev Kumar, Shabnam, D.K.
Sapru and Pratima Devi done a guest appearance.   
The film was produced by S. Krishnamurthy of Venus
Pictures. The films score was composed by Naushad 
with lyrics were provided by Majrooh Sultanpuri   
and Shakeel Badayuni, edited by N. M. Shankar     
and was filmed by Marcus Bartley.
