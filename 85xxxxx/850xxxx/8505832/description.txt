[size=150][b][color=green]"Mirrors" is a song recorded by American singer Justin Timberlake for his third studio album The 20/20 Experience (2013). It was released on February 11, 2013 as the album's second single, following his performance at the 2013 Grammy Awards. "Mirrors" is a pop song; its lyrics discuss a lover's "other half" after having taken a break in their relationship. The song received generally positive reviews from music critics, who complimented its production and lyrical content. It debuted at 26 on the US Billboard Hot 100 and later became a Top 3 hit, peaking at number 3 and at number one in the United Kingdom and within the top ten in several countries internationally[/color].[/b][/size]
