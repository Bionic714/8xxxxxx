
If you like popping bubbles and eat chickens, this is the game for you.

You are Walt, a nice fox, whose sole purpose is to catch and eat as many chickens as possible. Over the four seasons, you will meet new animals that will help you or slow you down in your endless quest for chickens. Try to get the highest score in each level to become the King of the Chickens.

Features:
- 4 different seasons!
- Different styles of play to unlock!
- Special powers to discover!
- Combos to earn more points!
