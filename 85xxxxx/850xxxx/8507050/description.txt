The Girl With The Dragon Tattoo

While working to solve the mystery of a 40-year-old murder, a discredited journalist and a mysterious tattooed computer hacker discover that even the wealthiest families have skeletons in their closets. Inspired by late author Stieg Larsson's successful trilogy of books. Winner of the 2011 BAFTA for Best Film not in the English Language. Directed by Niels Arden Oplev and stars Michael Nyqvist, Noomi Rapace and Lena Endre. (From Sweden, in Swedish)
