Fast & Furious 6 (Original Motion Picture Soundtrack) is the soundtrack to Fast & Furious 6, released on iTunes on May 17, 2013. It was released on CD on May 21, 2013.


Lucas Vidal composed the musical score for Fast & Furious 6. A soundtrack album to the film was released by Def Jam Recordings on May 17, 2013. It mainly featured many electronic and hip hop tracks
