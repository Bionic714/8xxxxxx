When a group of young ghost hunters spend the night in a haunted mansion in Beverly Hills they encounter more then they bargained for. Possessed by the wanton spirit of Chanel St. James the spook patrol is driven to one deviant act after another unable to resist her supernatural desires. Nothing can prepare them for an encounter with such Haunting Beauty.
