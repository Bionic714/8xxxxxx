BBC Doc. about the beginning of rock'n roll in Britain.

Cliff Richard, Marty Wilde, Joe Brown, Bruce Welch, Vince Eager, Cherry Wainer, Brian Locking, Vernon Nesbeth and The Quarrymen. 

Comment and analysis from respected authors Dominic Sandbrook, Peter Doggett, Dave Penny and skiffle expert Jim Carter.

 
Narrated by Roger McGough.
