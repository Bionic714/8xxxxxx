Title: Safari in Delhi 
Director: Nikhil Advani 
Cast: Carrie Eloise, Christopher Lloyd, Jane Lynch, Tara Strong 
Country: India 
Year: 2012 
Genre: Animation 
Duration: 92 min Summary: Animation "Delhi Safari" tells the extraordinary journey of several animals - a small leopard, his mother, monkey, bear and parrot Alex. When they discovered that the forest in which they live is about to be destroyed and turned into a construction site they go to Delhi to find the answers to the questions why it is necessary and why people always put at risk animals. Despite its good intentions, the group of animals fall more obstacles than expected. However we discourage them and they are ready to do everything to show people that if forests and wildlife continue to be destroyed humanity will not survive long.
