Plex is a media platform that is capable of organizing your media so that it can be 

viewed by any Plex app. In addition to allowing you to view all of your music, photos 

and videos, you can access online content from many of your favorite online providers 

via Plex channels. Plex currently supports over 200 channels including CNET, YouTube, 

Vimeo, Revision3 and many other popular online sources. With Plex Media Server for 

Mac you can stream additional content from Aperture, iPhoto, and iTunes.
