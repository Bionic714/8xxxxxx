These inventory cheats are for RC2.9 build 0008. Now planks, nails and saws can be spawn. So you can make your own little treehouse right away.
Based upon Not Too Much Items mod, made by acex222.

1. Put NTMI2 in gamedirectory media lua folder.

2. In-game, hold an F-key and press a letter key after that.

F1+Y=Popbottle
F1+U=Steak
F2+Y=Saw
F2+U=Hammer
F2+I=Sheet
F2+O=Nails
F2+P=Plank
F3+Y=Shotgun
F3+U=Shotgun Shells
F3+I=Axe
F3+O=Pistol
F3+P=9mm Rounds
F5+Y=Beta Blockers
F5+U=Painkillers
F5+O=Duffelbag
