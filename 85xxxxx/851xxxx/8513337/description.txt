'The Tortoise and the Hare'

Last Gasp Eco Funnies, 1971, 36 pages

Was to have been Air Pirates #3 before all future issues of Air Pirates were banned as a result of the "Walt Disney Productions vs. The Air Pirates" copyright infringement law suit. 10,000 copies of this comic were confiscated.

Contributions from: Gary Halgren, Dan O'Neill, Bobby London, Shary Flenniken, Ted Richards
