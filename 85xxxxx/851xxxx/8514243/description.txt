The first demo of melodic metal band Asphodel, titled Graces of the Fall and released in May 2013.

About the band:
Asphodel is a melodic metal band based in Patras, Greece. Their style is mostly influenced by gothic, black and doom metal.

Antreas - Guitars & clean/black vocals
Filon - Guitars & death growls
Mihalis - Keyboards
Periklis - Bass guitar
Haris - Drums


Tracklist:
