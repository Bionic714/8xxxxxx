Superman #83 (1993).  This is also the 34th issue of 1993, using the 'triangle numbering' that followed storylines through the various ongoing Superman titles in the 1990s.

This issue is the epilogue to the 'Funeral for a Friend' and 'Reign of the Supermen' storylines following Superman's death in the early '90s.  The issue features various DC Superheroes gathering to dismantle Engine City and erect a memorial to the destroyed Coast City.  Guest stars include Batman (Jean Paul Valley), Green Lantern (Hal Jordan), Aquaman, Guy Gardner and many others.
