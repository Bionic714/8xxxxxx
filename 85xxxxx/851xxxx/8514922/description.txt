Superman #95 (1994).  This is also the 46th issue of 1994, using the 'triangle numbering' that followed storylines through the various ongoing Superman titles in the 1990s.

This issue is part of the 'Dead Again' storyline that ran through the various Superman titles in 1994, involving the discovery of a body in Superman's old tomb and Superman's quest to find the culprit.  In this issue, Superman journeys to New Genesis to see if Brainiac is responsible.  Guest stars include various New Gods, as well as The Atom.
