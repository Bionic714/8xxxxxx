Adventures of Superman #518 (1994).  This is also the 47th issue of 1994, using the 'triangle numbering' that followed storylines through the various ongoing Superman titles in the 1990s.

This issue is part of the 'Dead Again' storyline that ran through the various Superman titles in 1994, involving the discovery of a body in Superman's old tomb and Superman's quest to find the culprit.  In this issue, Superman journeys to Apokolips and fights various people on the way to determine if Darkseid is behind the 'Dead Again' plot.  Includes a cameo by the Cyborg Superman...kinda.
