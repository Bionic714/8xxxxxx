
Steev, Vahine and the rest of the Squids are in danger! An infectious black ooze is corrupting their world. Only a few heroes can fight back and save their lush underwater kingdoms! Recruit a small army of stretchy Squids and lead them into turn-based battles against the dark hordes.

- Features
* Grab Squids by the tentacles, then fling them across the battlefield
* Mixes the best of tactical turn base with classic RPG elements
* Recruit a unique team of Squids in four different classes: shooters, scouts, troopers and healers
* Use strategy and skill to win turn-based battles, use the environment to your advantage
* Customize your party with special ability-boosting helmets
* Enjoy an epic story with giant turtle rides and heroic boss battles
* Discover the Squids' legendary world through gorgeous detailed levels

The game is localized in English, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean and Russian.
