"This Is What It Feels Like" is a song by Dutch Trance producer and DJ Armin van Buuren, featuring vocals from Canadian singer Trevor Guthrie. The song was released in the Netherlands by Armada Music as a Digital download on April 29, 2013 as the second single from his fifth studio album Intense (2013). It has peaked at number 2 in the Netherlands and number 6 in the UK Singles Chart. The song was written by Benno de Goeij, John Ewbank, Armin van Buuren, Jenson Vaughan and Trevor Guthrie.
