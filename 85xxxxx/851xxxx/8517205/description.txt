'Mama! Dramas'

Educomics, June 1978, 36 pages

Contributions from Trina Robbins, Shelby Sampson, Debra McGee, Terry Richards, Delores Thom, Edna Jundis, Suzy Varty, Joyce Farmer, Leonard Rifas


From the introduction...

"For you who are contemplating motherhood, or are already pregnant, may we give you some idea of what to expect and prepare for. For all of you who already have children, look through the pages of this book and find yourself. We are not alone!"

An underground about parenting and motherhood.
