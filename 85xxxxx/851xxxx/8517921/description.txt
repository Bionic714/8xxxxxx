We Were Heroes: 1st Cavalry Division (Airmobile), Vietnam
Vol. 3


The official story of the First Team in Vietnam including famous battles and operations at Plei Me, LZ X-Ray, LZ Albany, Bong Son, Khe Sanh and A Shau Valley. Filmed by the U.S. Army cameramen in the thick of the battle. It doesn't get any better than this.
