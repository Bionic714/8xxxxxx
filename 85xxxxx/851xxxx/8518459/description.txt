J.R.R.Tolkien - Master of The Rings

Also features artwork by The Brothers Hildebrandt, known for their trilogy illustrations, and Roger Dean, whose artwork is heavily influenced by Tolkien's books. This guide will navigate both the avid fan and the uninitiated through the majestic land of THE LORD OF THE RINGS.
