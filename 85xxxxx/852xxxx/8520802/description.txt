Description:
============

Trapcode Suite 12 consists of 10 plug-ins for After Effects, which are the industry standard for broadcasting, including ready-made effects, and flexible tools for 3D-modeling. Its flagship product, Particular 2, has a fast 3D particle system with the ability to custom particle shading and motion of particles in 3D space. Form 2 combines in the generation of particles with imported models OBJ, Mir creates a new fast rendering polygon mesh and Shine creates volumetric light rays. Use Trapcode Suite to create high quality text titles, animated backgrounds and logos, followed by rapid rendering. The package run: 3D Stroke, Echospace, Form 2, Horizon, Lux, Mir, Particular, Shine, Sound Keys, and Starglow.
