Lootera is an upcoming Bollywood period romance drama film directed by Vikramaditya Motwane. It is the second film directed by Motwane, after his critically acclaimed debut film Udaan. The film stars Ranveer Singh opposite Sonakshi Sinha in the lead roles. Lootera features music by Amit Trivedi and lyrics by Amitabh Bhattacharya.
