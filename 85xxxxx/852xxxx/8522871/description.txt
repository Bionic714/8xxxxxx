One Good Thing is the debut album of Nashville singer songwriter Sarah Gayle Meech. Album was released on August 8, 2012 and features 12 original and authentic honky-tonk hard country songs.

Track listing:

2 Old White Boots
3 All I Wants a Honky Tonk

6 Drink Myself to Sleep
7 Foolish
8 No Angel
9 Movin On Song
10 Sad and Lonely
11 Lucky Girl
12 Honey Put Your Money Where Your Mouth Is
