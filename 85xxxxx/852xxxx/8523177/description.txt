Genres:Fighting
Features:3D
For Android 2.2 and higher


Ultimate Stick Fight  the city is seized by a clan of a Dragon and only you can exempt the city from this crime. Battle to members of a gang, use combo blows, throw objects coming to your hands, earn coins and improve your skills.

Features:
choice of one of two heroes;
each hero has to master 20 achievements;
existence of special combo blows for making a big loss;
opportunity to use combinations of blows;
opportunity to throw various objects into enemies;
40 stages and battle with bosses;
8 unique locations;
3 levels of difficulty;
opportunity to earn coins and additional stars;
unblocking of new moves and purchase of combo blows;
modernization of skills.
