This FreeBible software is no longer available from its source since a long time ago.

Here it is with the King James Version, American Standard Version, and World English Bible with a batch installer.  It also includes Strong's Concordance by double clicking a word, Matthew Henry's Concise Commentary, Nave's Topical Bible, bookmarks, Rich Text notepad, a nice search function, maps and more.

Double click FreeBible_Insall.cmd and the install should take just a few seconds.  When finished, press any key to exit.

A shortcut will be created in your Start Menu or on the Windows 8 start screen.
