Cavalcade (2005)

Plot: An accomplished playboy Leo neglects his girlfriend and enjoys parties. His girlfriend parts from him and then, when he wants to see her once more, he has an accident. He survives, but his whole life is now to be changed. 
Cavalcade is a true story adapted from an autobiographical novel by Bruno de Stabenrath.
