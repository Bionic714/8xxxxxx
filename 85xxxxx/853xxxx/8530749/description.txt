Zapp 4 is no ordinary string quartet. This time they only played their own arrangements of pop-icon Radiohead. Blistering versions of songs such as A Wolf at the Door and My Iron Lung are now released on their new cd. The known melodies and electronic beats got transformed to pizzicato notes and a rich string sound.
