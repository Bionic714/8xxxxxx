Armiño negro (English language:Black Ermine) is a 1953 Argentine romantic film directed by Carlos Hugo Christensen and written by Rafael Maluenda and Pedro Juan Vignalle.

Una mujer de pasado turbio trata de ocultárselo a su hijo adolescente y cambiar. Pero no puede. Laura Hidalgo como una femme fatale frente al verdadero amor y los dramas familiares.

Directed by Carlos Hugo Christensen
Writer: Pedro Juan Vignale based on the novel by Rafael Malvenda
Cast: Laura Hidalgo, Roberto Escalada, Néstor Zavarce, Nicolás Fregues, Bernardo Perrone, Ricardo Galache, Enrique Abeledo, Gloria Ferrandiz, Federico Mansilla, Aurelia Ferrer, Juan Villarreal.
Language: Spanish
Country: Argentina
Genre: Melodrama
Runtime: 108'
Ripper: Retibuyendo
