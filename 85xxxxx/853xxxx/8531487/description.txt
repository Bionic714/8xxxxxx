[b]"Come & Get It" is a song recorded by American singer Selena Gomez for her upcoming debut solo studio album Stars Dance. It was released on April 8, 2013 by Hollywood Records as the lead single from the album. The song was written by Ester Dean, Mikkel S. Eriksen, and Tor E. Hermansen and was produced by Stargate. "Come & Get It" is a mid-tempo pop song with elements of Indian music; lyrically, it describes a female lover's attempt to rekindle a previously ended romance. Upon its release, "Come & Get It" received favorable reviews from music critics, who praised its musical diversity from Gomez's previous work.[/b]
