Morgan comes home after a tragic accident in the bikers race that has left him crippled and paraplegic, trying to meet his ends meet and starting a life after the accident.However, he tries to stay positive and doesn't make the accident, the end of his life. But deep within he has feelings of being useless and rather being dead. He meets Dean and the two strike a friendship. Later develop a romantic connection . Morgan wants to participate in the biking race in the handicap category more so because of his competitive nature and to prove his ability. Everyone is against that , even Dean.. leading to differences between the two. Finally , he realizes that its his fault and makes peace with himself. Written by Ronnie
