Family Tree

Genre: Comedy
Year: 2013
Country: USA
Runtime: 28 min
Producer: Christopher Guest, Jim Piddock
Cast: Chris O'Dowd, Tom Bennett, Nina Conti, Jim Piddock, Michael McKean

.: Plot :.
Centers on 30-year-old Tom Chadwick who, after losing his job and his girlfriend, begins exploring his family heritage after inheriting a mysterious box from a great aunt he never met. Unsure of his identity, he uncovers a world of unusual stories and characters as well as a growing sense of who he and his real family are.
