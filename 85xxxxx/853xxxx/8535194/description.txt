Dragon Lore: Curse of the Shadow

Genre: Action, Fantasy
Year: 2013
Country: USA
Director: John Lyde 
Casts: Danielle Chuchran, Richard McWilliams, Paul D. Hunt, James C. Morris, Eve Mauro and etc.	

.: Plot :.
A secret cabal, The Shadow, works to bring about the return of Goth Azul - the Undead God - in the rich world of SAGA, populated by elves, orcs, dwarves and dragons.
