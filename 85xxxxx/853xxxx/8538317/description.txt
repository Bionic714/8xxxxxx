Kuro no Tsuki
Company: Moon Parrot
Release date: May 18, 2001
Genre: Visual Novel
Language: English subtitled

Summary: Kuro no Tsuki tells the story of two royal brothers, Roland and Altraud. A sudden change in the once-kind Roland brings about the downfall of their kingdom and a hurt Altraud, having lost everything, begins the search to find out why. Could the Black Kingdom, having been vanquished centuries ago, be pulling the strings?
