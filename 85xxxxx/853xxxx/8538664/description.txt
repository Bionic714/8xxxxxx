The newspaper "Virtual Joy" is published in Belarus since 2000 and is a well-known Belarusian edition of the games.
----
For batch scanning software was used IrfanView 4.35 and A4 MFP scanner from Canon MP250 (three scans on each page), staple the pages, rotate and crop in ArcSoft Scan-n-Stich Deluxe, creating *. djvu in Documents Express Editor 6.0.1 (compression with the profile of Normal, 300 dpi, OCR, text quality lossless).
