Description:
Gunpoint can be described as "Deus Ex in 2D". The goal of each level of the game - to get to a computer in the bowels of the building and carefully guarded brazenly steal sensitive information. In the course are all sorts of means - hell zaprygi in the windows and on the roof, scuffle, gadgets, and most importantly - the "redrawn" wiring in the building, allowing cause lifts open / close doors, turn on / off the light and deliver protection vserazlichnye trouble. Each level can be completed in more than one way.
