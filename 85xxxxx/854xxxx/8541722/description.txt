This is an Introduction to Guitar course that was offered by Coursera and Berklee College of Music. The instructor is Berklee's own Thaddeus Hogarth. Included in the torrent are videos in .mp4 format and accompanying .pdf's.

The course was 6 weeks long and it covered the following lessons:

Week 1: Acoustic / Electric Guitar and the Basics
Week 2: Getting Started / Fundamental Guitar Skills
Week 3: The Twelve Half Steps and Basic Notation
Week 4: Scales: Construction and Fingerings
Week 5: Chords: Building Easy Triads and Power Chords
Week 6: Putting it All Together: The Pentatonic Scale and Songs

Thank you for downloading and enjoy learning to play the guitar!
