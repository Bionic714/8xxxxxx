 Heres another take on this flick for anyone that wants it...
 The main bulk of the video came from a DVDR we were kindly given ( thxs ).
 The video was nice but was at a angle,interlaced,and lacked some life.

 So first thing was to make a script with tivtc to fix the interlace issues and drop 
 that into vdub,from there used the quad filter to pull the video level and resized it.
 Then fixed up the brightness,contrast,colouring etc....
 For the ending " the after credit scene " opted to use a youtube video ...this is lower Q but
 complete.
 Needed to fix up the ending as that was also cammed at an angle and was washed out.

 Now the audio ....the sync work goes to millenium :) However did drop it into adobe and remastered the
 audio to my liking.
 Now the after credit scene came with cam audio but i had some different line,so needed to cut that and sync
 it to the video to make it a full line copy.

 Will be honest and say video is dull in parts and sometimes has that "dirty" look to it....but fixing some
 of the issues creates more issues eg glares etc so i balanced it the best i could.
 Check samples of light and darker scenes to see what i mean.
