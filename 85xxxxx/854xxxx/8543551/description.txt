Thieving Birds are a band from Fort Worth, Texas that was formed in September of 2010. These five friends combine Rock, Country, Roots and Blues to create a unique musical offering to their audience.  They released their debut, self-titled album on June 25th, 2011 and entertain and amuse audiences with their high- energy live sets.
