Pokemon White Version 2 (U)



Pokemon White 2, a companion piece to Pokemon Black 2 is a sequel to Pokemon White. As you return to Unova, two years after the events that occurred in a previous game, you assume the role of a Pokemon trainer to capture and battle your way to the top. Unova region has been expanded and changed and Pokemon White 2 introduces the Pokemon World Tournament, where you can battle myriads of champions from Pokemon games. Discover new, legendary Pokemons and try to capture them in this epic Gen V Pokemon game that you can download and play on emulators for Pokemon.
