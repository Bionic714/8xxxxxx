Blood Brothers: The Complete Bad Breed Feud In ECW 


This comp features all seven of Ian and Axl Rotten's singles matches from their 1995 feud that were released on ECW Home Video and the two fancam matches that RF Video released. 

 Matches such as: 

 1. First singles match in ECW from Double Tables 
 2. First ever barbed wire bat match in the United States from Return of the Funker 
 3. Hair vs. Hair match from Three Way Dance 
 4. Strap match from 4/14/95 (fancam) 
 5. Bad breed death match from Hostile City Showdown 
 6. Barbed wire bat match from 4/29/95 (fancam) 
 7. Barbed wire bat and chair match from Enter Sandman 
 8. Barbed wire bat match from Mountain Top Madness 
 9. And finally the infamous first ever taipei death match from Hardcore Heaven 

 This is not for the squeamish
