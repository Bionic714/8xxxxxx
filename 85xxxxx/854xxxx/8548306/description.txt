This is 'Transformers: Another Time and Place', a text story written by Simon Furman with a few panels of accompanying artwork by Staz (pencils), Lesley Dalton (inks) and Caroline Steeden (colour).  This story was originally published in the Transformers (UK) Annual 1992.  Events in this story follow the Marvel Transformers G1 issue #80 and serve as one possible conclusion to the events in that series.

These scans are taken from a print-out of a pdf of the story and the quality is not as good as a direct scan from the source comic would be.  However, the story is still readable.
