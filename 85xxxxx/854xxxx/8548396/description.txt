This is the Infinite Crisis: Secret Files and Origins 2006 one-shot comic.  This issue was written by Marv Wolfman with Dan Jurgens, Jerry Ordway, Cam Smith, Howard Chaykin and others providing art duties.

The main story in this issue, "Heaven", serves as a prequel to Infinite Crisis, detailing some events from the perspective of Alexander Luthor, Superboy Prime, Earth-2 Superman and Earth-2 Lois Lane in the pocket dimension where they traveled after Crisis on Infinite Earths.  Also included are profile pages for all four characters.
