This is the special edition of Saga of the Swamp Thing #21, released in May 2009 as part of DC's 'After Watchmen' promotion.  This issue re-presents the classic Swamp Thing story 'The Anatomy Lesson', where Jason Woodrue (The Fluoronic Man) dissects the body of the Swamp Thing and makes a shocking discovery.  Written by Alan Moore, with art by Stephen Bissette and John Totleben.
