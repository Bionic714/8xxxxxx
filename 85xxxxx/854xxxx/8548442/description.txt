Superman #140 (1998).  This is also the 43rd issue of 1998, using the 'triangle numbering' that followed storylines through the various ongoing Superman titles in the 1990s.

While visiting the Bottle City of Kandor, Superman's friend goes missing after determining the true identity of the mysterious "Inventor" and Superman is framed for murder while searching for her.  Featuring the return of the Cyborg Superman.
