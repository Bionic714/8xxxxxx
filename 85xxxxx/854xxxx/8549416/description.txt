Release Description:

War of the Human Tanks is a story driven strategy game that pits the group of Human Tanks commanded by the player against the opposing Commanding Officers and their forces in a campaign fought on a series of grid-based maps. 
Players will have to use their wits to upgrade their units and utilize them to the best of their abilities in order to progress throughout the game.


Features:

Fast-paced strategic battles in over 30 maps,
Use resources earned from battles to develop new tanks,
Customize your tanks with various modules to deadly effect,
Multiple endings based on your performance,
Replay the story keeping your previously earned units and resources,
Free battle mode to practice and gather extra supplies,
English localization features two all-new bonus maps, hand-drawn battle backgrounds and an improved user interface

Publisher: Fruitbat Factory
Developer: Fruitbat Factory
Genre: Strategy, Adventure

Size:   1.00 GB
