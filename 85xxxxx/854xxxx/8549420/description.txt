Before his passing, Philip Coppens wrote an informative response to the YouTube documentary titled "Ancient Aliens Debunked". This 90 minute long video basically says almost everything on the popular TV series is misinformed and fictitious. For some fans this may be a disappointment. However, Chris White, the creator of the "Ancient Aliens Debunked" video, has made many false claims and errs in his narration and Philip Coppens will give clear examples of these, effectively Debunking Ancient Aliens Debunked.
 
And so the debate continues...
