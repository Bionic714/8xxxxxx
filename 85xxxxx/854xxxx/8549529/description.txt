Subtle Hustle is an in your face funk rock explosion of memorable melodies and solid grooves. They provide a six piece arrangement of two guitars, bass, drums, and various combinations of saxophones and flute. The band members hail from Washington DC to Baltimore and are known for inciting dance parties with or without their instruments present. Everyone in the group composes from a diverse background of influence, making it hard to pinpoint an exact comparison to other acts. Whether you love to dance or just enjoy a good show, Subtle Hustle is guaranteed to provide an energetic and unique experience.
