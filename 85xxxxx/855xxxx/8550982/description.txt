Bob and Fiona's impending marriage hits the rocks when Bob gets cold feet and demands a pre-nup. Soon all their friends are dragged into the middle of the argument as they try to renegotiate the terms of their relationships. Fiona learns how kinky her friends really are and that love is all about trusting the right person; the one who loves you for who you really are.
