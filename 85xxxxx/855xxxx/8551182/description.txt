Up-tight: The Velvet Underground Story By Victor Bockris, Gerard Malanga
1990 | 136 Pages | ISBN: 0711901686 | EPUB | 2 MB



The best-selling definitive history of The Velvet Underground. Widely acclaimed as one of the greatest rock books ever published it first appeared in 1983 and has remained in print in several languages ever since. Written and compiled by Victor Bockris and Gerard Malanga Up-Tight is based on interviews with all four members of the Velvets as well as others who became part of Andy Warhols circle of artistic collaborators.
