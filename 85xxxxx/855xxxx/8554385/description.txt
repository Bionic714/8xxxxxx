The 17th century: a young rebel steals a miraculous potion from the despotic ruler of a small kingdom. The perfume of this elixir has the power to restore youth. Trying to escape the King's guards, the boy is forced to leave the precious liquid with his best friend, who can't resist trying it out on himself and his wife. Their youthfulness returned, they begin to explore every erotic experience, without limits or rules. They're starting a new life...
