Marilyn Chambers undeniably changed the face of the adult film industry and here she stars in the classic ground-breaking ingenue role (her first!). A young woman is captured, ministered to and then licked into calm complacence by robed female attendants during a high-class, theatrical orgy staged for a black-tie audience. Brought to the screen by the innovative talents of the Mitchell Brothers, the viewer is taken on a cinematic journey into the sublime... This is the only Artie J. Mitchell video. Adult stars also making their debut in this video: James L. Mitchell, George Marconi, George McDonald, Ted McKnight, Barbara Parker, Ariel Porny, Leticia Torrez, Barry Vane, Nancy Wilson.
