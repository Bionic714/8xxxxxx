And here you go, Alices newest work, Pandemonium.

In 1951, a mother and her young daughter entered one of Americas most prestigious institutions for the treatment of tuberculosis, the Waverly Hills Sanatorium. The events that befell them shortly thereafter, both of a supernatural and criminal nature, were some of the most sordid of the times. Based on real events.
