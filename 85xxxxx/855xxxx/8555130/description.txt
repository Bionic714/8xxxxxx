After months of preparation Naval Intelligence agents Lenny and Mela pursue a group of sexual terrorists lead by crime boss Ian Scott. Since the success of the operation depends on the agents intimate involvement, the kinky duo use any means necessary to infiltrate and break up one of the largest sexual terrorist rings in history.
