Arity is a progressive metal band formed out of West Covina, California. The current line-up consists of Nicole Macias, Albert Burrola, Anthony Burrola, Chris Hunter, and Shawn Hernandez. After taking a break for a year or so from the music scene, Arity came together with influences of Protest the Hero, Periphery, Dream Theater, Devin Townsend, etc.
