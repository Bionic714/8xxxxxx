Eminem Presents: The Re-Up is a hip hop compilation album performed by various artists of Shady Records, The album features performances by Shady Records artists Eminem, D12, 50 Cent, Obie Trice, Stat Quo, Bobby Creekwater, and Cashis. The album debuted at number two on the Billboard 200 and has since sold over one million copies in the U.S alone and is certified platinum.
