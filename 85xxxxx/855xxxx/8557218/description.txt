
You find yourself trapped in a dungeon room, you don't remember how you got there or who tied you to that chain. Luckily that somebody forgot (?) the key right there so you can set yourself free.

Free? 
Will you be able to find your way out of this odd place you've never seen before? All you got is yourself and some old spirits with their magic powers to help you out in the escape.

Be ready to deal with mind twisting puzzles, deadly traps and the truth about this place during your getaway!

FEATURES
- Challenging puzzles
- Innovative gameplay featuring action and thinking
- Smooth difficulty curve
- 70 levels
- Many different special cubes such as jumpers, teleports, traps and more!
- Hundreds of ways to solve each level
- Universal APK for smartphone and tablets
- Fully independent game (support indie games!)

Stuck on a level? Visit http://kyubo.chestnutgames.com and find there the level walkthroughs weekly updated!

Kyubo is a puzzle action game that combines thinking and action. Completing each level will require you both perfect timing when moving or jumping and accurate decision making on how to move the cubes and in which order to disarm traps, enable triggers, access far locations and finally getting to the exit door and move on to the next room. 

Only completing the level using the spirits the minimum necessary will award you a crown! Will you get all of them?
Don't miss the in-game tips, they might contain extremely useful information about the gameplay and tricks you can use to complete levels and collect the crowns.
