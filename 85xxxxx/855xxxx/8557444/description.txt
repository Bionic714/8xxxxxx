American singer and songwriter Beyoncé Knowles, known simply as Beyoncé, began her career in the late 1990s as the lead vocalist of one of the best-selling American girl groups of all time, Destiny's Child, who sold around 60 million records worldwide. Her discography as a solo artist began in 2003 and consists of four studio albums, two extended plays, five live albums, one remix album and thirty-six singles (including ten as a featured artist) and twelve soundtrack and promotional singles.
