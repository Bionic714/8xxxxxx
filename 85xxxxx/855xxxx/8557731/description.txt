Chef Wanted with Anne Burrell - Season 2, Episode 2



Sunset Strip Icon

Mirabelle, a legendary restaurant on Hollywood's Sunset Strip, has just completed a renovation and is looking for an executive chef to complete their new vision. Chef Anne Burrell brings in four candidates who are tasked with using the scraps of high-end ingredients and in creating a new identity for the Mirabelle menu. The final two chefs face the ultimate test of running the kitchen for a night: One chef battles nerves, while the other must course correct to fix consistency issues.



Original Air Date: 07-Feb-2013
