
Navigate mind-bending puzzles on the fringes of the universe using only the gravity of planetary bodies to reach your destination. Become immersed in the nebulous tones and beautiful colors of deep space.

Grav features 31 Campaign levels spanning 4 galaxies punctuated by epic boss battles. Compete against your best times on each level and earn stars for the fastest times. Master each level to earn the coveted 3 star rating!

In Free Play Mode, guide your ship through an infinitely scrolling level filled with intergalactic hijinks. Face the toughest challenges from the campaign along with new power-ups and dark matter enemies. Score massive points by racking up a high multiplier and keeping your ship in one piece.
