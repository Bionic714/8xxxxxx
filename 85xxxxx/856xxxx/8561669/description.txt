Ancient Discoveries

History Documentary hosted by Sean Barrett, published by History Channel in 2003 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

Shot on location in the Mediterranean and the Middle East, Ancient Discoveries unearths amazing technologies we think of as modern, but which actually have their origins in antiquity. The series travels back in time to uncover remarkably sosphisticated inventions and to celebrate the ancient engineers whose ingenuity laid the foundations for todays technology.
Produced by Wild Dream Films.


1)  Computing 
The Antikythera Mechanism, Ctesibius' Water Clock.

2)  Medicine 
Brain Surgery and Galen, Doctor to the Gladiators.

3)  Robotics 
Heron of Alexandria's Miracle Machines, Steam Engine.

4)  Warfare 
Greek Fire, Roman Battering Rams, Helepolis Siege Tower.

5)  Ships 
Roman Naval Engineering and Caligula's Lake Nemi Pleasure Barges.

6)  Power 
The Baghdad Batterey, Roman Water Wheels, Archimedes Death Ray.
