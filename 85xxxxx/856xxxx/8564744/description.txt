Dazzling Reflections,  at the museum of fine arts of Rouen
==========================================================

Description
-----------

Dazzling Reflection (Éblouissants Reflets in French) is an exhibition of impressionnist paintings  at the museum of fine arts of Rouen, Normandy, France.

It is part of the impressionnist festival Impressionnist Normany (Normandie Impressionniste) which takes place in many museums in Normandy during the summer 2013.

This torrent contains these high-resolution scans of a number of paintings, rebuilt from the tiled version available on the exhibition website.
