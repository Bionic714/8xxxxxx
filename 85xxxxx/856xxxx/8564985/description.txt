
Increase the liveliness of your Android device with this moving wallpaper.

Features:
- Creates 3D effect from any image
- Includes high resolution Jelly Bean 4.2 wallpaper pack
- Efficient OpenGL hardware accelerated graphics
- Adjustable movement options
- Use your own images
- Close one eye to enhance the parallax effect

3D depth effect works best with images with strong edges - i.e. not plain colours or smooth gradients.
To start, long-press on an open area on your homescreen, select Wallpapers, Live 
Wallpaper and then pick 3D Image Live Wallpaper from the list.

The app will only install on devices that have a Gyro sensor.
