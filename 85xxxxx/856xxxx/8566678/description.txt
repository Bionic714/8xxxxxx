H kafth THessalonikia Voyla Vavatsh kai h dhmosiografos Xristina Rousakh enwnoun tis... dynameis tous kai ektinassoun th... limpinto ston aera. Mazi tous h Keli me ton.. apistefto kwlo, h Elena, o Zafeirhs, o Giorgio, o Apollwnios alla kai o... giatros, oi opoioi... mouskeyoun ta thrania.
To stori ekselissetai se ena kollegio thhlewn opou dieftharmenoi kathhghtes apoplanoyn tis foithtries se akolastes prakseis kai orgia, me epathlo enan... kalo vathmo sto telos ths xronias. Oi afeleis foithtries omws ekselissontai se kolasmena thhlyka pou zoun gia to seks kai... ksezoumizoun tous andres.
 
H Voyla Vavatsh entypwsiazei gia akomh mia fora me ton afthormhtismo kai thn fysikothta ths se oles tis skhnes, akomh kai stis legomenes... dyskoles, ekei opou xreiazetai antoxh ston pono kai ta dakrya...
