Get the know-how you need to create beautiful quilts and decorative quilted items.
Quilting is a fun hobby -- but where do you begin? From selecting fabrics and designing a quilt to stitching by hand or machine, this friendly guide shows you how to put all the pieces together -- and create a wide variety of quilted keepsakes for your home.
We'll have you in stitches in no time!

Discover how to
- Select the right fabrics and threads
- Design your masterpiece
- Use quilting software
- Save time with rotary cutters and other cool tools
- Quilt by hand or machine
- Get creative with applique
