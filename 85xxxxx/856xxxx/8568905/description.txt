
iOS 7 theme HD for Go Launcher is based on newly released apple iOS icons and wallpapers. This theme may work with other launchers like NOVA, APEX and SMART.

- Superb quality icons.
- Regular updates.
- Full HD (1080p) wallpapers.
*** Installation Instruction***
- GO Launcher EX: MENU &gt; theme preferences.
- Select and Apply iOS7 Theme.
