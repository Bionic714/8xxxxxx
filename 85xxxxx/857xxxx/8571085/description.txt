Upon returning to Galway a year on from the events in The Guards Jack is hired to investigate the murder of a man who fell from the scaffolding of a construction site. 
Assisted by Cody, a naive young man who hero worships him as the best private investigator in Ireland, Jack becomes drawn into the world of a vigilante group that seeks to deal out justice to those its leader believes have escaped suitable punishment by the law. 
Re-establishing contact with Anne Henderson he finds her in an abusive relationship with a local businessman.
