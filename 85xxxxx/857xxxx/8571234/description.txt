Out of desperation Maxwell brings in a very unconventional nanny into his upper class home. And while Fran is definitely lacking in formal training, the more than makes up for it with her street smarts and good looks. Before they know it, everyone is falling for her and she is running the show. The Upper East Side will never be the same!
