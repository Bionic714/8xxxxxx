Suehiro Maruo
Ultra Gash Inferno
Paperback: 214 pages
Publisher: Creation Books (29 May 2001)
Language: English

Compendium of graphic-novellas by the leading stylist of ero-guru ('erotic-grotesque') style of Japanese Manga, Suehiro Maruo. Unlike anything seen in Western comics, this unique style is a dramatic fusion of sex and violence. Available in English for the first time, and with an introduction by Romain Slocombe, this will be an essential purchase for fans of comic books and extreme fiction.
