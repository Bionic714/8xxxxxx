"Masha and the Bear" - a Russian animated series, aimed at a general audience. Cartoon created using three-dimensional graphics. For modeling, rigging and animation software package used Autodesk Maya. Render done on its own render farm of "Animakkord." To be aired on TV channel "Russia 1" in the "Good night, kids!" And the channel Carousel. Also been shown in France, Switzerland and Canada. [1] Since November 2009, the animated series of license is issued on DVD and Blu-Ray Mystery by sound.

In 2012 came the first spin-off called "Machines tales", currently consisting of 18 episodes based on Russian folk tales.
