"Throw the dice" video is filmed by Patrik Aronsson, Aronsson & Co AB at Verket, Avesta, Sweden on March 3rd 2013.
Edited by Patrik Aronsson and Johnny Öhlin.

The Song is a bonus track from the upcoming release of the remastered albums "Chased By Time" and "Without Remorse" 
Music by Johnny Öhlin. Lyrics by Isaac Isacsson.
