Kylie Minogue - The Abbey Road Sessions (2012)

The Abbey Road Sessions is an orchestral compilation album by Australian recording artist Kylie Minogue, released on 24 October 2012 by Parlophone. The album features sixteen tracks, all radically reworked, spanning Minogue's 25 years in the music industry. The album was recorded at London's Abbey Road Studios with Minogue's band and a full orchestra during November 2011. Musically, the album is primarily orchestral music, which features several instrumentation of guitars, pianos, strings and drums.
