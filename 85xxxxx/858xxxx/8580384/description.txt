Johnny Hodges - 1953 - Collates No. 2

Alternate tracks and single recordings Johnny Hodges made for the Verve label in the early and mid-'50s. They were issued at that time on 10-inch discs. These are both combo and larger orchestra cuts, and are now out of print. (AllMusic)
