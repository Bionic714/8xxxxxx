In the aftermath of Earth's destruction, the shaken and weary survivors struggle to start a brave new world. With the Legion of Super-Heroes having departed for deep space, another group--familiar yet different--offers the citizens a new symbol for hope. Long Live the Legionnaires!  This series runs in tandem with Legion of Super-Heroes v4 (1989).
