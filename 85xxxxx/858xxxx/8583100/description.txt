Deadly Tarot

Deadly Tarot - Sabine Clausen is torn between two men, her current boyfriend Uwe and Dietz, whom she loves dearly. To help make a decision, Claudia visits a tarot card fortune-teller. When the cards tell of bad fortune, this sets off a chain of tragic events. Stars Gedeon Burkhard, Heinz Weixelbraun and Martin Weinek.
