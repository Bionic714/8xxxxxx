A documentary about a paramilitary cleaning crew from Hell called Dirt Busters. Aired on Danmarks Radio (DR) i 2012.

About the movie: 

Kan man score damer på at rode i lokummer? Kan man blive et mere selvsikkert menneske af at samle affald? Hvis man er Dirtbuster, så kan man. Denne dokumentar følger Dirtbusters, et broderskab af en vilde fyre fra Danmark, der ophøjer rengøring til noget, man er stolt af at udføre - især når man kan trække en blodig tampon op ad et festivaltoilet. Men bag Dirtbusters ligger også en filosofi om, at det er sundt at gøre det modsatte af, hvad samfundet forventer.

Fisse!

Translated:

Can you get ladies by cleaning toilets? Can you become more confident by collecting trash? If you're a Dirtbuster, you can. This documentary follows Dirtbusters, a brotherhood of crazy guys from Denmark,they exalt cleaing to some you're proud of doing - especially if you can pull a bloody tampon fra a festival outhouse. But behind Dirtbusters is a philosophy, that it's healthy to do the opposite of what society expect.
