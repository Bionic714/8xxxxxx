▌EN▐ A flash game created by Crazy Monkey Games and released in May 11th, 2007. It's like Yu-Gi-Oh!, but the attack and defense of the monsters are not rigid... they have attributes of maximum attack and defense, and for each battle a dice is rolled to determine what is the attack and defense of each monster involved, ranging from 0 to the number written in the card. The rest is very similar and don't need to be explained here.
