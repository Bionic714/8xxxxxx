Try your luck at slot machines in this 3D environment. Casino Slots has Random Number Generators (RNG) which are continually generating so the game play is always changing. This is a nice free way to try out your luck on slot machines without using real money. 

You can move around the casino using the [w,s,a,d] keys. Left-Click anything you want to play with. Left-Click and hold the button down to look around.
