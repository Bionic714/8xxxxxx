SOLSIDAN.

"Folk säger att det är barnen som håller ihop äktenskapet men så är det inte. Det är de hemliga kontona." - Fredde.

-----

Swedish dentist Alexander 'Alex' has mixed feeling about moving his family from mundane Stockholm to fancy residential suburb Solsidan, in his natal home. He was right, as manners or even love are no guarantee against trouble with his parents, especially mother who refuses to move out, and the bothersome neighbors.
