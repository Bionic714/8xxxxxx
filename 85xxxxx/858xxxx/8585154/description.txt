'Damaged Goods'

Markosia Enterprises Ltd, 2012, 128 pages

Written by Richard McAuliffe

Illustrated by Mark Chilcott

A collection of dark, twisted horror stories designed to get into your head and under your skin. Nobody is safe. Nothing is off limits. No one can be trusted. This tome of twisted and sometimes horrific tales explains why not all damaged goods can be returned! Damaged Goods is primal horror that permeates your very soul and will be sure to stay with you long after you put the book down!
