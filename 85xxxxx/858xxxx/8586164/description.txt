'Hieronymus B'

Top Shelf Productions, 2008, 64 pages

Written and Illustrated by Ulf K.

Top Shelf is proud to be one of the five international publishers simultaneously releasing Hieronymus B., the all-new hardcover graphic novel from award-winning German cartoonist, Ulf. K. Winner of Max and Moritz Prize for Best Cartoonist at the International Comic Salon in Erlangen, Ulf K. is best known for his endearing silent stories, and this graphic novel - featuring tales of the loveable and humble clerk, Hieronymus B. - will charm everyone.
