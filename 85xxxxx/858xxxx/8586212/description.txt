Cold Prey 2

Jannicke, the lone survivor of a ruthless killer, wakes up in hospital bloodied and weak. All of her friends are dead. As she walks through the dark corridors of the soon-to-be-closed hospital, she thinks she is alone. But the nightmare isn’t over yet. Star Ingrid Bolsø Berdal received a Best Actress nomination at the 2009 Amanda Awards, Norway. Directed by Mats Stenberg and also stars Marthe Snorresdotter Rovik and Kim Wifladt.
