Bhalobasa O Andhokar (1992)

Direction : Deepranjan Basu

Music : Tapas (Bapi) Das & Gautam Chattopadhyay

Music Directed By : Gautam Chattopadhyay

Set in the 1970's, when both sides of Bengal were going through civil war, East Bengal (now Bangladesh) fighting for freedom against Pakistan and West Bengal witnessing the ultra-left Naxalite movement, "Bhalobasa O Andhokar" depicts am unusual love story between a Hindu boy from Kolkata and a refugee Muslim girl from Bangladesh.
