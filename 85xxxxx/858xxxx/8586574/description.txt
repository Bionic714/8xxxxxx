'The Ancient Oak'

Arcana, 2012, 25 pages

Written by Sean Patrick O'Reilly

Illustrated by Chris Uminga

A princess struggles to save her Kingdom from an ever-lasting winter. Will a stranger save the Kingdom or destroy it? The Ancient Oak is an all ages fable that teaches the importance of doing the right thing.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is a children's book, but was still an enjoyable read. I realize books like this aren't what I usually upload, but I think they're important because we need kids to get into comics again.
