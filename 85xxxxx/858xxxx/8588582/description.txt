When Jackie discovers that she's slept with 10 more guys than the national average, she decides to track down all her ex boyfriends and see if one of them could be 'The One.' She enlists the help of her neighbor Robby, who hides at her place when the girls he brings home won't leave. Eventually, Jackie comes to the realization that true love might be closer than she thinks!
