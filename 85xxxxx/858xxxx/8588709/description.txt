The Secret Life of Rubbish

Sociopolitical Documentary hosted by Chris Durlacher, published by BBC in 2012 - English narration


Information
------------------------------

With tales from old binmen and previously unseen film archive, this two-part series offers an original view of the history of modern Britain - from the back end where the rubbish comes out.
Part 1.
A history of British refuse collection from the Second World War to the consumerist 1960s.
Part 2.
How privatisation of public services and environmentalism became big ideas in the 1970s.
