'Daddy's Little Girl'

Arcane Studio, 2011, 82 pages

Written by Mark Poulton 

Illustrated by Stephen Sistilli and Dexter Weeks

Daddy's Little Girl is about rich, party girl, Bryn Tilton, who suddently finds out the father she never knew just passed away. She travels to Atlantic City to learn more about this man, only to discover a dark secret and a new profession... Atlantic City is infested with monsters and she's inherited her father's old job at the city's monster morgue. It's like CSI meets Buffy the Vampire Slayer.
