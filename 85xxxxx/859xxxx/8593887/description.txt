'Rocco Vargas'

Dark Horse Comics, 1997, 284 pages

Written and Illustrated by Daniel Torres

Farmers, lock up your daughters! That intergalactic gadabout, the astronautical inamorato, Rocco Vargas is coming! Daniel Torres, European comics giant, brings his man of mystery to the States. Now American readers will be able to experience the adventures of Torres' popular casanova crime-fighter in this new, hardcover collection which features four of the Roco Vargas stories - "Triton", "The Mystery of Whisper", "Saxxon", and "The Far Star", plus a short, introductory story and 40 pages of new illustrations and roughs in color.
