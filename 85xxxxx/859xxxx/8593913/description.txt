Royal Pains


Genre: Comedy, Family, Medical
Year: 2012
Country: USA
Runtime: 82 min
Producer: Andrew Lenchewski
Cast: Mark Feuerstein, Paulo Costanzo, Jill Flint, Reshma Shetty

.: Plot :.
Dr. Hank Lawson is a promising young doctor in a high stress, high visibility position in a major New York hospital. When a hospital trustee dies under his watch, Hank's career is over, or is it? With the encouragement of his brother Evan and Divya, an ambitious physician assistant, Hank embarks on a new career as a concierge doctor in the Hamptons, a refuge of the rich and famous.
