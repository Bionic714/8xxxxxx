Female Agents

In 1944, a group of French female resistance fighters are recruited by the British Secret Service to rescue a geologist who holds secrets to the impending Normandy landing. They soon find their mission must continue to Paris for the dangerous task of assassinating an SS Colonel. Based on a true story. Directed by Jean-Paul Salome and stars Sophie Marceau, Marie Gillain and Deborah Francois. 

From France - in French and in some German,English
