'Alexander The Great - The Life Of A King And Conqueror'

Rosen Publishing Group, 2005, 48 pages

Written by Rob Shone, Anita Ganeri

Illustrated by Chris Odgers

Within a period of only eleven years, Alexander the Great conquered the largest empire the ancient world had ever known. Stretching from Greece in the West to India in the East, Alexander's conquests were the result of his brilliant military strategies, thirst for power, and desire to spread the culture and ideas of Greece.
