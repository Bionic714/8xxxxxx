Day of Love  (or KoiYoubi)
Company: Princess Soft
Release date: Apr 25, 2000
Rating: All Ages
Genre: Visual Novel
Language: English subtitled

Day of Love is about Haruki, the main character, that knew about a girl named Maho when he was young, but forgot about her when he was older, which made her very angry when she asked him years later when they were in high school. So to get back at him for not remembering, she dresses up as a guy and lives in the same dorm as him using the alias Mao.


Translation: Hirameki International {defunct}
