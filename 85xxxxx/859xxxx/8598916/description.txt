"There are countless music players out there offering buckets of features and flair, but sometimes you just want a basic app to play music from a folder structure. Beam Player fits the bill for this use perfectly..."android central

Beam player is a music player based on a folder organisation. While staying very simple Beam Player tries to bring the best of android in a pure and good looking interface for both smartphones and tablets. Beam player is built arround a file explorer so once you have setup your music folder you will find your music in the same structure as in your storage. Select your folder in your internal storage or your sd card and you good to go.
Features:
- Headset support (play/pause)
- lockscreen controls
- Edit ID3 tags
- Make lists from m3u files
- Play folders
- Sleep timer
