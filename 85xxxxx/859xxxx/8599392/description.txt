d3D Sculptor is a digital sculpting tool that combines 3D modeling, texturing and painting. d3D offers tools to push, pull, extrude, move, rotate, stretch, or otherwise manipulate a digital object as if it were made of a real-life substance such as clay. Import OBJ for further detailing or texturing.
Inspired by zBrush, 3Ds Max, Maya and Blender.
-The is the Full version with no restrictions-
Export OBJ format with textures to be used in 3D design programs such as 3DS Max.
