Canada Customs Invoice (PDF): NAFTA "LOW VALUE" ORIGIN STATEMENT, is used where goods are valued at less than $2500,00 Canadian, the formal Certificate of Origin is not required. Instead, the exporter or producer may provide a statement in the form outlined below.

This "Low Value" statement may be handwritten, stamped, or typed on the actual invoice for which preferential NAFTA rates are being claimed.

Each shipment must be supplied with a commercial invoice or Canada Customs invoice. If the goods are dutiable a NAFTA Certificate of Origin is needed for duty
exemption for commercial shipments. It is the responsibility of the exporter (vendor) to issue and supply these documents.
